Three Cities, Three Thousand Years

——4-day 3-night itinerary in Henan Province

Henan, Cradle of China’s 3,000-Year CivilizationStretching across the heart of the Central Plains, the gentle Luo River winds through this land, where Chinese civilization first took root and the Yellow River culture has flourished for three millennia.

Zhengzhou preserves the mysteries of early Huaxia kingdoms; Luoyang radiates the grandeur of the prosperous Sui and Tang dynasties; Kaifeng revives the bustling glory of the Song Dynasty’s Eastern Capital. Spanning just three hundred li, this route links the three ancient capitals of Zhengzhou, Bianjing (Kaifeng) and Luoyang, carrying travelers straight into the scroll of Chinese history.

Far from rushed, superficial sightseeing, this itinerary delivers an immersive cultural journey. Savor authentic Henan cuisine, watch intangible cultural heritage operas, and witness authentic Shaolin Kung Fu performances. Amid stone grottoes, ancient Buddhist temples and Song imperial palaces, you will trace the enduring cultural lineage of China spanning three thousand years.
