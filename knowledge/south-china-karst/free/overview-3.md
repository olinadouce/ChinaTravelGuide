## Overall Itinerary

This free version gives the route structure and main planning logic. It intentionally keeps timing, hotels, restaurants and ticket choices broad so you can first judge whether the route fits your travel style.

| Stage | Route | Recommended Area to Stay | Food Ideas / Transfer Style |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1–2 | Arrive in Guilin → easy city nature sights | Guilin city center / Two Rivers and Four Lakes area | Guilin rice noodles, Guangxi dishes · airport/rail taxi + city taxi |
| Day 3–5 | Li River Cruise → Yangshuo → Yulong River and flexible nature day | Yulong River area or outer West Street | Beer fish, countryside meals · cruise + local taxi/e-bike |
| Day 6–7 | Yangshuo → Longji Rice Terraces | Ping’an Village or Dazhai / Jinkeng | Guesthouse meals, bamboo rice · mountain-road transfer, light luggage |
| Day 8–10 | Longji → Libo → Xiaoqikong / Daqikong | Libo County or near Xiaoqikong gate | Sour soup fish, bean hotpot · long transfer + scenic shuttle |
| Day 11–12 | Libo → Huangguoshu / Anshun waterfall area | Huangguoshu scenic area or downtown Anshun | Buyi dishes, Guizhou cuisine · early entry recommended |
| Day 13–14 | Longgong / easy Anshun rest → Guiyang departure | Guiyang downtown, North Station or airport area | Beef noodles, sour soup fish · final buffer, avoid distant sights on departure day |

| Free-version planning rule: use this as a route preview. Reconfirm exact trains, scenic tickets, hotel passport check-in and transfer prices before making final bookings. |
