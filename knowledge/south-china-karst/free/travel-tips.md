## Travel Tips

Keep these points in mind before you start the route.

| Payment Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry a small amount of RMB as backup. | Internet Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment. |
| --- | --- |
| Long transfers This route includes mountain roads and long scenic-area connections. Keep transfer days lighter. | Scenic tickets Use official channels, Trip.com, Ctrip, Qunar, hotel desks or scenic counters. Reconfirm rules close to travel. |
| Luggage Do not bring large suitcases uphill at Longji. Use a small overnight bag when staying in terrace villages. | Weather Rain can affect rafting, viewpoints and visibility, but caves and waterfalls can still work as flexible options. |
