## Day 9 | Full Day at Libo Xiaoqikong

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Emerald water, waterfalls, Water Forest and ancient bridge - the most beautiful water scenery on the route. |
| Route | Libo -> Xiaoqikong Scenic Area -> Libo |
| Overnight | Libo |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,000 / USD 97-278 (including hotel, ticket, shuttle, meals and taxi) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and transfer to scenic entrance (1 hour). Enter early to avoid group-tour peaks.

08:30-10:00 | Wolong Pool (1-1.5 hours). One of the best places for water-color photos; visit in the morning.

10:00-11:30 | Yuanyang Lake / Cuigu Waterfall area (1-1.5 hours). Add water activities only if you have energy.

11:30-13:00 | Simple scenic-area lunch or packed food (about 1 hour). Food options are limited; bring water and snacks.

13:00-15:30 | Water Forest and 68-level waterfalls (2-2.5 hours). Strong experience but slippery; wear non-slip shoes.

15:30-16:30 | Xiaoqikong Ancient Bridge (30-60 minutes). The symbol of the area; expect crowds and be patient for photos.

17:00-18:00 | Return to hotel and rest (1 hour). Avoid a packed evening after a walking-heavy day.

Transport and Distance Notes

Libo County to Xiaoqikong entrance by taxi/private car takes 30-60 minutes and costs around RMB 60-200 (USD 8-28).

Xiaoqikong ticket: RMB 100-120 (USD 14-17); scenic shuttle about RMB 40 (USD 6). The shuttle is strongly recommended.

Use scenic shuttle buses between key points rather than walking the entire site.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Breakfast: hotel breakfast.

Lunch: simple scenic meal or packed light food (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

Dinner: sour fish soup, bean hotpot or ecological beef (RMB 60-150 / USD 8-21).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Continue staying in Libo. If visiting Daqikong next day, staying near the scenic area is convenient; if dining matters more, stay in the county.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Carry your passport in case of real-name ticket checks.

Bring a waterproof phone bag and non-slip shoes.

The scenic area is large; check shuttle directions carefully.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

In rain, water color can still be beautiful, but reduce Water Forest walking. If tired, visit only Wolong Pool, Xiaoqikong Bridge and the waterfall highlights.
