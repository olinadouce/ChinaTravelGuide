## Day 14 | Depart from Guiyang

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Departure day; only light city activity or direct departure. |
| Route | Guiyang -> next destination |
| Overnight | - |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 100-600 / USD 14-83 (excluding long-distance flight/rail ticket) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

Morning | Hotel breakfast and packing (1 hour). Check passport, flight/rail ticket, payment tools and luggage.

Depending on departure time | Optional light city activity (1-2 hours). If departure is late, walk nearby, eat in a mall or visit a nearby park. Do not visit distant attractions.

Before departure | Transfer to airport / rail station (30-60 minutes). Arrive at the airport 2.5-3 hours before international flights; arrive at rail station 1-1.5 hours before departure.

Transport and Distance Notes

Downtown Guiyang to Longdongbao Airport by taxi costs about RMB 40-120 (USD 6-17) and takes 30-60 minutes.

Guiyang has several railway stations. Confirm Guiyang North, Guiyang East or Guiyang Railway Station before leaving.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Breakfast: hotel breakfast.

Lunch/dinner: mall restaurant, airport or rail-station simple meal according to departure time (RMB 40-150 / USD 6-21).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

No planned accommodation. If your flight is changed or delayed, choose a chain hotel near the airport or railway station.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Do not schedule distant scenic areas on departure day.

Check the Chinese name of your airport or station in advance.

Allow extra time for traffic, security checks and passport-related processes.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If departure is delayed, stay near the airport or rail station.
