## Day 12 | Full Day at Huangguoshu Waterfall

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The natural climax of the whole route: Main Waterfall, Doupotang and Tianxing Bridge. |
| Route | Huangguoshu Scenic Area |
| Overnight | Huangguoshu / Anshun |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389 (including hotel, ticket, shuttle, meals and transport) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:00-08:00 | Breakfast and transfer to scenic area (1 hour). If staying in Anshun, leave earlier. Scenic-area hotels make early entry easier.

08:00-10:30 | Huangguoshu Main Waterfall (2-2.5 hours). Visit the core waterfall first. In high-water season, expect strong mist.

10:30-11:30 | Water Curtain Cave if open (30-60 minutes). Opening depends on water volume, safety and maintenance; check on site.

11:30-12:30 | Lunch / supplies (1 hour). Scenic-area simple meal or packed food.

12:30-14:00 | Doupotang Waterfall (1-1.5 hours). Easier route and wide waterfall views.

14:00-17:00 | Tianxing Bridge (2-3 hours). A karst walkway of stone, water, trees and caves. If tired, walk only the highlights.

17:00-18:00 | Return to hotel (1 hour). Rest in the evening after heavy walking.

Transport and Distance Notes

Huangguoshu ticket + shuttle reference: RMB 210 (USD 29). The scenic area is large, so use shuttles efficiently.

Round-trip transport from Anshun to Huangguoshu costs about RMB 240-500 (USD 33-69).

Do not board the wrong internal shuttle; show Chinese attraction names to staff if needed.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: simple scenic-area meal or packed food (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

Dinner: Huangguoshu Hotel restaurant, Buyi Eight Bowls or Guizhou food in Anshun (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Continue staying near Huangguoshu or in Anshun. If going to Longgong on Day 13, downtown Anshun is more convenient.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Wear non-slip shoes, not flip-flops.

Keep phone, passport and camera in a waterproof bag.

Do not combine this day with a flight or long rail transfer.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

For older visitors or families, visit only Main Waterfall and Doupotang. Add full Tianxing Bridge only if energy is good.
