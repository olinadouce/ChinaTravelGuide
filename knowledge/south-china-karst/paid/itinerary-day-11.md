## Day 11 | Libo - Huangguoshu / Anshun

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Transfer from Libo to the waterfall region; use the day only for transport and preparation. |
| Route | Libo -> Huangguoshu / Anshun |
| Overnight | Huangguoshu / Anshun |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,500 / USD 125-486 (depending on accommodation and transport level) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout (1 hour). Confirm luggage, tickets or driver.

09:00-15:00 | Libo to Huangguoshu/Anshun (4.5-6.5 hours). Private car is recommended; budget travelers can use rail/train combinations.

15:00-16:30 | Check in (about 1 hour). If staying near Huangguoshu, confirm the next-day entrance. If staying in Anshun, confirm the morning car to the scenic area.

16:30-18:00 | Rest and prepare gear (about 1 hour). Prepare raincoat, waterproof bag, non-slip shoes, passport and ticket order.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner (1.5 hours). Eat local food near Huangguoshu or choose from more options in Anshun city.

Transport and Distance Notes

Libo to Huangguoshu/Anshun by private car costs around RMB 1,000-1,800 (USD 139-250) and takes about 4.5-6 hours.

Rail/train combination costs around RMB 200-500 (USD 28-69), but you must confirm station names, schedules and taxi distance.

Anshun downtown to Huangguoshu scenic area the next morning takes 45-75 minutes and costs about RMB 120-250 (USD 17-35).

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Dinner: Buyi Eight Bowls, Yun-Gui restaurants, Qianren Baiwei or hotel restaurants (RMB 60-180 / USD 8-25 per person).

Higher-budget visitors can choose the Bolian Huangguoshu hotel restaurant (RMB 150+ / USD 21+).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Stay near Huangguoshu for early entry; stay in downtown Anshun for dining convenience. Because Day 12 is intensive, choose a hotel with convenient transport and reliable breakfast.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Do not schedule evening activities.

Buy Huangguoshu ticket + shuttle in advance.

Prepare a raincoat, not just an umbrella.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If transfer is delayed, check in and rest; choose a hotel or nearby restaurant for dinner.
