## Day 1 | Arrival in Guilin

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, hotel check-in, payment and network check. Do not schedule intensive sightseeing. |
| Route | Departure city -> Guilin city |
| Overnight | Guilin |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 450-1,800 / USD 63-250 (including accommodation, local transport and dinner) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

After arrival | Airport / rail station to hotel (suggested time: 40-60 minutes). Take a taxi or pre-book hotel pickup. The goal is a smooth check-in, not saving money through complex transfers.

16:00-18:00 | Hotel rest and payment check (1-2 hours). Confirm Alipay, WeChat Pay, foreign bank card, cash, translation app and mobile data.

18:30-20:00 | Easy dinner (1-1.5 hours). Choose Guilin rice noodles, Chunji Roasted Goose or a nearby hotel restaurant.

20:00-21:00 | Optional short walk around Two Rivers and Four Lakes (30-60 minutes). Keep it short and close to the hotel.

Transport and Distance Notes

Guilin Liangjiang Airport to downtown usually takes 40-60 minutes and costs about RMB 80-150 (USD 11-21).

Guilin has several railway stations. Confirm whether you arrive at Guilin Station, Guilin North or Guilin West, as taxi time and cost differ significantly.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Dinner: Chunji Roasted Goose (RMB 80-150 / USD 11-21) or Guilin rice noodles (RMB 10-25 / USD 1-4).

If you just arrived and are not yet comfortable with local food, choose a hotel or mall restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Stay near Two Rivers and Four Lakes, Elephant Trunk Hill or Zhengyang Pedestrian Street. First-time visitors should prioritize international brands or major-platform hotels for smoother passport check-in.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Save the hotel Chinese name, address and phone number.

Do not overfill the arrival day.

For late-night arrivals, use hotel pickup or official taxis.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If your flight is delayed, cancel all evening activities and go directly to the hotel.
