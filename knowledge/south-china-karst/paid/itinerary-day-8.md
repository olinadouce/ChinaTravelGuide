## Day 8 | Longji - Libo

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long transfer day; the goal is to reach Libo safely. |
| Route | Longji -> Libo |
| Overnight | Libo |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,000-3,600 / USD 139-500 (depending on transport and hotel level) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and checkout (1 hour). Settle bills and confirm luggage down the mountain.

08:30-16:30 | Longji to Libo transfer (6.5-8 hours). Recommended: direct private car, or Longji-Guilin-rail/train-Libo combination. No sightseeing today.

16:30-18:00 | Arrive in Libo and check in (1-1.5 hours). Confirm Xiaoqikong entry gate, tickets and shuttle for Day 9.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Libo (1.5 hours). Recommended: sour fish soup, Libo local dishes or bean hotpot.

Transport and Distance Notes

Direct private car costs about RMB 1,500-2,500 (USD 208-347), suitable for families and small groups.

Rail/train combination costs about RMB 300-800 (USD 42-111), but transfers are complicated and schedules must be checked for the exact date.

Do not schedule any attraction on Day 8, as mountain roads, traffic or transfers may cause delays.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Dinner: Kaili-style sour fish soup or Libo local dishes (RMB 60-150 / USD 8-21 per person).

If arriving late, choose a simple meal near the hotel.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Stay near Xiaoqikong East/West Gate for early entry, or in Libo County for easier dining. Confirm passport check-in before booking.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Save the Libo hotel Chinese address.

Confirm Xiaoqikong ticket, entry gate and shuttle tonight.

Avoid long evening activities.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If the transfer is exhausting, start Day 9 later or use the light Xiaoqikong route.
