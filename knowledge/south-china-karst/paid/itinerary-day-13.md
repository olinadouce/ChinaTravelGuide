## Day 13 | Longgong / Anshun Rest / Transfer to Guiyang

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Buffer day after Huangguoshu: choose underground-river cave or go directly to Guiyang. |
| Route | Anshun / Huangguoshu -> Longgong -> Guiyang |
| Overnight | Guiyang / Anshun |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,500 / USD 97-347 (depending on Longgong and hotel level) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and energy check (1 hour). Choose the plan according to Day 12 fatigue.

09:30-13:00 | Option A: Longgong Scenic Area (2-4 hours). Suitable for visitors who like caves, underground rivers and boat-in-cave experiences. Ticket reference: RMB 130-170 / USD 18-24.

Morning/afternoon | Option B: Supplementary Huangguoshu visit (2-4 hours). For visitors who did not complete Tianxing Bridge or want more waterfall photos.

12:00-15:00 | Option C: Rest and transfer to Guiyang (half day). The safest option to reduce Day 14 departure risk.

16:00-18:00 | Arrive in Guiyang and check in (1-2 hours). Strongly recommended if you have an early flight or train next day.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Guiyang (1.5 hours). Try sour fish soup, beef noodles or a mall restaurant.

Transport and Distance Notes

Anshun/Huangguoshu to Longgong by taxi/private car takes 1-1.5 hours and costs RMB 150-300 (USD 21-42).

Anshun to Guiyang by high-speed rail starts from about 30 minutes, usually RMB 50-100 (USD 7-14); private car takes 1.5-2 hours and costs RMB 400-600 (USD 56-83).

If departing early on Day 14, arrive in Guiyang this afternoon.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: Longgong simple meal or Anshun restaurant (RMB 60-150 / USD 8-21).

Dinner: Guiyang sour fish soup, beef noodles or mall restaurant (RMB 30-150 / USD 4-21).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

For the final night, stay near Guiyang airport, Guiyang North Station or downtown. Choose airport-area hotels for early flights and North Station hotels for high-speed rail.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Longgong cave paths are slippery, and boat routes may be affected by water levels.

If Day 12 was exhausting, prioritize rest and transfer to Guiyang.

Do not schedule a distant attraction before departure day.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If tired, cancel Longgong. For early flights, stay near Guiyang airport.
