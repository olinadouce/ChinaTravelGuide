| Accommodation note: International visitors usually need their original passport to check in at hotels in China. International chains, higher-star hotels and properties bookable through major platforms usually handle foreign guests more smoothly. Mountain guesthouses, scenic-area inns and smaller-town stays should be contacted before payment to confirm whether passport check-in is accepted. “Confirm before booking” does not mean the hotel is not recommended; it means the visitor should confirm again through Trip.com, Ctrip customer service or the hotel before paying. |
