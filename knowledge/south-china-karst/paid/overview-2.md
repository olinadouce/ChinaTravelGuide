## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Guilin | Arrival, rest, payment and network check | Guilin |
| Day 2 | Guilin city natural sights | Reed Flute Cave, Elephant Trunk Hill, optional Two Rivers and Four Lakes / Seven Star Park | Guilin |
| Day 3 | Guilin - Li River Cruise - Yangshuo | Li River cruise, karst peak forests, arrival in Yangshuo | Yangshuo |
| Day 4 | Yulong River in Yangshuo | Bamboo rafting, Gongnong Bridge, Ten-Mile Gallery, Moon Hill | Yangshuo |
| Day 5 | Flexible Yangshuo nature day | Xianggong Mountain, Xingping Li River, Ruyi Peak, Silver Cave or rest | Yangshuo |
| Day 6 | Yangshuo - Longji Rice Terraces | Long mountain-road transfer and terrace sunset | Longji |
| Day 7 | Full day in Longji | Sunrise, viewpoints and terrace walking | Longji |
| Day 8 | Longji - Libo | Long transfer and arrival rest | Libo |
| Day 9 | Libo Xiaoqikong | Wolong Pool, Water Forest, waterfalls and Xiaoqikong Bridge | Libo |
| Day 10 | Daqikong / Maolan / Rest | Canyon, natural bridge, forest karst or recovery day | Libo |
| Day 11 | Libo - Huangguoshu / Anshun | Transfer and preparation for waterfall day | Huangguoshu / Anshun |
| Day 12 | Full day at Huangguoshu Waterfall | Main Waterfall, Doupotang and Tianxing Bridge | Huangguoshu / Anshun |
| Day 13 | Longgong / Anshun Rest / Transfer to Guiyang | Cave underground river or easy transfer | Guiyang / Anshun |
| Day 14 | Depart from Guiyang | Departure day, no distant scenic area | - |
