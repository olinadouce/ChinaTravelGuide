## Day 6 | Yangshuo - Longji Rice Terraces

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Transfer from Yangshuo peak forests to mountain terraces; focus on travel and sunset. |
| Route | Yangshuo -> Longji Rice Terraces |
| Overnight | Longji |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,000 / USD 125-417 (including hotel, private car, meals and ticket) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout (1 hour). Prepare a small overnight bag and confirm large-luggage arrangements with the driver/hotel.

09:00-14:00 | Yangshuo to Longji by road (4-5.5 hours). Private car is recommended. Rest stops can be arranged; do not add extra attractions.

14:00-15:30 | Enter scenic area and reach guesthouse (1-1.5 hours). Some guesthouses require walking or local transfer; contact the hotel in advance.

16:00-18:00 | Viewpoints near the guesthouse / sunset (1-2 hours). If arrival is late, do not walk mountain paths after dark.

18:30-20:00 | Guesthouse dinner (1.5 hours). This is the most convenient dining option in the mountains.

Transport and Distance Notes

Yangshuo to Longji private car costs about RMB 700-1,200 (USD 97-167) and takes 4-5.5 hours.

Shared tourist bus costs about RMB 120-250 (USD 17-35) but involves more transfers and communication.

Internal scenic transport may cost RMB 40-80 (USD 6-11), depending on village location.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: roadside or simple meal (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

Dinner: guesthouse meal (RMB 50-120 / USD 7-17); try bamboo rice, bamboo-shoot cured pork and bamboo chicken.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Ping’an Village suits first-time and lower-energy visitors. Dazhai/Jinkeng suits photographers and sunrise visitors. Confirm foreign-passport check-in, vehicle drop-off point and luggage help before booking.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Do not bring large suitcases uphill.

Ask the hotel in advance whether they can help carry luggage.

Mountain roads are winding; bring motion-sickness medicine if needed.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If arriving late, just check in and rest. Cancel sunset viewing in rain.
