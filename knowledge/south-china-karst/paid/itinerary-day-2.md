## Day 2 | Guilin City Natural Sights

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | A light introduction to Guilin caves and city landscape. |
| Route | Guilin city |
| Overnight | Guilin |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-1,800 / USD 90-250 (including accommodation, tickets, meals and taxis) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-09:30 | Breakfast: Guilin rice noodles or hotel breakfast (30-60 minutes). Keep it simple before visiting Reed Flute Cave.

09:30-11:30 | Reed Flute Cave (1-1.5 hours). A classic Guilin cave with stalactites, stalagmites and lighting. Ticket reference: RMB 82-90 / USD 11-13.

11:30-13:00 | Return downtown for lunch (1-1.5 hours). Choose local Guangxi dishes and avoid overexertion.

14:00-16:00 | Elephant Trunk Hill (1-2 hours). A Guilin landmark, suitable for easy photos. Usually free but may require reservation.

16:00-17:30 | Two Rivers and Four Lakes lakeside walk or hotel rest (about 1 hour). Return to the hotel if hot or tired.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner and Day 3 cruise confirmation (1.5 hours). Confirm pier, meeting time, luggage and pickup.

Transport and Distance Notes

Downtown Guilin to Reed Flute Cave by taxi takes about 20-30 minutes and costs around RMB 30-60 (USD 4-8).

From Reed Flute Cave to Elephant Trunk Hill, take a taxi or return to the hotel before going out again. Public-bus transfers are not recommended for international visitors.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: Xiao Nan Guo / Guangxi cuisine (RMB 60-120 / USD 8-17).

Dinner: Chunji Roasted Goose (RMB 80-150 / USD 11-21) or a hotel restaurant (RMB 100-200 / USD 14-28).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Continue staying in central Guilin for easier departure to the Li River pier on Day 3.

Foreign Visitor Notes

The cave floor can be slippery; wear non-slip shoes.

Elephant Trunk Hill may require advance reservation.

Tonight, confirm the Li River cruise pier in Chinese.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

In rain, keep Reed Flute Cave and cancel outdoor walking. If tired, visit only Reed Flute Cave and Elephant Trunk Hill.
