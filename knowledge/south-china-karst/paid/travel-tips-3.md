## Risks and Backup Plans

| Risk | Possible Impact | Backup Plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rain | Yulong River rafts, Xianggong sunrise, Longji walking, Xiaoqikong Water Forest and Huangguoshu Tianxing Bridge may be affected | Yangshuo: Silver Cave or hotel rest; Longji: short viewpoint walk; Xiaoqikong: reduce Water Forest walking; Huangguoshu: keep Main Waterfall and Doupotang |
| Low Energy | Fatigue after many consecutive walking and transfer days | Skip Moon Hill in Yangshuo; choose one viewpoint in Longji; use Xiaoqikong shuttle; visit only Main Waterfall + Doupotang; cancel Longgong on Day 13 |
| Transport Delay | Most likely on Day 8 Longji-Libo and Day 11 Libo-Huangguoshu | Use these days only for transfer; stay in Guiyang on the final night; do not combine major attractions with flights |
| Ticketing Failure | Online system may not accept passports; scenic-area quotas may fill up on holidays | Use Trip.com/Ctrip international version, ask hotel front desk, go to manual ticket window or switch to a backup attraction |
| Payment Failure | Foreign-card binding fails, weak mountain network, small merchants do not support mobile payment | Prepare four options: Alipay, WeChat, bank card and cash. Pay key transport arrangements in advance where possible. |
