## Day 10 | Daqikong / Maolan / Libo Rest Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose a lighter canyon, advanced forest route or rest according to energy. |
| Route | Around Libo |
| Overnight | Libo |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 600-2,000 / USD 83-278 (depending on Maolan and transport method) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and decide the day’s option (1 hour). Base the choice on Day 9 energy level.

09:30-13:00 | Option A: Daqikong (2-4 hours). Default recommendation: canyon, natural bridge and river scenery; easier than Xiaoqikong.

09:00-16:00 | Option B: Maolan (5-7 hours). Suitable for light-hiking and forest exploration visitors. Requires private car and open-area confirmation.

Morning/afternoon | Option C: Libo rest (half day or full day). Good for families, older visitors or anyone tired from Day 9. Sort photos, eat in town and restock supplies.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner and Day 11 transport confirmation (1.5 hours). Confirm transport to Huangguoshu/Anshun, hotel address and departure time.

Transport and Distance Notes

Taxi/private car from Libo County/Xiaoqikong to Daqikong costs about RMB 60-200 (USD 8-28).

Maolan is best done by private car; half-day/full-day reference: RMB 400-800 (USD 56-111). Confirm route in advance.

If Day 11 transport is early, choose accommodation that is convenient for departure.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: simple meal near Daqikong or return to town (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

Dinner: Libo local dishes, ecological beef or bean hotpot (RMB 60-120 / USD 8-17).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Continue staying in Libo. If leaving by private car, location is flexible; if using rail/train combination, stay in the county for easier taxi access.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Ordinary visitors should choose Daqikong by default rather than going to Maolan spontaneously.

English service in Maolan is limited; arrange driver and route in advance.

Do not walk muddy forest trails in rain.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If Day 9 was tiring, rest. In rain, choose Daqikong or rest in town.
