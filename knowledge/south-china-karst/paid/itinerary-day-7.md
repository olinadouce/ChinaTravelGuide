## Day 7 | Full Day in Longji Rice Terraces

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Sunrise, terrace viewpoints and light walking. |
| Route | Inside Longji Rice Terraces |
| Overnight | Longji |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 550-1,900 / USD 76-264 (including hotel, meals and local scenic transport) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

05:30-07:30 | Terrace sunrise (1-2 hours). Choose Golden Buddha Peak, West Hill Music, Seven Stars with Moon or Nine Dragons and Five Tigers according to where you stay.

07:30-09:00 | Breakfast and rest (1-1.5 hours). Return to the guesthouse after sunrise.

09:30-12:00 | Easy or standard terrace walk (2-2.5 hours). Easy version: nearest viewpoint only. Standard version: connect 2-3 viewpoints.

12:00-13:30 | Guesthouse lunch (1-1.5 hours). Eating at or near the guesthouse is easiest.

14:00-16:30 | Afternoon viewpoint or rest (1-2.5 hours). Visit another viewpoint for different light, or rest at the guesthouse.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner and Day 8 transport confirmation (1.5 hours). Confirm driver, departure time and Libo hotel address.

Transport and Distance Notes

Inside Longji, travel is mainly on foot, by shuttle or with guesthouse assistance. Some viewpoints can be reached by cableway or local transport.

Dazhai/Jinkeng has broader views but longer distances; Ping’an Village has more developed paths.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Breakfast: guesthouse breakfast (usually RMB 20-50 / USD 3-7).

Lunch/dinner: guesthouse or local restaurant (RMB 50-120 / USD 7-17 per person).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Stay in the same guesthouse for two nights to avoid moving luggage.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Wear non-slip shoes; stone paths and field edges are slippery after rain.

Do not step into terraces or damage field ridges.

English service is limited; save the Chinese address.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

In rain, walk only a short viewpoint route. If tired, choose one viewpoint only.
