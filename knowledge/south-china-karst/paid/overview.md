14-Day South China Karst Nature Route

Guilin - Yangshuo - Longji - Libo - Huangguoshu

This handbook is written directly for international visitors. It is not a general sightseeing introduction, but an actionable 14-day nature route guide. Visitors can use it to choose accommodation areas, plan daily sightseeing, connect transport segments, select meals, estimate budgets and handle backup plans. All prices are reference prices only. Final costs should be checked again according to travel dates on official platforms, Trip.com, Ctrip, Qunar, Dianping, hotel front desks or scenic-area ticket counters.

| Basic Information | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Route Theme | Karst mountains and rivers, Li River peak forests, Yulong River countryside, Longji Rice Terraces, Libo emerald-water forest, Huangguoshu Waterfall and Longgong underground river |
| Recommended Duration | 14 days / 13 nights |
| Route Entry / Exit | Enter from Guilin; leave from Guiyang |
| Suitable For | First-time international visitors to China, nature photographers, families, couples and light-hiking travelers |
| Best Seasons | April-June and September-November; summer has stronger water flow but is humid, while winter is quieter but less colorful |
| Difficulty | Moderate; no high-altitude sickness risk, but includes mountain roads, scenic shuttles, long-distance transfers and some walking |
| Exchange Rate Used | USD 1 approximately RMB 7.2. USD amounts are rounded and for budgeting only. |
