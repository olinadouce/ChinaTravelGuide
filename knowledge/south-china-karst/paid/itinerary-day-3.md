## Day 3 | Guilin - Li River Cruise - Yangshuo

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Use the Li River cruise as the core scenic transfer from Guilin to Yangshuo. |
| Route | Guilin -> Li River Cruise -> Yangshuo |
| Overnight | Yangshuo |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,000-3,500 / USD 139-486 (including hotel, cruise, meals and transfers) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:30-08:30 | Hotel breakfast and checkout (1 hour). Handle large luggage according to cruise or hotel instructions.

08:30-09:30 | Transfer to the Li River pier (40-70 minutes). The pier is outside the city center; book a cruise with pickup or arrange a private car.

09:30-14:00 | Li River Cruise (4-5 hours). A 4-star cruise is recommended (about RMB 360 / USD 50). Enjoy karst peak forests, river reflections and the Xingping section.

14:00-15:30 | Arrive in Yangshuo and go to the hotel (1-1.5 hours). Do not schedule intense activities after arrival.

16:00-18:00 | Hotel rest or short riverside walk (1-2 hours). Settle in and confirm Day 4 Yulong River plans.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Yangshuo (1.5 hours). Try beer fish or dine at the hotel.

Transport and Distance Notes

Guilin hotel to the cruise pier costs about RMB 80-200 (USD 11-28). A cruise ticket with pickup is easier.

3-star cruise: about RMB 215 (USD 30); 4-star cruise: about RMB 360 (USD 50). The 4-star option is better for international visitors and families.

If weather or water levels affect the cruise, take a private car or expressway transfer to Yangshuo, about 1.5-2 hours, RMB 350-600 (USD 49-83).

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Onboard lunch is usually simple; bring water and snacks.

Dinner: Master Beer Fish or Huoxiaoniu Beer Fish (RMB 80-150 / USD 11-21); higher-budget visitors can choose a hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

For natural scenery, stay in the Yulong River area. For dining convenience, stay near but not inside the West Street nightlife core.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Carry passport and booking screenshots.

Confirm whether luggage travels with you or separately.

Do not search the pier at the last minute; use the Chinese pier name.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

If not taking the cruise, transfer by private car to Yangshuo and spend the afternoon resting at the hotel or walking on the edge of West Street.
