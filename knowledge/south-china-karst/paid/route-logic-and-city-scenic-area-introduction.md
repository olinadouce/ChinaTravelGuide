## Route Logic and City / Scenic Area Introduction

This route links together the most representative karst landscapes of South China. Guilin and Yangshuo present open river-and-peak-forest scenery. Longji Rice Terraces add mountain and seasonal agricultural landscapes. Libo presents forested water scenery and World Heritage karst. Huangguoshu and Longgong complete the route with waterfalls and underground rivers. The route focuses on natural scenery rather than shopping, nightlife or cultural ancient-town tourism.

| Place | City / Scenic Area Introduction | Accommodation and Travel Advice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Guilin | One of China’s most classic landscape cities and a suitable first stop for international visitors entering the South China karst route. City sightseeing is relatively easy, with many hotel and transport choices. | Stay near Two Rivers and Four Lakes, Elephant Trunk Hill or Zhengyang Pedestrian Street for easy arrival and first-night recovery. |
| Yangshuo | The endpoint of the Li River cruise and the most suitable slow-travel town on this route. Yulong River, Ten-Mile Gallery, Xianggong Mountain and Xingping are all nearby. | Stay in the Yulong River area if scenery is the priority; stay on the edge of West Street for dining convenience, but avoid the bar-street core. |
| Longji Rice Terraces | Mountain terrace scenery suitable for sunrise, clouds and seasonal rice-field colors. May-June is best for mirror-like flooded fields; September-October is best for golden rice fields. | Ping’an Village is more developed; Dazhai/Jinkeng is better for photography. Do not bring large suitcases uphill. |
| Libo | Xiaoqikong, Daqikong and Maolan form a combination of forest, water, canyon and karst landscapes. Xiaoqikong has the most beautiful water color on this route. | Stay near the east or west gate of Xiaoqikong for early entry; stay in Libo County for easier dining. |
| Huangguoshu / Anshun | Huangguoshu is a major waterfall scenic area with the Main Waterfall, Doupotang and Tianxing Bridge sections. Anshun functions as the hotel and transport hub. | Stay near Huangguoshu for early entry; stay in downtown Anshun if dining and city convenience are more important. |
| Guiyang | The capital of Guizhou and the best final-night buffer city. Hotels, airport access, rail stations and dining options are more complete. | Stay near the airport for early flights, near Guiyang North Station for high-speed rail, or downtown for a later departure. |
