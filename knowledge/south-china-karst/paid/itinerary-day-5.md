## Day 5 | Flexible Yangshuo Nature Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose high viewpoints, caves or rest according to weather and energy. |
| Route | Yangshuo -> Xianggong Mountain / Xingping / Ruyi Peak / Silver Cave |
| Overnight | Yangshuo |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-3,800 / USD 111-528 (depending on private transport and hotel level) |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

05:00-08:30 | Option A: Xianggong Mountain sunrise (2-3 hours). Recommended in good weather for Li River bends, peaks and morning mist. Do not wake early in rain. Ticket reference: RMB 60-80 / USD 8-11.

09:30-12:00 | Xingping Li River section (1.5-2 hours). Suitable for riverside walking and classic Li River photos. It can be crowded, so watch belongings.

12:00-13:30 | Lunch (1-1.5 hours). Simple meal in Xingping or return to Yangshuo.

14:00-16:30 | Option B/C: Ruyi Peak or Silver Cave (2-2.5 hours). Ruyi Peak is better in clear weather; Silver Cave is better on rainy days.

After 17:00 | Hotel rest / café / riverside walk (1-2 hours). Reduce intensity if the previous days were tiring.

Transport and Distance Notes

Yangshuo to Xianggong Mountain round-trip private car costs about RMB 300-600 (USD 42-83). Early departure must be arranged in advance.

Yangshuo to Xingping takes about 40-60 minutes and costs RMB 120-250 (USD 17-35) by taxi/private car.

Ruyi Peak or Silver Cave can be decided based on weather; half-day private car costs about RMB 300-600 (USD 42-83).

Dining Suggestions for the Day

Lunch: Simple meal in Xingping (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11) or return to Yangshuo town.

Dinner: Local restaurant or light meal near the West Street edge (RMB 80-200 / USD 11-28).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

Continue staying in Yangshuo. Rest early tonight before the long mountain-road transfer on Day 6.

Foreign Visitor Notes

Check weather before Xianggong Mountain; do not force a sunrise trip in rain or heavy fog.

Laozhai Mountain is not the default option for ordinary visitors.

Pack and organize luggage tonight for Day 6.

Rainy-Day / Low-Energy Alternative

In rain, choose Silver Cave. If tired, make this a rest day in Yangshuo.
