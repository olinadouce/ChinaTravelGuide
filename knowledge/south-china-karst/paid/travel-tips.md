## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For and Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Regular hotels/guesthouses, some shared cars/rail, simple meals | RMB 9,500-13,500 | USD 1,319-1,875 | Young travelers or visitors with China independent-travel experience; more transfers and lower comfort |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Comfort hotels + private cars for key segments + local restaurants | RMB 16,000-24,000 | USD 2,222-3,333 | Best for most international visitors; balanced transport and accommodation experience |
| High-End | High-end resort/view hotels + mostly private cars + selected restaurants | RMB 32,000-55,000 | USD 4,444-7,639 | Families, couples and higher-budget visitors; reduces communication and transfer pressure |

| Category | Budget | Comfort | High-End |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Accommodation | RMB 4,000-7,000 / USD 556-972 | RMB 8,000-14,000 / USD 1,111-1,944 | RMB 20,000-38,000 / USD 2,778-5,278 |
| Intercity Transport / Private Cars | RMB 2,000-3,500 / USD 278-486 | RMB 4,000-7,000 / USD 556-972 | RMB 8,000-12,000 / USD 1,111-1,667 |
| Tickets / Scenic Shuttles | RMB 1,800-2,800 / USD 250-389 | RMB 2,200-3,200 / USD 306-444 | RMB 3,000-4,500 / USD 417-625 |
| Dining | RMB 1,700-3,000 / USD 236-417 | RMB 3,000-5,500 / USD 417-764 | RMB 6,000-10,000 / USD 833-1,389 |
| Backup Cash / Miscellaneous | RMB 500-1,200 / USD 69-167 | RMB 1,000-2,000 / USD 139-278 | RMB 2,000-4,000 / USD 278-556 |

| Budget note: These are per-person reference budgets, excluding international flights, shopping and travel insurance. If two travelers share hotels and private cars, the per-person accommodation and transport cost may decrease. During Chinese public holidays, summer peak season or high-end hotel sell-out periods, accommodation and private-car prices may rise significantly. |
