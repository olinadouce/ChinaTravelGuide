## Day 2 · Continue the Route and Depart

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00–10:00 | Transfer to Fuxi Mountain | Drive toward the scenic area near Xinmi. | Mountain roads and travel times vary. |
| 10:00–14:00 | Fuxi Mountain route | Choose a standard scenic route with viewpoints, bridges and marked paths. | Allow 3–4 hours and follow staff instructions. |
| Optional | Love Sky Ladder / high-altitude feature | Attempt only when officially open and weather conditions are suitable. | The reported ladder is 13.14 m and lacks side handrails; skip if uncomfortable. |

| 15:00 onward | Return to Zhengzhou | Continue by rail or air. | Add generous buffer for traffic. |
