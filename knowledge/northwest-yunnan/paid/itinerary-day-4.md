## Day 4 | Dali - Lijiang: Buffer Before Snow Mountain

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Move to Lijiang and use the afternoon for a gentle snow-mountain view, not a heavy sightseeing day. |
| Route | Dali -> Lijiang |
| Overnight | Lijiang |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-3,000 / USD 97-417. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and checkout.

• 10:00-12:00 | Dali to Lijiang by train (about 1.5-2 hours) or private car (about 2.5-3 hours).

• 12:00-14:00 | Arrive, transfer to hotel, lunch and rest.

• 15:00-17:30 | Baisha Village or Black Dragon Pool (1.5-2.5 hours). Use this as a low-intensity view of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Lijiang: Naxi cuisine, cured-rib hotpot or Yunnan dishes.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Dali to Lijiang rail: RMB 60-110 / USD 8-15. Private car: RMB 400-700 / USD 56-97.

• Lijiang Station to Shuhe / Old Town / Baisha hotels: RMB 40-120 / USD 6-17.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: simple Lijiang meal (RMB 40-100 / USD 6-14).

• Dinner: A Ma Yi Naxi Cuisine, Dian Chu or cured-rib hotpot (RMB 70-180 / USD 10-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay in Shuhe, Baisha or Old Town edge. Avoid deep Old Town lanes if carrying luggage.

• Choose a hotel that can help book Jade Dragon Snow Mountain transport.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Confirm Day 5 tickets, cableway, departure time, warm clothing and oxygen arrangements tonight.

• Avoid alcohol and heavy meals before high-altitude day.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If snow mountain is hidden, still use Baisha or Black Dragon Pool as a gentle walking day.
