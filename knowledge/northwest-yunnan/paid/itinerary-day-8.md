## Day 8 | Full Day at Pudacuo National Park

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Highland lake, forest, meadow and boardwalk scenery in a controlled scenic-area system. |
| Route | Shangri-La -> Pudacuo -> Shangri-La |
| Overnight | Shangri-La |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,800 / USD 111-389. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and transfer to Pudacuo (40-60 minutes).

• 09:00-12:00 | Shudu Lake / open boardwalk section (2-3 hours). Walk slowly and use shuttles.

• 12:00-13:00 | Simple lunch or packed food.

• 13:00-15:30 | Forest / meadow / wetland sections according to current open route (2-2.5 hours).

• 15:30-17:00 | Return to Shangri-La and rest.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner and preparation for the long road to Meili.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Shangri-La to Pudacuo taxi/private car: RMB 150-400 / USD 21-56.

• Pudacuo ticket + shuttle reference: RMB 120-180 / USD 17-25. Open sections can change.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: packed food or simple scenic-area meal (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

• Dinner: Shangri-La town meal (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Continue staying in Shangri-La. Choose a warm hotel and sleep early.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Carry passport, warm layers and rain protection.

• Do not rush the boardwalk; altitude makes walking harder.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If altitude symptoms or heavy rain appear, shorten to one lake section and return. If severe, cancel Pudacuo and rest in town.
