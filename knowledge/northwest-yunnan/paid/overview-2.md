## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Kunming | Arrival, rest, payment and network check | Kunming |
| Day 2 | Kunming - Dali | High-speed rail transfer, easy Erhai adaptation, dinner outside Dali Old Town | Dali |
| Day 3 | Dali Cangshan / Erhai | Choose Cangshan cableway or Erhai ecological corridor according to energy | Dali |
| Day 4 | Dali - Lijiang | Rail / private car transfer, Baisha or Black Dragon Pool for distant Jade Dragon views | Lijiang |
| Day 5 | Full day at Jade Dragon Snow Mountain | Glacier Park cableway, Blue Moon Valley, Yunshanping / Yak Meadow backup | Lijiang |
| Day 6 | Lijiang - Tiger Leaping Gorge - Shangri-La | Jinsha River gorge, Upper / Middle Tiger Leaping, highland arrival in Shangri-La | Shangri-La |
| Day 7 | Shangri-La acclimatization day | Napa Lake / Yila Grassland, easy Dukezong edge walk, optional Songzanlin exterior or short visit | Shangri-La |
| Day 8 | Pudacuo National Park | Shudu Lake, highland forest, meadow wetlands and boardwalks | Shangri-La |
| Day 9 | Shangri-La - Baima Snow Mountain - Feilai Temple | Highland road, Baima Snow Mountain pass, Lancang River valley, Feilai Temple stay | Feilai Temple / Deqin |
| Day 10 | Meili Snow Mountain sunrise / Mingyong Glacier | Sunrise over Meili, Feilai Temple viewpoint, Mingyong Glacier or easy viewpoint day | Feilai Temple / Deqin |
| Day 11 | Yubeng Option Day 1 / Easy Meili Alternative | Enter Yubeng or visit Wunongding, Lancang River valley and Deqin rest areas | Yubeng / Feilai Temple |
| Day 12 | Yubeng Hiking / Meili Backup Day | Sacred Waterfall / Ice Lake hiking, or Mingyong Glacier and second Feilai sunrise | Yubeng / Feilai Temple |
| Day 13 | Meili / Deqin - Shangri-La - Lijiang | Long return transfer, preferably to Lijiang or Shangri-La as buffer | Lijiang / Shangri-La |
| Day 14 | Depart from Lijiang / Shangri-La / Kunming | Departure day; no distant scenic areas | - |
