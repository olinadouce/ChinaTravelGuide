## Day 6 | Lijiang - Tiger Leaping Gorge - Shangri-La

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Transfer through the Jinsha River gorge and arrive in Shangri-La for altitude acclimatization. |
| Route | Lijiang -> Tiger Leaping Gorge -> Shangri-La |
| Overnight | Shangri-La |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,500 / USD 125-486. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout.

• 09:00-11:00 | Drive to Tiger Leaping Gorge area (about 2 hours).

• 11:00-13:00 | Upper Tiger Leaping Gorge viewpoint (1.5-2 hours). This is the default route for most travelers.

• 13:00-14:00 | Simple lunch near the gorge or guesthouse.

• 14:00-16:00 | Optional Middle Tiger section only for fit travelers in good weather. Otherwise continue to Shangri-La.

• 16:00-18:00 | Arrive in Shangri-La, check in and rest.

• 18:30-20:00 | Light dinner. Avoid alcohol and heavy food on the first highland night.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Lijiang-Tiger Leaping-Shangri-La private car / group transfer: RMB 300-1,000 / USD 42-139 depending on vehicle and stops.

• Upper Tiger is easier and safer. Middle Tiger requires hiking, stairs and good weather.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: simple noodle/fried rice at gorge guesthouse (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

• Dinner: light Tibetan/Yunnan meal or yak hotpot in moderation (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay near Dukezong Old Town edge or central Shangri-La. Heating is important in cold months.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Shangri-La is high altitude; walk slowly after arrival.

• If headache or nausea appears, rest and hydrate.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If rain or landslide risk exists, visit Upper Tiger only or skip the gorge and drive directly to Shangri-La.
