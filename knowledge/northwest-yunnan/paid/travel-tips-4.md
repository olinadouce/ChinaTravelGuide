## Risks and Backup Plans

| Risk | Possible Impact | Backup Plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Altitude sickness | May affect Shangri-La, Pudacuo, Feilai, Meili, Yubeng and Jade Dragon Glacier Park. | Slow down, rest, hydrate, avoid alcohol, skip high routes, use Yunshanping instead of Glacier Park, skip Yubeng if symptoms persist. |
| Rain / snow | May affect Cangshan cableways, Tiger Leaping Gorge trails, Baima road, Meili sunrise and Yubeng hikes. | Use Erhai/Xizhou for Dali, Upper Tiger only for gorge, stay in Shangri-La if mountain roads are unsafe, cancel long hikes. |
| Cableway / ticket failure | Jade Dragon Glacier Park may sell out or close due to weather. | Book early; switch to Yunshanping + Blue Moon Valley or Baisha/Black Dragon Pool lowland route. |
| Road delays | Most likely on Shangri-La-Feilai, Deqin/Yubeng return and mountain-road sections. | Treat Day 9 and Day 13 as transfer days; do not combine them with flights; keep a final buffer night. |
| Low energy | Long road days and high altitude may cause fatigue. | Reduce sightseeing: skip Middle Tiger, shorten Pudacuo, skip Yubeng, use Feilai/Wunongding viewpoints instead. |
| Restaurant changes | Small restaurants may close temporarily or move. | Use the restaurant table as a first choice, then search Chinese name on Dianping/Ctrip or ask the hotel for a similar nearby option. |
| Payment failure | Weak network or small vendors may not accept foreign-card-linked mobile payment. | Carry Alipay, WeChat Pay, bank card and cash. Keep some small notes for drivers and village meals. |
