## Day 5 | Full Day at Jade Dragon Snow Mountain and Blue Moon Valley

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The first major snow-mountain day: Glacier Park, high-altitude platform and Blue Moon Valley. |
| Route | Lijiang -> Jade Dragon Snow Mountain -> Lijiang |
| Overnight | Lijiang |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,000-4,500 / USD 139-625. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 06:30-07:30 | Breakfast and departure preparation. Bring passport, warm layers, water, snacks and oxygen if needed.

• 07:30-08:30 | Transfer to Jade Dragon Snow Mountain scenic area (40-70 minutes).

• 08:30-12:00 | Glacier Park cableway and 4,506-4,680 m viewing area (2.5-3.5 hours). Move slowly and do not run.

• 12:00-13:00 | Simple lunch / rest.

• 13:00-15:30 | Blue Moon Valley (1.5-2.5 hours). This is the easiest and most photogenic lower-altitude section.

• 15:30-17:00 | Optional Yunshanping or return to hotel depending on energy.

• 18:30-20:00 | Warm dinner in Lijiang, such as cured-rib hotpot or soup-based meal.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Lijiang to the scenic area by private car/tour car: RMB 150-400 / USD 21-56.

• Snow-mountain package cost varies: RMB 250-650 / USD 35-90 depending on ticket, shuttle and cableway.

• Glacier Park cableway is capacity-controlled and often sells out; book early.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: simple scenic meal or packed food (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

• Dinner: Naxi cured-rib hotpot or Yunnan restaurant (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Continue staying in Lijiang. Do not move hotels after snow-mountain day.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Carry original passport and booking screenshot.

• Altitude is high; stop ascending if headache, nausea, chest tightness or strong dizziness appears.

• Do not drink alcohol tonight.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If Glacier Park is sold out, choose Yunshanping + Blue Moon Valley. If weather is very poor, use Baisha / Black Dragon Pool / Lijiang rest instead.
