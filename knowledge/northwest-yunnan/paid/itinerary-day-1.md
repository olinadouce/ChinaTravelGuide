## Day 1 | Arrival in Kunming

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, rest, hotel check-in, payment and mobile-data check. Do not schedule intensive sightseeing. |
| Route | Departure city -> Kunming |
| Overnight | Kunming |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 450-1,600 / USD 63-222 (hotel, airport transfer and dinner). |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• After arrival | Airport / rail station to hotel (40-70 minutes). Use taxi, DiDi or hotel pickup. The goal is smooth arrival, not saving money on complex transfers.

• 16:00-18:00 | Hotel rest and payment check (1-2 hours). Confirm Alipay, WeChat Pay, foreign bank card, cash, translation app and mobile data.

• 18:30-20:00 | Easy dinner (1-1.5 hours). Choose steam-pot chicken, rice noodles or a nearby hotel / mall restaurant.

• 20:00-21:00 | Optional short walk near hotel. Keep it close and avoid late-night over-scheduling.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Kunming Airport to city usually takes 40-70 minutes and costs RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25.

• If arriving by train, confirm Kunming Railway Station or Kunming South Station; they are far apart.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Dinner: Fuzhaolou steam-pot chicken or hotel restaurant (RMB 80-160 / USD 11-22).

• Light option: Jianxinyuan crossing-the-bridge rice noodles (RMB 25-60 / USD 3-8).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay near Green Lake, Nanping Street, Kunming Station or the airport depending on arrival time and Day 2 rail plan.

• First-time visitors should choose a reliable chain or international hotel for easier passport check-in.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Save hotel Chinese name and phone number.

• Do not overfill the arrival day.

• For late arrival, use official taxi or hotel pickup.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If flight is delayed, cancel all evening activities and go directly to hotel.
