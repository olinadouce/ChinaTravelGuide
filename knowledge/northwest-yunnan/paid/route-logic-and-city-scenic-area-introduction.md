## Route Logic and City / Scenic Area Introduction

This route connects the most representative snow-mountain, gorge and highland-lake scenery in northwest Yunnan. Kunming functions as a lower-altitude arrival gateway. Dali provides a gentle transition through Cangshan Mountain and Erhai Lake. Lijiang is the transport center for Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, Blue Moon Valley and Tiger Leaping Gorge. Shangri-La introduces highland lakes, grasslands, Tibetan-style towns and Pudacuo National Park. Meili Snow Mountain and Feilai Temple form the most dramatic snow-mountain viewing section of the route. The route focuses on natural scenery. Ancient towns are used mainly as accommodation, dining and transport bases, not as the main travel theme.

| Place | City / Scenic Area Introduction | Accommodation and Travel Advice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kunming | The capital of Yunnan, around 1,900 m above sea level, with strong airport and rail connections. It is the easiest starting point for international visitors before entering higher areas. | Stay near Green Lake / Nanping Street / Kunming Railway Station / airport area. On the first night, focus on rest, payment testing and mobile-data setup. |
| Dali | A comfortable transition city between Cangshan Mountain and Erhai Lake. Natural highlights include Cangshan views, Erhai lakeside cycling or walking, Xizhou fields and relaxed scenery. | Stay just outside Dali Old Town for dining and taxis; stay by Erhai for resort views; stay closer to Cangshan for mountain scenery and quieter nights. |
| Lijiang | The core transport hub of this route. Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, Blue Moon Valley, Baisha, Black Dragon Pool and Tiger Leaping Gorge can all be arranged from here. | Stay in Shuhe, Baisha or outside the Old Town core. Avoid bar-street centers if you carry luggage or want quiet sleep. |
| Jade Dragon Snow Mountain | The most important snow-mountain day trip from Lijiang. The core experience is Glacier Park cableway, the 4,506-4,680 m viewing platforms and Blue Moon Valley. | Stay in Lijiang for 1-2 nights before going up. If altitude sensitivity is a concern, use Yunshanping or Yak Meadow instead of Glacier Park. |
| Tiger Leaping Gorge | A dramatic Jinsha River gorge between Jade Dragon and Haba Snow Mountains. It can be visited as a short scenic stop or a hiking route. | For this 14-day route, most international visitors should visit Upper Tiger Leaping and optional Middle Tiger Leaping, then continue to Shangri-La. Full hiking requires extra time. |
| Shangri-La | A highland city at about 3,100 m. It is the acclimatization base for Napa Lake, Dukezong, Songzanlin Monastery, Pudacuo and the road to Meili. | Stay near Dukezong Old Town edge or central Shangri-La. Choose hotels with heating, oxygen support or reliable front-desk service in colder months. |
| Pudacuo National Park | A highland wetland, lake, forest and meadow park near Shangri-La. It is easier than long hiking and suitable for most travelers if they pace slowly. | Visit after one acclimatization night in Shangri-La. Use park shuttles and boardwalks; do not rush at high altitude. |
| Meili Snow Mountain / Feilai Temple | The most powerful snow-mountain viewing section of the route. Feilai Temple and Wunongding are the classic sunrise locations for Kawagebo and the Meili range. | Stay in Feilai Temple or Deqin. Book a mountain-view room if budget allows, but remember sunrise is weather-dependent and never guaranteed. |
| Yubeng | A remote highland village below Meili Snow Mountain, suitable for hikers. Main routes include Sacred Waterfall and Ice Lake. | Only choose Yubeng if physically ready and weather is stable. Ordinary travelers can replace it with Mingyong Glacier, Wunongding and Feilai viewpoints. |
