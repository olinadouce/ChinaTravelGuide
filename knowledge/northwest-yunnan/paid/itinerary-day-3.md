## Day 3 | Dali Cangshan / Erhai Nature Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose one main nature experience: Cangshan mountain or Erhai lakeside. |
| Route | Dali city area |
| Overnight | Dali |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,500 / USD 90-347. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast. Check weather and wind before deciding Cangshan cableway.

• 09:30-13:30 | Option A: Cangshan cableway and mountain viewpoint (3-4 hours). Best for clear weather and mountain views.

• 09:30-13:00 | Option B: Erhai ecological corridor / Xizhou fields (2-4 hours). Better for relaxed travelers, families and windy mountain days.

• 13:00-14:30 | Lunch in Dali Old Town, Xizhou or Erhai area.

• 15:00-17:30 | Short second activity only: Xizhou village, lakeside cafe or hotel rest.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner and preparation for Lijiang transfer.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Cangshan cableway/shuttle costs vary widely: RMB 80-300 / USD 11-42.

• Erhai short bike/e-bike rentals are about RMB 30-150 / USD 4-21.

• Do not ride long distances around Erhai if you are unfamiliar with local traffic.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: Xizhou baba/snacks (RMB 10-50 / USD 1-7) or Bai cuisine (RMB 60-120 / USD 8-17).

• Dinner: Dali local restaurant or hotel dinner (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay in the same Dali hotel. Keep the evening quiet before Day 4 transfer.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Check cableway operation before leaving.

• Carry sun protection and water; Dali has strong UV.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If Cangshan is closed or cloudy, use Erhai / Xizhou as the main route. If low energy, do only Erhai corridor and rest.
