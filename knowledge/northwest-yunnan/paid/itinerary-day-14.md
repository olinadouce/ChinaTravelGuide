## Day 14 | Depart from Lijiang / Shangri-La / Kunming

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Departure day. Do not plan distant attractions. |
| Route | Lijiang / Shangri-La / Kunming -> next destination |
| Overnight | - |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 100-800 / USD 14-111 excluding long-distance flight/rail. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• Morning | Breakfast, packing and document check (1 hour).

• Depending on departure time | Optional short walk near hotel, cafe or mall meal (1-2 hours).

• Before departure | Transfer to airport or railway station (30-90 minutes depending on city and hotel).

• For international flights | Arrive at airport 2.5-3 hours early. For trains, arrive 1-1.5 hours early.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Confirm exact airport or rail station. Lijiang, Shangri-La and Kunming each have different transfer distances.

• Airport/station transfer reference: RMB 40-200 / USD 6-28.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Breakfast: hotel breakfast.

• Lunch/dinner: airport, railway station, mall or simple local meal (RMB 40-150 / USD 6-21).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• No planned accommodation. If delayed, choose a chain hotel near the airport or station.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Do not schedule Jade Dragon, Pudacuo, Meili or any distant site on departure day.

• Keep extra time for passport-related station procedures.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If departure is delayed, stay near the station/airport rather than returning to distant scenic areas.
