## Day 13 | Meili / Deqin - Shangri-La - Lijiang

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long return transfer. Use today only for transport and recovery. |
| Route | Yubeng / Feilai / Deqin -> Shangri-La -> Lijiang or Shangri-La |
| Overnight | Lijiang / Shangri-La |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,800 / USD 125-528. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and checkout.

• 08:30-15:30 | Return from Feilai/Deqin/Yubeng access area to Shangri-La (5-8 hours depending on origin).

• 15:30-18:30 | Option A: stay in Shangri-La as buffer. Option B: continue to Lijiang by train/car if timing is safe.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner and rest. No sightseeing.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Deqin/Feilai to Shangri-La: RMB 250-1,500 / USD 35-208, 5-7 hours.

• Shangri-La to Lijiang: train/private car may take 1.5-4 hours depending on schedule and method.

• Do not combine this return day with an international flight.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: roadside simple meal (RMB 40-100 / USD 6-14).

• Dinner: Shangri-La or Lijiang city restaurant (RMB 60-180 / USD 8-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• If next departure is from Lijiang, return to Lijiang tonight only if timing is comfortable. Otherwise stay in Shangri-La.

• Choose a reliable hotel near station/airport if leaving early.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Keep passport and tickets accessible.

• Mountain-road delays are possible.

• Build buffer into the final day.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If road is delayed, stay in Shangri-La and adjust departure. If tired after Yubeng, do not continue to Lijiang at night.
