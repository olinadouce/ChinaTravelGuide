## Day 9 | Shangri-La - Baima Snow Mountain - Feilai Temple / Deqin

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long highland road transfer from Shangri-La to the Meili viewing area. |
| Route | Shangri-La -> Baima Snow Mountain road -> Deqin / Feilai Temple |
| Overnight | Feilai Temple / Deqin |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,000-4,500 / USD 139-625. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout.

• 09:00-12:00 | Drive through highland road and valley sections. Stop only at safe viewpoints.

• 12:00-13:30 | Lunch in Benzilan / roadside town if appropriate.

• 13:30-16:30 | Continue via Baima Snow Mountain / Lancang River valley to Deqin / Feilai Temple.

• 16:30-18:00 | Check in, rest and confirm sunrise viewpoint.

• 18:30-20:00 | Warm dinner at hotel or nearby restaurant. Sleep early for sunrise.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Shangri-La to Feilai/Deqin: usually 5-7 hours by road; private car RMB 800-1,500+ / USD 111-208+ depending on vehicle and stops.

• Do not drive this section at night. Winter snow or road control may change the route.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: simple roadside meal (RMB 40-100 / USD 6-14).

• Dinner: hotel restaurant or Feilai view restaurant (RMB 60-200 / USD 8-28).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay in Feilai Temple for sunrise convenience, or Deqin for more town services. Mountain-view rooms are weather-dependent in value.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Bring warm clothes for sunrise.

• Confirm heating and hot water.

• Altitude and road curves can cause discomfort; prepare motion-sickness medicine.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If roads are affected by snow/rain, stay in Shangri-La one more night and postpone Meili.
