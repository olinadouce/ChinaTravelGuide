## Accommodation Guide: Location, Foreign-Visitor Convenience and Reasons to Stay

| Destination | Recommended Stay | Location | Why Recommended | Foreign-Visitor Convenience | Reference Average Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kunming | Sofitel / Grand Park / InterContinental / central chain hotels | Green Lake, Nanping Street, Kunming Railway Station or airport area | Good first-night base, more reliable passport check-in, easy food and taxi access. | High | RMB 450-1,500 / USD 63-208 |
| Kunming | Comfort chain hotels | Nanping Street, Kunming Station, Dongfeng Square | Budget-friendly, convenient for rail transfer to Dali the next morning. Confirm foreign-passport check-in if using smaller chains. | Medium-High | RMB 250-700 / USD 35-97 |
| Dali | Hilton Dali Resort / Hotel Indigo / Erhai resort hotels | Erhai side or Cangshan-facing areas | Good scenery, more comfortable for families and higher budgets; quieter than Old Town core. | High | RMB 800-2,500 / USD 111-347 |
| Dali | Comfort hotels outside Dali Old Town | Old Town edge, Cangshan gate, or transport-convenient areas | Best balance of dining, taxis and quiet sleep. Avoid dragging luggage inside narrow lanes. | Medium-High | RMB 300-900 / USD 42-125 |
| Dali | Xizhou / Erhai guesthouses | Xizhou village or lakeside villages | Good for slow scenery and photography. Confirm passport check-in before payment. | Confirm before booking | RMB 350-1,200 / USD 49-167 |
| Lijiang | InterContinental / Hotel Indigo / Pullman / Banyan Tree type hotels | Old Town edge, Shuhe or Baisha direction | Better foreign-guest reception and transport coordination for Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. | High | RMB 800-3,500 / USD 111-486 |
| Lijiang | Comfort hotels in Shuhe / Baisha / Old Town edge | Quieter areas outside the Old Town bar core | Good for snow-mountain access, less noise and easier luggage movement. | Medium-High | RMB 300-1,000 / USD 42-139 |
| Shangri-La | Songtsam Linka / Shangri-La Resort / highland resort hotels | Near Songzanlin, Dukezong edge or city center | More reliable heating, oxygen support, transport coordination and foreign-passport service. | High | RMB 700-2,800 / USD 97-389 |
| Shangri-La | Comfort hotels near Dukezong edge | Dukezong outer ring or central road areas | Convenient for restaurants and taxis while avoiding the cold and noise of inner lanes. | Medium-High | RMB 300-900 / USD 42-125 |
| Feilai Temple / Deqin | Songtsam Meili Lodge / high-end view hotels | Feilai Temple or Wunongding view areas | Best sunrise viewing and most stable service in the Meili section; book early. | High | RMB 1,200-4,000 / USD 167-556 |
| Feilai Temple / Deqin | Mountain-view guesthouses / standard hotels | Feilai Temple road, Deqin town | Practical for most travelers; confirm heating, hot water and passport check-in. | Confirm before booking | RMB 300-1,200 / USD 42-167 |
| Yubeng | Yubeng guesthouses / lodges | Upper or Lower Yubeng Village | Only for hikers. Expect basic facilities, limited English and weather-dependent access. | Confirm before booking | RMB 300-1,200 / USD 42-167 |
| Return Buffer | Lijiang / Shangri-La airport or station hotels | Near airport, rail station or city edge | Useful before departure. Choose reliable chain hotels if flight or train leaves early. | Medium-High | RMB 300-1,000 / USD 42-139 |

| Accommodation note: International visitors normally need the original passport for hotel check-in in China. International chains, higher-star hotels and properties bookable through major platforms usually handle foreign guests more smoothly. In Shangri-La, Feilai Temple and Yubeng, always confirm passport check-in, heating, hot water, oxygen availability and vehicle coordination before payment. |
