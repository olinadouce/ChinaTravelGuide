## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For and Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Comfortable chain hotels / guesthouses, rail where practical, shared/private cars only for difficult sections, simple meals | RMB 11,000-16,000 | USD 1,528-2,222 | For young or experienced independent travelers. More transfers and lower comfort. |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Comfort hotels + private cars for mountain sections + reliable local restaurants | RMB 20,000-32,000 | USD 2,778-4,444 | Best for most international visitors. Balances comfort, safety and cost. |
| High-End | High-end mountain/resort hotels + mostly private cars + selected restaurants + view rooms | RMB 40,000-75,000 | USD 5,556-10,417 | For families, couples and higher-budget travelers; reduces communication and transfer stress. |

| Category | Budget | Comfort | High-End |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Accommodation | RMB 4,500-8,000 / USD 625-1,111 | RMB 9,000-18,000 / USD 1,250-2,500 | RMB 24,000-50,000 / USD 3,333-6,944 |
| Intercity Transport / Private Cars | RMB 3,500-5,500 / USD 486-764 | RMB 6,000-10,000 / USD 833-1,389 | RMB 10,000-16,000 / USD 1,389-2,222 |
| Tickets / Shuttles / Cableways | RMB 2,000-3,500 / USD 278-486 | RMB 2,800-4,500 / USD 389-625 | RMB 4,000-6,000 / USD 556-833 |
| Dining | RMB 2,000-3,500 / USD 278-486 | RMB 4,000-7,000 / USD 556-972 | RMB 8,000-14,000 / USD 1,111-1,944 |
| Backup Cash / Miscellaneous | RMB 800-1,800 / USD 111-250 | RMB 1,500-3,000 / USD 208-417 | RMB 3,000-6,000 / USD 417-833 |

| Budget note: These are per-person reference budgets, excluding international flights, shopping and travel insurance. If two travelers share hotel rooms and private cars, per-person costs may decrease. During Chinese public holidays, summer peak season, snow-mountain clear-weather periods or high-end hotel sell-out periods, accommodation and private-car prices can rise significantly. |
