## Day 10 | Meili Snow Mountain Sunrise / Mingyong Glacier

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Meili Snow Mountain sunrise and optional glacier-side scenery. |
| Route | Feilai / Deqin -> Meili viewpoints / Mingyong -> Feilai / Deqin |
| Overnight | Feilai Temple / Deqin |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-3,500 / USD 97-486. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• Before sunrise | Feilai Temple or Wunongding viewpoint (30-90 minutes). Dress very warmly and arrive early.

• 08:00-09:30 | Breakfast and rest after sunrise.

• 10:00-15:00 | Option A: Mingyong Glacier (3-5 hours including transfer). Good if weather and road are stable.

• 10:00-14:00 | Option B: Wunongding + Lancang River valley + Deqin rest. Better for low energy or cloudy weather.

• 16:00-18:00 | Return to hotel, rest and review weather for Day 11.

• 18:30-20:00 | Warm dinner and early sleep.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Feilai/Wunongding local transfers: RMB 100-500 / USD 14-69.

• Mingyong costs vary by entrance, shuttle and route: RMB 55-200 / USD 8-28. Confirm before departure.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Breakfast: hotel meal or noodle shop (RMB 20-60 / USD 3-8).

• Lunch: simple Mingyong/Deqin meal (RMB 40-100 / USD 6-14).

• Dinner: hotel or Feilai restaurant (RMB 60-180 / USD 8-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay another night in Feilai/Deqin. Do not change hotels unless entering Yubeng the next day.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Sunrise is never guaranteed. Cloudy weather is normal.

• Do not stand in exposed cold for too long if feeling unwell.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If no sunrise, try again Day 11. If Mingyong is closed, choose Wunongding / Deqin rest.
