## 14-Day Execution Checklist

| Day | Default Route | Food / Preparation Focus | If Something Goes Wrong |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive Kunming, hotel rest | Steam-pot chicken or noodles; test Alipay/WeChat/cash | Delay: go straight to hotel. |
| Day 2 | Train to Dali, easy Erhai walk | Bai dinner; confirm Day 3 weather | Rail delay: skip Erhai. |
| Day 3 | Cangshan or Erhai/Xizhou | Try Xizhou baba / sour-spicy fish | Wind/cloud: choose Erhai/Xizhou. |
| Day 4 | Dali to Lijiang, Baisha/Black Dragon Pool | Naxi dinner; confirm snow-mountain tickets | If tired: rest at hotel. |
| Day 5 | Jade Dragon + Blue Moon Valley | Warm dinner, no alcohol | Cableway sold out: Yunshanping + Blue Moon Valley. |
| Day 6 | Tiger Leaping Gorge to Shangri-La | Light highland dinner | Rain: Upper Tiger only / direct Shangri-La. |
| Day 7 | Napa Lake acclimatization | Yak hotpot in moderation | Altitude symptoms: hotel rest. |
| Day 8 | Pudacuo boardwalks | Pack snacks/water | Symptoms/rain: shorten to one lake. |
| Day 9 | Road to Feilai Temple | Warm dinner, early sleep | Road issue: stay Shangri-La. |
| Day 10 | Meili sunrise + Mingyong / Wunongding | Hot soup, simple meals | Cloudy: try again next day. |
| Day 11 | Yubeng entry or easy Meili | Guesthouse meal / Deqin rest | If unfit: skip Yubeng. |
| Day 12 | Yubeng hike or Meili backup | Hiking snacks, warm dinner | Rain: cancel long hike. |
| Day 13 | Return to Shangri-La / Lijiang | Roadside simple meals | Delay: stay Shangri-La. |
| Day 14 | Departure | Hotel breakfast, station/airport meal | No distant sightseeing. |
