## Scenic Highlights and Backup Plans

| Scenic Site | Most Worth Seeing | Backup / Replacement Option |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cangshan Mountain | Mountain views over Dali and Erhai; cableway scenery; alpine vegetation. | If cloudy or windy, choose Erhai ecological corridor or Xizhou fields. |
| Erhai Lake | Open lake views, relaxed cycling/walking, villages and sunset light. | If windy or rainy, do a short lakeside section only and return to Dali Old Town edge. |
| Baisha / Black Dragon Pool | Gentle views of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain before the high-altitude day. | If the mountain is hidden, use this as a low-energy Lijiang acclimatization day. |
| Jade Dragon Snow Mountain Glacier Park | High-altitude snow-mountain cableway and 4,506-4,680 m platforms. | If cableway sold out or altitude risk is high, choose Yunshanping + Blue Moon Valley. |
| Blue Moon Valley | Bright blue water, snow-mountain background and easy walking paths. | If crowded, visit later or use internal electric car and focus on 1-2 viewpoints. |
| Tiger Leaping Gorge | Jinsha River rapids and dramatic gorge walls between snow mountains. | If weather or energy is poor, visit Upper Tiger Leaping only and skip Middle Tiger. |
| Napa Lake / Yila Grassland | Seasonal wetland, grassland, horses and open highland views. | If windy/rainy, reduce to a car loop and return to town. |
| Pudacuo National Park | Shudu Lake, forests, boardwalks, wetlands and highland meadow scenery. | If altitude symptoms appear, shorten the route and use shuttles only. |
| Baima Snow Mountain road | High mountain road, pass scenery and transition to Meili region. | If road conditions are poor, stay in Shangri-La and postpone Meili. |
| Meili Snow Mountain sunrise | Kawagebo and Meili range at sunrise from Feilai or Wunongding. | If cloudy, try again next morning or visit Lancang River valley / Mingyong. |
| Mingyong Glacier | Accessible glacier-side scenery beneath Meili Snow Mountain. | If roads or weather fail, stay near Feilai viewpoints and rest. |
| Yubeng Sacred Waterfall | Classic Yubeng pilgrimage hike, easier than Ice Lake. | If not fit enough, skip Yubeng and do Feilai/Wunongding/Mingyong. |
| Yubeng Ice Lake | Harder highland hiking route with stronger wilderness feeling. | Only for fit hikers; replace with Sacred Waterfall or rest. |

8A. Scenic Site Execution Cards

| Scenic Site | Most Worth Seeing | Recommended Time | How to Visit | Backup Plan |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cangshan Mountain | Cableway views over Dali, Erhai and mountain forest. | 3-5 hours | Choose one cableway route; do not try to cover all Cangshan sections. | If windy, skip cableway and use Erhai / Xizhou. |
| Erhai Ecological Corridor | Flat lakeside walking, cycling, villages and sunset. | 2-4 hours | Rent bike/e-bike for short sections or use taxi between viewpoints. | If hot or windy, do a short walk only. |
| Black Dragon Pool | Classic Jade Dragon reflection view from Lijiang. | 1-1.5 hours | Go in late afternoon on Day 4 if weather is clear. | If mountain invisible, use it as a low-energy park walk. |
| Baisha Village | Quiet Naxi village, snow-mountain background and cafes. | 1.5-3 hours | Best as a relaxed Lijiang buffer before snow-mountain day. | If crowded, stay in Shuhe or hotel rest. |
| Jade Dragon Glacier Park | Highest viewpoint, snow and cableway experience. | 4-6 hours | Book tickets early; go in the morning; carry warm clothes and oxygen if needed. | If sold out, choose Yunshanping + Blue Moon Valley. |
| Blue Moon Valley | Turquoise pools and easy walking under the mountain. | 1-2 hours | Combine after snow-mountain cableway; use electric car if tired. | If crowded, focus on upper pools only. |
| Tiger Leaping Gorge Upper Section | Powerful Jinsha River rapids and easy viewpoint access. | 1.5-2 hours | Best default stop during Lijiang-Shangri-La transfer. | If rain, visit only the official safe viewpoint. |
| Tiger Leaping Gorge Middle Section | More dramatic hiking and deeper gorge views. | 3-5 hours | Only for fit travelers with good shoes and clear weather. | Skip if rain, vertigo, children or tight timing. |
| Napa Lake / Yila Grassland | Open highland wetland, horses and seasonal water/grass. | 2-3 hours | Do a private-car loop and short stops. | In poor weather, reduce to one viewpoint. |
| Songzanlin Monastery | Iconic Tibetan-style monastery near Shangri-La. | 1.5-2.5 hours | Optional; go slowly and avoid too many stairs if altitude symptoms appear. | If tired, view from outside or skip. |
| Pudacuo National Park | Boardwalk lakes, forests and highland wetland. | 4-6 hours | Use park shuttles; walk selected boardwalk sections only. | If altitude or rain affects you, shorten to Shudu Lake. |
| Feilai Temple Viewpoint | Classic Meili sunrise location. | 30-90 min | Arrive before sunrise; dress very warmly. | If cloudy, try again next morning. |
| Wunongding Viewpoint | Broader Meili panorama, less crowded than Feilai at times. | 1-2 hours | Use private car from Feilai/Deqin. | If road/weather poor, stay at hotel viewpoint. |
| Mingyong Glacier | Glacier-side scenery and forest route under Meili. | 3-5 hours | Check road, shuttle and trail status before going. | If closed, choose Feilai second sunrise or Deqin rest. |
| Yubeng Sacred Waterfall | Classic Yubeng hike with religious and landscape meaning. | 5-7 hours | Start early, carry water, snacks and rain gear. | If tired, turn back early or rest in village. |
| Yubeng Ice Lake | Harder alpine hike with stronger wilderness views. | 7-9 hours | Only for fit, acclimatized hikers in stable weather. | Use Sacred Waterfall or village rest instead. |
