## Day 11 | Yubeng Option Day 1 / Easy Meili Alternative

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose between entering Yubeng for hiking or staying with an easier Meili scenic plan. |
| Route | Feilai / Deqin -> Yubeng access or Meili viewpoints |
| Overnight | Yubeng / Feilai Temple |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-3,800 / USD 111-528. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• Option A Yubeng | 08:00-13:00 | Transfer to Yubeng access point and enter village by local vehicle / hiking route depending on current policy. Confirm with guesthouse.

• Option A | 13:00-16:00 | Check in and rest in Yubeng. Do not start long hikes on arrival day.

• Option B Easy Meili | Morning sunrise retry + Wunongding / Deqin / Lancang River valley (3-5 hours).

• Option B | Afternoon rest at Feilai or Deqin. Prepare for return route.

• Evening | Warm dinner. In Yubeng, pre-order guesthouse meal.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Yubeng access costs are variable: RMB 200-600 / USD 28-83 depending on local transport and route.

• Feilai/Deqin easy route private car: RMB 200-600 / USD 28-83.

• Do not enter Yubeng without confirming accommodation, access policy and weather.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Yubeng dinner: guesthouse meal, hot noodles, fried rice, soup (RMB 50-150 / USD 7-21).

• Feilai dinner: hotel/view restaurant (RMB 60-180 / USD 8-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Yubeng is for hikers only and facilities are basic. Feilai/Deqin is better for ordinary travelers, families or low-energy visitors.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Pack only a small hiking bag if entering Yubeng.

• Leave large luggage in Deqin/Feilai if possible.

• Check weather and road before committing.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If tired, do not enter Yubeng. Use Wunongding + Feilai sunrise + Deqin rest instead.
