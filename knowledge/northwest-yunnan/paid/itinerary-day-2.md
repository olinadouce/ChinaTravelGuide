## Day 2 | Kunming - Dali: Easy Erhai Adaptation

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Rail transfer to Dali and a low-intensity lake / town adaptation day. |
| Route | Kunming -> Dali |
| Overnight | Dali |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,400 / USD 97-333. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout. Keep luggage simple for the rail station.

• 09:00-12:00 | Kunming to Dali by high-speed rail / train (about 2-2.5 hours). Buy with passport via 12306, Trip.com or station counter.

• 12:00-13:30 | Dali Station to hotel (30-60 minutes) and check in or drop luggage.

• 14:30-17:30 | Easy Erhai lakeside walk / short ecological corridor section (1.5-3 hours). Do not attempt a full lake loop.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner outside Dali Old Town core. Try sour-spicy fish or Bai cuisine.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Kunming to Dali rail costs about RMB 145-200 / USD 20-28.

• Dali Station to Old Town / Erhai hotels costs about RMB 50-150 / USD 7-21 by taxi.

• Keep the first Dali day light to prepare for Cangshan / Lijiang.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: simple station or hotel-area meal (RMB 30-80 / USD 4-11).

• Dinner: Duan Gongzi / Bai cuisine or Erhai-view restaurant (RMB 80-180 / USD 11-25).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Old Town edge is best for food and taxis. Erhai hotels are scenic but less convenient.

• If traveling with large luggage, avoid deep lanes inside Dali Old Town.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Use staffed rail gates if passport scanning fails.

• Confirm the hotel can accept foreign passports if it is a small guesthouse.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If rail delay occurs, skip Erhai walk and just have dinner near the hotel.
