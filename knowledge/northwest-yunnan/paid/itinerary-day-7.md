## Day 7 | Shangri-La Acclimatization: Napa Lake / Yila Grassland

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Slow highland acclimatization with grassland, lake and easy town time. |
| Route | Shangri-La city area |
| Overnight | Shangri-La |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,500 / USD 90-347. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• 08:30-09:30 | Late breakfast. Check how everyone feels at altitude.

• 10:00-12:30 | Napa Lake / Yila Grassland car loop (2-2.5 hours). Stop briefly for photos; do not over-walk.

• 12:30-14:00 | Lunch in town.

• 14:30-16:30 | Optional Songzanlin Monastery short visit or exterior viewpoint (1.5-2 hours).

• 16:30-18:00 | Hotel rest.

• 18:30-20:00 | Dinner near Dukezong or hotel.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Napa Lake private car/taxi loop: RMB 150-300 / USD 21-42.

• Songzanlin ticket/transport often totals about RMB 90-120 / USD 13-17; confirm before visiting.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Lunch: light Tibetan/Yunnan meal (RMB 60-120 / USD 8-17).

• Dinner: yak hotpot or Tibetan restaurant, but avoid overeating (RMB 80-200 / USD 11-28).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay in the same Shangri-La hotel to avoid packing and to improve acclimatization.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• This is not a day for intense hiking.

• If altitude symptoms continue, skip Songzanlin and rest.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• If weather is poor, do a short town walk or cafe rest. If symptoms are strong, stay in hotel and consider medical advice.
