## Day 12 | Yubeng Hiking / Meili Backup Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Hiking day if in Yubeng; otherwise a second Meili scenic day. |
| Route | Yubeng or Feilai / Deqin area |
| Overnight | Yubeng / Feilai Temple |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-3,500 / USD 97-486. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

• If in Yubeng | 07:30-15:00 | Sacred Waterfall hike (5-7 hours). This is the default Yubeng route for most hikers.

• If very fit | 07:00-16:00 | Ice Lake hike (7-9 hours). Only choose with stable weather and good fitness.

• If not in Yubeng | 08:00-12:00 | Sunrise retry + Feilai / Wunongding viewpoint.

• If not in Yubeng | Afternoon | Mingyong Glacier, Deqin rest or short valley drive depending on weather.

• Evening | Warm dinner, pack and confirm Day 13 return vehicle.

Transport and Distance Notes

• Yubeng hiking is mostly on foot; start early and avoid afternoon storms.

• Local route costs depend on village, guide/horse/local vehicle arrangements. Confirm locally.

• Feilai alternatives use private car or hotel-arranged vehicle.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

• Yubeng: guesthouse breakfast/lunch pack/dinner (RMB 80-200 / USD 11-28 total).

• Feilai/Deqin: hot meals and hotel restaurant (RMB 60-180 / USD 8-25 per meal).

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

• Stay in the same Yubeng guesthouse if hiking. If not hiking, stay in Feilai/Deqin for stable facilities.

Foreign Visitor Notes

• Do not do Ice Lake if you have altitude symptoms, knee issues, rain, snow or late start.

• Carry water, snacks, rain gear and power bank.

Weather / Altitude / Low-Energy Backup

• Low-energy travelers should choose village rest or Feilai alternative. In rain, cancel long hikes.
