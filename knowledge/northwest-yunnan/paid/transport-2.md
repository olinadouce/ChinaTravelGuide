## Public Transport Use Notes for International Visitors

• High-speed rail / trains: use the 12306 English site, Railway 12306 App, Trip.com or station manual counters. Foreign visitors buy tickets with a passport and must carry the same original passport when entering the station.

• Station entry and exit: some gates may read passports or app QR codes, but this is not always smooth. If the gate fails, go directly to the staffed channel and show your passport and order.

• Alipay / WeChat Pay: Kunming, Dali, Lijiang and Shangri-La city areas usually support mobile payment for taxis, restaurants and QR-code payments. Feilai Temple, Yubeng and highland villages still require cash backup.

• Urban buses: large cities may support Alipay transport codes, but this is not guaranteed for international users in every city. For mountain sections, do not rely on buses; use private cars, hotel pickups or pre-booked vehicles.

• On-site ticket buying: if an online platform does not accept passports, go to the manual ticket counter with your passport, hotel Chinese address, attraction Chinese name and cash/mobile payment.

• Private cars: confirm one day in advance for Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, Tiger Leaping Gorge, Shangri-La to Feilai Temple and Feilai Temple to Mingyong/Yubeng access. Avoid night driving on mountain roads.
