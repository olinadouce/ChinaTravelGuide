## Overall Itinerary

This free version gives the route structure and main planning logic. It intentionally keeps timing, hotels, restaurants and ticket choices broad so you can first judge whether the route fits your travel style.

| Stage | Route | Recommended Area to Stay | Food Ideas / Transfer Style |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Kunming -> rest and setup | Green Lake / Nanping Street / station or airport area | Steam-pot chicken, rice noodles · airport/rail taxi + hotel check-in |
| Day 2-3 | Kunming -> Dali -> Cangshan / Erhai | Dali Old Town edge, Cangshan side or Erhai area | Bai cuisine, sour-spicy fish, Xizhou snacks · rail + taxi + short lakeside transfer |
| Day 4-6 | Dali -> Lijiang -> Jade Dragon Snow Mountain -> Tiger Leaping Gorge -> Shangri-La | Lijiang Shuhe/Baisha/Old Town edge, then Shangri-La | Naxi cuisine, cured-rib hotpot, simple gorge meals · train/private car + cableway/shuttle |
| Day 7-8 | Shangri-La acclimatization -> Pudacuo National Park | Dukezong edge or central Shangri-La | Yak hotpot, Tibetan dishes, warm soups · private car/taxi + park shuttle |
| Day 9-12 | Shangri-La -> Feilai Temple / Deqin -> Meili sunrise / Mingyong / optional Yubeng | Feilai Temple / Deqin, optional Yubeng | Hotel/view restaurants, noodle soup, guesthouse meals · mountain-road car + optional hiking access |
| Day 13-14 | Return toward Shangri-La / Lijiang / Kunming -> departure | Lijiang, Shangri-La or Kunming buffer area | Final light meal · long return transfer, no distant sights on departure day |

| Free-version planning rule: use this as a route preview. Reconfirm exact trains, scenic tickets, weather, altitude conditions, hotel passport check-in and transfer prices before making final bookings. |
