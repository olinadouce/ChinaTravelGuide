## Travel Tips

Keep these points in mind before you start the route.

| Payment Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry RMB cash backup for mountain areas and small guesthouses. | Internet Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment. |
| --- | --- |
| Altitude Shangri-La, Feilai Temple, Meili and Yubeng require slower pacing. Avoid alcohol and heavy exercise on first highland nights. | Mountain roads Avoid night driving on mountain roads. Use buffer days for Meili, Yubeng and long return transfers. |
| Tickets Use official channels, 12306, Trip.com, Ctrip, hotel desks or scenic counters. Jade Dragon Snow Mountain cableway should be booked early. | Weather Snow-mountain sunrise is never guaranteed. Rain, fog or landslide risk can affect cableways, roads and hiking routes. |
