## Day 2 | Lanzhou Ink Danxia + Yellow River Scenic Belt

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Enter the Danxia theme. Ink Danxia offers red sandstone ravines, rolling ridges and open desert-edge views; the Yellow River scenic belt works as an easy urban-nature supplement. |
| Route | Lanzhou city -> Lanzhou Ink Danxia -> Yellow River Scenic Belt / Zhongshan Bridge / Baita Mountain view -> Lanzhou city |
| Overnight | Lanzhou |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-1,900 / USD 97-264, including hotel, hired car/taxi, scenic transport and meals. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and sun/wind preparation. Bring water, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, lip balm and a light jacket.

- 09:30-11:00 | Transfer from city hotel to Lanzhou Ink Danxia. One way is about 1-1.5 hours. A half-day hired car is recommended.

- 11:00-15:00 | Visit Ink Danxia. Use the scenic shuttle to move between viewing platforms; focus on red sandstone layers, valley lines and distant ridges.

- 15:00-16:30 | Return to Lanzhou city and rest at hotel or cafe.

- 17:00-19:00 | Yellow River scenic belt: Zhongshan Bridge, riverside walk and optional Baita Mountain distant view.

- 19:00-20:30 | Dinner in Lanzhou. Keep it relaxed because Day 3 is a rail-transfer day.

Transport and Distance Notes

- City to Ink Danxia is about 50-70 km / 1-1.5 hours one way.

- Half-day hired car costs about RMB 350-600 / USD 49-83. One-way ride-hailing may cost RMB 180-300 / USD 25-42, but return cars can be harder to find.

- Yellow River scenic belt within the city is best done by taxi or short walk depending on hotel location.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Ink Danxia is a softer Danxia landscape, good for understanding red sandstone layers before Zhangye’s stronger colors.

- Do not expect the intense rainbow colors of Zhangye; this stop is about ridges, valleys, desert light and transition scenery.

- Optional project: short cable car/river-viewing project along the Yellow River if open and if you have energy.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Breakfast: beef noodles near hotel.

- Lunch: simple meal or packed snack during Ink Danxia visit.

- Dinner: Zhengning Road snacks only if you still have energy; otherwise choose a proper local restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay a second night in Lanzhou to avoid unnecessary hotel moves.

- Confirm Day 3 train station, train time and Zhangye hotel address tonight.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Carry the original passport because some ticket systems or transport checks may ask for identification.

- Wind and dry air can be uncomfortable; carry water and lip balm.

- If visibility is poor, shorten the Danxia visit and spend more time in the city.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Strong wind / sand: cancel Danxia and use Yellow River scenic belt + museum/cafe rest.

- Low energy: visit only Ink Danxia or only Yellow River, not both.

- Transport issue: ask hotel to arrange a return car instead of relying on a random taxi at the scenic area.
