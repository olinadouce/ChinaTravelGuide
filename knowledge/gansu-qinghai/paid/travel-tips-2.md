## Practical Tips for International Visitors

Documents and ticketing

- Carry your original passport. Hotels, railway stations and some scenic areas may require the physical passport, not only a photo.

- If an online scenic ticket page does not accept passports, try Trip.com, Ctrip international version, the scenic-area manual counter, or hotel assistance.

- Book Mogao Caves and major railway tickets early during summer and holidays.

Payment and cash

- Use Alipay as the main payment tool and WeChat Pay as backup. Carry Visa/Mastercard plus RMB 500-1,500 cash.

- In Dachaidan, Chaka, Qinghai Lake and small restaurants, cash can still be useful if mobile payment or foreign-card binding fails.

Weather and gear

- Bring sunglasses, sunscreen, lip balm, hat, wind jacket, water bottle and comfortable walking shoes.

- Protect cameras and phones from sand at Mingsha Mountain and Yardang.

- Salt-lake areas can be extremely bright; eye protection is essential.
