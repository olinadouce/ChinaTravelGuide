## Day 3 | Lanzhou to Zhangye

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Move from Lanzhou into the Hexi Corridor Danxia core area. Today is a transfer day, so do not plan demanding sightseeing. |
| Route | Lanzhou hotel -> Lanzhou West Station -> Zhangye West Station / Zhangye Station -> Zhangye city hotel |
| Overnight | Zhangye |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-1,800 / USD 90-250, including hotel, rail ticket, food and local transport. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:00-09:30 | Breakfast and checkout. Confirm passport, train ticket, station name and hotel address in Chinese.

- 09:30-10:30 | Transfer to Lanzhou West Station. Allow extra time for station entry and passport/manual-lane checks.

- Late morning / early afternoon | High-speed rail or train to Zhangye. Travel time is usually around 3-4 hours depending on train type.

- After arrival | Taxi to Zhangye hotel and check in. Rest and buy water/snacks for Day 4.

- 18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Zhangye city. Try local noodles, juanzi chicken or lamb.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Rail is the recommended method. Second-class fare is roughly RMB 150-300 / USD 21-42.

- Use 12306, Trip.com or station manual counter with your passport. Ticket name/document must match your passport.

- Zhangye West Station to city hotels usually takes 15-30 minutes by taxi.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- This day is not scenic-heavy, but it is important operationally: you are entering the core Danxia region.

- Use the afternoon to prepare for Day 4 sunset timing, because the best Colorful Danxia light is time-sensitive.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple rail-station meal or snacks.

- Dinner: Zhangye chao pao, cuo yuzi, juanzi chicken or local lamb. Recommended areas: city-center restaurants or hotel-advised local shops.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay in Zhangye city center or near Zhangye West Station. City hotels are more practical than scenic-area inns for foreign visitors.

- Confirm with the hotel or driver whether Day 4 goes first to Binggou/Pingshan Lake or directly to Colorful Danxia.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Do not confuse Zhangye Station and Zhangye West Station.

- Use the manual lane at railway stations if passport gates fail.

- Avoid scheduling a sunset Danxia visit today unless your train arrives very early and energy is good.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Train delay: check in and rest; move all Danxia to Day 4.

- Low energy: eat near hotel and sleep early.

- Bad weather forecast for Day 4: ask the driver to keep route flexible.
