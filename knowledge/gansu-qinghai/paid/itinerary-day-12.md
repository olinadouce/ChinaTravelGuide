## Day 12 | Chaka to Qinghai Lake

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | See the largest lake in China, with plateau grassland, distant mountains, open sky and seasonal flower fields. The value is scale rather than one single photo point. |
| Route | Chaka / Qinghai Lake west side -> Qinghai Lake viewpoints / Erlangjian / lakeside road -> Qinghai Lake or Xining direction |
| Overnight | Qinghai Lake / Xining |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,800 / USD 111-389, depending on hotel location, hired car and whether you enter a paid scenic area. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and departure. Dress in layers; lake weather can be windy.

- 09:30-12:30 | Drive from Chaka toward Qinghai Lake. Enjoy highland road scenery and safe viewpoint stops.

- 12:30-14:00 | Lunch near lake area or simple roadside meal.

- 14:00-17:30 | Qinghai Lake viewing. Choose Erlangjian/official area or safe lakeside viewpoints depending on weather and crowd level.

- 17:30-19:30 | Check in near lake or transfer to Xining if you prefer a reliable final city stay.

- 19:30-20:30 | Dinner and rest.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Chaka to Qinghai Lake: about 150-200 km / 2.5-3.5 hours.

- Hired car reference: RMB 500-900 / USD 69-125 depending on route and stops.

- Qinghai Lake to Xining adds about 2.5-4 hours, so decide before afternoon whether to sleep by lake or in Xining.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Qinghai Lake is not a small attraction. The appeal is the combination of lake, sky, grassland, road and mountains.

- Seasonal highlights: rapeseed fields in summer, very open sky in clear weather, lake-blue views after wind clears haze.

- Avoid illegal/private lake access points. Use official areas or safe roadside stops arranged by your driver.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple Qinghai meal near the lake or packed food.

- Dinner: lakeside Tibetan/Qinghai restaurant, or better choices after returning to Xining.

- Try Qinghai yogurt, noodle slices, lamb or yak-beef dishes. Confirm prices before ordering large meat dishes.

Accommodation Suggestions

- If you want sunrise/sunset, stay near the lake in a confirmed foreigner-friendly hotel.

- If your flight/train is soon or if you want better hotel reliability, sleep in Xining.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Lake altitude and wind can cause fatigue. Walk slowly and keep warm.

- Do not enter private grassland without permission.

- Final-night risk management: if Day 14 departure is early, stay in Xining tonight.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Bad weather: treat the lake as a scenic drive and head to Xining.

- Low energy: skip paid scenic area and use safe viewpoints only.

- Hotel risk near lake: switch to Xining city hotel.
