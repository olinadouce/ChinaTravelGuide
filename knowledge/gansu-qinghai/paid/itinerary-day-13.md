## Day 13 | Qinghai Lake to Xining; Optional Kanbula

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Buffer day before departure. Default plan is to return to Xining and rest. Add Kanbula only if weather is good, energy is strong and Day 14 is not an early departure. |
| Route | Qinghai Lake -> Xining; optional Qinghai Lake/Xining -> Kanbula -> Xining |
| Overnight | Xining |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,500 / USD 97-347, depending on whether Kanbula is added. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and check-out. Decide whether to add Kanbula based on weather, energy and Day 14 departure time.

- Default plan | Drive back to Xining, check in, rest and have a good final dinner.

- Optional plan | Full-day Kanbula: red-rock cliffs, lake/reservoir valley and mountain-road viewpoints.

- Evening | Stay in Xining. Prepare passport, luggage and departure documents.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Qinghai Lake to Xining: 150-220 km / 2.5-4 hours; hired car about RMB 500-900 / USD 69-125.

- Xining to Kanbula round trip is a full-day add-on, roughly 250-320 km total; hired car about RMB 600-1,000 / USD 83-139.

- Do not add Kanbula if you have an early flight/train the next day.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Kanbula is different from salt lakes and Qinghai Lake: it combines red-rock cliffs, water surface and valley road scenery.

- Best activities: viewpoint stops, short walks, lake-valley photography and observing red rock above water.

- Default Xining rest is also valuable because the previous days involve long road transfers.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple meal en route or in Xining.

- Dinner: Xining final meal - Ma Zhong Restaurant, Mojia Street area, Qinghai hotpot or yak-beef restaurant.

- Use the evening to rest, not to start another night outing.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay in Xining city center, near Xining Railway Station or on the airport route.

- For early flights, choose an airport-area or easy highway-access hotel.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- This is the final risk-control day. Do not sleep outside Xining if leaving the next day.

- Confirm departure airport/station in Chinese and English.

- Ask hotel to estimate morning drive time and help arrange taxi if needed.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Bad weather: skip Kanbula and rest in Xining.

- Low energy: hotel rest, laundry and easy dinner.

- Departure stress: choose airport-area hotel and do not add sightseeing.
