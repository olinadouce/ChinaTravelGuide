## Transport Table: Distance, Public Transport and Taxi / Hired-Car Costs

| Transport is the most important operational part of this route. Public transport connects major cities, but many scenic areas are outside city centers. The recommended structure is: high-speed rail for main city transfers, taxi/hired car for scenic areas, and hired car from Dunhuang through the Qinghai lake section. |
| --- |

| Lanzhou West Station -> city hotel | 5-15 km / 20-40 min | Ride-hailing / metro | Metro RMB 3-6 / USD 0.5-1; taxi if unfamiliar. | RMB 25-70 / USD 3-10 | Direct taxi if arriving late or carrying luggage. |

| Dunhuang -> Yardang direction | 160-200 km / 2.5-3.5 hrs one way | Day tour / hired car | Public transport is not suitable for most foreign visitors. | Full day RMB 600-1,200 / USD 83-167 | Avoid night driving back if tired; bring water and snacks. |

| Chaka -> Qinghai Lake | 150-200 km / 2.5-3.5 hrs | Hired car | Public transport is limited and indirect. | RMB 500-900 / USD 69-125 | Plan lake-view stops but avoid unsafe private access points. |

| Xining -> Kanbula -> Xining | Round trip 250-320 km / full day | Hired car | Public transport is not recommended for a day trip. | RMB 600-1,000 / USD 83-139 | Only add if weather is good and Day 14 is not early departure. |
| Xining hotel -> airport / station | 30-60 km / 30-70 min | Taxi / ride-hailing / hotel drop-off | Airport bus/rail options may exist; taxi is easier. | RMB 50-180 / USD 7-25 | Leave extra time for passport checks and traffic. |
