## Day 4 | Zhangye Colorful Danxia + Binggou Danxia / Pingshan Lake Grand Canyon

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Core Zhangye nature day. Colorful Danxia is for layered color and sunset light; Binggou is for castle-like shapes; Pingshan Lake is for canyon walking. |
| Route | Zhangye city -> Binggou Danxia or Pingshan Lake Grand Canyon -> lunch/rest -> Colorful Danxia sunset -> Zhangye city |
| Overnight | Zhangye |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,400 / USD 125-333, depending on hired car, scenic combination and hotel level. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and departure preparation. Bring water, sun protection, wind jacket and power bank.

- 09:00-13:00 | Option A: Binggou Danxia for ordinary visitors. Visit castle-like red formations with less physical pressure.

- 09:00-14:30 | Option B: Pingshan Lake Grand Canyon for fit visitors. Expect stairs, canyon walking and more fatigue.

- 13:30-15:30 | Lunch/rest. Do not arrive at Colorful Danxia too early under strong sun unless weather is changing.

- 16:00-19:00 | Colorful Danxia sunset visit. Use the shuttle and prioritize viewing platforms with wide color bands.

- 19:30-20:30 | Return to Zhangye city for dinner.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Zhangye city to Colorful Danxia: about 35-45 km / 45-70 minutes; round-trip taxi/hired car about RMB 180-350 / USD 25-49.

- Binggou or Pingshan Lake add-on requires a hired car. Add-on transport can cost RMB 200-500 / USD 28-69 depending on distance and waiting time.

- Public buses are less flexible for a sunset-based itinerary; foreign visitors should book a driver.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Colorful Danxia: the main attraction is not one single rock but the layered color fields across hills and valleys. Sunset makes colors warmer and shadows clearer.

- Binggou Danxia: look for castle, palace and column-shaped rock forms. It pairs well with Colorful Danxia because the focus is shape rather than color.

- Pingshan Lake: choose it only if you want to walk into a canyon. It is more active and can be hard on knees.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple restaurant near scenic area or packed food; avoid heavy lunch before stairs.

- Dinner: Zhangye juanzi chicken, chao pao or lamb dishes in the city.

- Carry snacks because scenic-area food can be basic and crowded at sunset.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay in Zhangye again. Do not change hotels after a long scenic day.

- If staying near Colorful Danxia, make sure the hotel confirms passport check-in and has transport for the next morning.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Start Colorful Danxia late afternoon for best light, but arrive early enough for shuttle lines.

- Strong wind is common; protect eyes and camera.

- Do not climb outside designated platforms or cross barriers for photos.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Low energy: skip Binggou/Pingshan Lake and do only Colorful Danxia.

- Bad visibility: still visit Colorful Danxia for landform structure, but shorten stay.

- Knee problems: avoid Pingshan Lake and choose Binggou.
