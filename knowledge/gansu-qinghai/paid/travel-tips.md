## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Comfort city hotels/basic confirmed hotels, more rail, shared or lower-cost car arrangements, local simple meals | RMB 10,500-15,500 | USD 1,458-2,153 | Travelers with China independent-travel experience who accept more transfers and lower comfort. |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Foreigner-friendly mid/high-grade hotels, hired cars for key sections, stable restaurants and selected scenic projects | RMB 18,000-28,000 | USD 2,500-3,889 | Most international visitors, small groups and families. |
| High-End | Higher-end hotels, mostly hired car after Dunhuang, better meals and more comfortable scenic pacing | RMB 35,000-58,000 | USD 4,861-8,056 | Couples, families and higher-budget photography travelers. |

| Cost Category | Budget | Comfort | High-End |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Accommodation (standard/basic rooms) | RMB 4,500-7,500 / USD 625-1,042 | RMB 8,000-14,000 / USD 1,111-1,944 | RMB 18,000-32,000 / USD 2,500-4,444 |
| Intercity transport / hired cars | RMB 2,500-4,500 / USD 347-625 | RMB 5,500-9,000 / USD 764-1,250 | RMB 10,000-16,000 / USD 1,389-2,222 |
| Tickets / scenic shuttles / projects | RMB 1,800-3,000 / USD 250-417 | RMB 2,500-4,000 / USD 347-556 | RMB 3,500-5,500 / USD 486-764 |
| Dining | RMB 1,800-3,200 / USD 250-444 | RMB 3,000-5,500 / USD 417-764 | RMB 5,500-9,000 / USD 764-1,250 |
| Backup cash / miscellaneous | RMB 500-1,300 / USD 69-181 | RMB 1,000-2,000 / USD 139-278 | RMB 2,000-4,500 / USD 278-625 |

| Budget note: These are per-person reference budgets, excluding international flights, shopping and travel insurance. If two travelers share hotels and hired cars, the per-person transport and room cost may decrease. During July-August, Chinese public holidays and peak photo seasons, hotel and car prices can rise significantly. |
