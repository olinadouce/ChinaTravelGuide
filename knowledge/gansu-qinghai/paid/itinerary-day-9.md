## Day 9 | Dunhuang to Dachaidan

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long transfer from Gansu desert into the Qaidam Basin of Qinghai. This is not a sightseeing day but a landscape-transition day. |
| Route | Dunhuang -> Dangjinshan / Aksai direction -> Dachaidan town |
| Overnight | Dachaidan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,000-3,000 / USD 139-417, depending on hired car and accommodation. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout. Prepare water, snacks, warm layer and motion-sickness medicine if needed.

- 09:00-15:30 | Road transfer from Dunhuang to Dachaidan. Enjoy Gobi, mountain-pass and basin scenery from the vehicle; do not add far detours.

- 15:30-17:00 | Arrive in Dachaidan and check in.

- 17:00-18:30 | If energy and light are good, short town walk or supply run only.

- 18:30-20:00 | Simple dinner and rest.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Dunhuang to Dachaidan is roughly 340-420 km and takes about 5-6.5 hours depending on road, stops and weather.

- Private car/hired driver reference: RMB 1,000-1,600 / USD 139-222 for the segment, often as part of multi-day Qinghai package.

- Public transport is difficult and not recommended for first-time foreign visitors.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- The key scenery today is the change from Dunhuang oasis/Gobi to Qaidam Basin desert-mountain landscapes.

- Treat the road itself as part of the nature route, but keep photo stops safe and brief.

- Do not walk far from the road or vehicle in remote desert areas.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: packed food or simple roadside meal.

- Dinner: Dachaidan noodle, yak/beef or simple stir-fry restaurants.

- Restock water and snacks because Day 10 may involve salt-lake areas.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Only stay in Dachaidan hotels that confirm foreign-passport check-in before payment.

- If hotel confirmation is unclear, consider staying in Delingha where city hotel services are more stable.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- This is the day most affected by road fatigue. Do not schedule a major attraction after arrival.

- Carry cash because remote towns may have weaker payment/foreign-card support.

- Keep warm layers accessible because temperatures can change quickly.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Road delay: go straight to hotel and rest.

- Hotel issue: ask driver/platform to help switch to another confirmed hotel or continue to Delingha if feasible.

- Low energy: cancel all evening activity.
