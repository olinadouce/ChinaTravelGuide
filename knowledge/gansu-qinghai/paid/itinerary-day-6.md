## Day 6 | Jiayuguan to Dunhuang + Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Spring

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Enter the route’s most recognizable desert scenery: sand dunes, Crescent Spring, camel trails and sunset light. |
| Route | Jiayuguan -> Dunhuang city hotel -> Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Spring -> Dunhuang city |
| Overnight | Dunhuang |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389, depending on transfer method and Dunhuang hotel level. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout. Keep sun protection and water accessible.

- 09:00-15:00 | Transfer from Jiayuguan to Dunhuang by train/bus/private car. Try to arrive by mid-afternoon.

- 15:00-16:30 | Check in, rest and prepare for desert visit. Do not go to the dunes at noon in hot months.

- 16:30-20:00 | Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Spring. Enter late afternoon, choose camel ride if desired, climb a sand ridge for sunset if conditions are safe.

- 20:00-21:00 | Return to city and have dinner.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Jiayuguan to Dunhuang: usually 4-6 hours depending on transport. Public train/bus may cost RMB 80-200 / USD 11-28; private car RMB 1,000-1,600 / USD 139-222.

- Dunhuang city to Mingsha Mountain: 5-8 km / 15-25 minutes; taxi RMB 15-40 / USD 2-6.

- After sunset, taxis can be busy. Pre-arrange pickup if traveling with family or luggage.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Crescent Spring is best seen from higher on the dune, where the oasis shape and desert contrast are clearest.

- Main activities: camel ride, sand ridge walking, sunset photography, shoe-cover rental and desert night atmosphere.

- Do not run downhill on sand or carry uncovered cameras in wind. Sand can damage lenses and phones.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple transfer meal or packed food.

- Dinner: Dunhuang donkey meat yellow noodles, lamb dishes or Shazhou Night Market snacks.

- Bring water into the scenic area; desert dryness makes people tired quickly.

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay in Dunhuang city center for food and transport convenience, or near Mingsha Mountain for desert atmosphere.

- Do not move hotels after sunset; choose a hotel that can help with Mogao or Yardang transport.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Bring passport, booking screenshots and enough water.

- Avoid alcohol after desert heat and before Mogao/Yardang days.

- If you ride a camel, follow staff instructions and secure loose items.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Strong wind: skip high dune climbing and keep to lower viewing areas.

- Late arrival: postpone Mingsha to the next evening if possible.

- Low energy: only view Crescent Spring and sunset; skip camel ride.
