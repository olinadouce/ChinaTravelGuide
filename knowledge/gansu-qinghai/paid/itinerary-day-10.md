## Day 10 | Dachaidan Emerald Lake

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Qinghai salt-lake color day. Emerald Lake is formed by mineral-rich salt ponds showing blue, green and deep-blue color blocks. |
| Route | Dachaidan town -> Emerald Lake -> optional nearby salt-lake / Delingha direction -> Dachaidan or Delingha |
| Overnight | Dachaidan / Delingha |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,500 / USD 111-347, depending on hired car, side stops and accommodation. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and weather check. Emerald Lake is best with sun and low wind.

- 09:30-10:00 | Transfer to Emerald Lake.

- 10:00-13:00 | Emerald Lake visit. Walk only on designated safe paths and view different colored ponds.

- 13:00-14:30 | Lunch/rest in Dachaidan town.

- Afternoon | Optional second light stop or transfer toward Delingha if your route needs to reduce Day 11 driving.

- 18:30-20:00 | Dinner and prepare for Chaka/Qinghai Lake section.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Dachaidan town to Emerald Lake: 10-20 km / 20-40 minutes. Taxi or driver waiting cost about RMB 40-150 / USD 6-21.

- A day car for local salt-lake area may cost RMB 400-800 / USD 56-111 depending on waiting time and side trips.

- If staying in Delingha, add several hours of driving depending on exact route.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- The main appeal is color contrast: turquoise, blue-green, deep blue, white salt edges and brown basin land.

- Best photos come from clear weather and stable wind. On cloudy days, color may still be visible but reflections are weaker.

- Do not step onto soft salt crust or unmarked lake edges. They can be unsafe and damage the landscape.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: simple town meal after the lake visit.

- Dinner: Qinghai noodle, yak-beef dish or hot soup meal.

- Bring sunglasses; salt flats are extremely bright.

Accommodation Suggestions

- If Dachaidan hotel quality is uncertain, use Delingha as a more stable city alternative.

- Confirm next day’s destination: Chaka town or Qinghai Lake direction.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Carry sunscreen, sunglasses and lip balm.

- Do not expect urban-level restaurants or hotel services in Dachaidan.

- If traveling with older visitors, keep the lake visit shorter and avoid long walking on salt surfaces.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Windy/cloudy weather: focus on color blocks rather than mirror reflections.

- Low energy: visit only Emerald Lake and rest; skip side stops.

- Hotel uncertainty: move to Delingha earlier.
