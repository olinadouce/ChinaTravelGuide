## Day 1 | Arrival in Lanzhou

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, hotel check-in, payment and network check. Do not plan physically demanding sightseeing today; the goal is to enter the northwest route smoothly. |
| Route | Lanzhou Zhongchuan Airport / Lanzhou West Station / Lanzhou Station -> Lanzhou city hotel |
| Overnight | Lanzhou |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 450-1,600 / USD 63-222, depending on hotel level and airport/station transfer. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- After arrival | Transfer from airport or railway station to hotel. Reserve 60-90 minutes. For first arrival in northwest China, use taxi, ride-hailing or hotel pickup rather than complex transfers.

- 16:00-18:00 | Check in and complete travel-tool checks: Alipay, WeChat Pay, DiDi/ride-hailing, map app, translation app, 12306/Trip.com orders, hotel Chinese address and driver contacts.

- 18:30-20:00 | Easy dinner. Choose beef noodles or a proper meal near the hotel. Do not spend the first night queuing far away at a night market.

- 20:00-21:00 | If you still have energy, take a short Yellow River walk and view Zhongshan Bridge or riverside lights. Keep walking to 30-60 minutes.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Lanzhou Zhongchuan Airport to Chengguan District usually takes 60-90 minutes; ride-hailing costs about RMB 120-220 / USD 17-31.

- Lanzhou West Station to a Chengguan hotel usually takes 20-40 minutes; ride-hailing costs about RMB 25-70 / USD 3-10.

- For late-night arrivals, use hotel pickup or official taxi. Do not spend too much time studying public transport after a long flight.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Tonight is not about ticking off attractions. The Yellow River scenic belt is a short adaptation walk with city lights, river views and Zhongshan Bridge photos.

- Save screenshots of Chinese names for later scenic areas, hotels and drivers. Network conditions can be weaker later in Gobi, salt-lake and lake areas.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Dinner options: Mazilu Beef Noodles, Amber Beef Noodles, Mogouyan Beef Noodles or a reliable local halal restaurant near the hotel.

- Ordering tip: Lanzhou beef noodles have noodle-width choices such as erxi, sanxi or maoxi. If unsure about spice, say "less chili" or "no chili".

Accommodation Suggestions

- Stay in Chengguan District, Zhangye Road pedestrian area, Dongfanghong Square or near the Yellow River scenic belt for easier dining, taxis and first-night rest.

- Choose a hotel bookable on major international platforms and confirm passport check-in. Carry the original passport to the front desk.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- If your flight is delayed, cancel all walks and night-market plans and go directly to the hotel.

- If mobile payment fails, use cash or ask hotel staff for help instead of waiting at the airport/station.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Late arrival: hotel only; no riverside walk.

- Rain/wind: eat near the hotel and prepare for Day 2.

- Low energy: sleep early; this improves the following road-trip days.
