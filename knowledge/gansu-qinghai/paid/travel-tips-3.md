## Risks and Backup Plans

| Risk | Possible Impact | Backup Plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wind / sand / low visibility | Danxia colors, Mingsha dune climbing, Yardang sunset and salt-lake reflections may be affected. | Lanzhou: switch to Yellow River scenic belt. Dunhuang: museum/hotel rest. Salt lakes: shorten stay. Prioritize safe transport. |
| Long-distance fatigue | Days 8-12 include long drives and can become tiring. | Keep each transfer day to one main goal. Use Day 10 or Day 13 as flexible rest if needed. Avoid night driving. |
| Hotel cannot check in foreign guests | Remote small hotels may not know passport registration. | Use main table hotels only. Before payment ask: "Can foreign guests check in with passports?" If unclear, switch to Delingha/Xining city hotels. |
| Transport delay | Train delays, road conditions, difficult return taxis after sunset. | Arrange return cars for sunset sites. Stay in Xining on final night. Do not place a distant scenic site on departure day. |
| Ticketing failure | Passport not accepted online or quota full during holidays. | Use Trip.com/Ctrip international version, hotel front desk, manual ticket windows, or backup scenic options. |
| Payment failure | Foreign-card mobile payment fails or remote area network is weak. | Prepare four options: Alipay, WeChat Pay, bank card and cash. Pay key drivers/hotels in advance where possible. |
