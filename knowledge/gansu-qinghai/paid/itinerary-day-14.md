## Day 14 | Depart from Xining

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Departure day with only low-risk arrangements. Do not add distant nature attractions. |
| Route | Xining city hotel -> Xining Caojiabao Airport / Xining Railway Station |
| Overnight | - |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 100-600 / USD 14-83, excluding long-distance flight or rail ticket. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- Morning | Hotel breakfast and packing. Check passport, flight/rail ticket, power bank, cash and luggage.

- If departure is late | Optional easy city meal, cafe or short nearby walk. Keep it close to the hotel.

- Before departure | Transfer to airport or railway station. For international flights, arrive 2.5-3 hours early; for trains, allow 1-1.5 hours.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Xining city to airport/railway station usually takes 30-70 minutes depending on hotel and traffic.

- Taxi/ride-hailing/hotel drop-off costs about RMB 50-180 / USD 7-25.

- Confirm whether your station is Xining Railway Station or another transport point before leaving.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- No major scenic highlight today. The best plan is smooth departure.

- If you have extra time, choose only very close, low-risk city activities.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Breakfast: hotel breakfast.

- Lunch/dinner: airport, railway station or nearby mall/restaurant depending on departure time.

- Keep snacks and water for the journey.

Accommodation Suggestions

- No planned overnight stay. If flight/train is cancelled, book a chain hotel near the airport or railway station.

- Keep the hotel phone number and address until departure is complete.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Do not schedule Qinghai Lake, Kanbula or any distant site today.

- Use the manual lane if passport checking at the station gate fails.

- Leave extra time for traffic and security checks.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Flight/train delay: stay near the airport or station.

- Weather delay: do not leave the city for sightseeing.

- Low energy: hotel lobby/cafe rest until departure.
