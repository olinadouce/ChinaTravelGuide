## Day 11 | Dachaidan / Delingha to Chaka Salt Lake

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Move toward the “Mirror of the Sky” landscape. Chaka Salt Lake is about shallow water, white salt and sky reflection under the right conditions. |
| Route | Dachaidan or Delingha -> Chaka Salt Lake -> Chaka town or Qinghai Lake direction |
| Overnight | Chaka / Qinghai Lake area |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389, mainly depending on driving distance and lodging choice. |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

- 07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and early departure. This can be a long driving day, especially from Dachaidan.

- 08:30-14:00 | Transfer toward Chaka. Stop only for safe rest and fuel/food.

- 14:00-17:30 | Chaka Salt Lake visit if weather is acceptable. Use the scenic transport or walking route according to site rules.

- 17:30-19:00 | Check in at Chaka or continue toward Qinghai Lake if your driver/energy plan allows.

- 19:00-20:30 | Dinner and warm rest.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Dachaidan/Delingha to Chaka can range from 300-500 km and 4.5-6.5 hours depending on start point.

- Hired car reference: RMB 1,000-1,800 / USD 139-250 for a long segment. Rail options may exist from some stations but must be verified by date.

- Chaka to Qinghai Lake direction adds 2.5-3.5 hours; do not continue if arriving late or tired.

Scenic Highlights and What to Do

- Chaka is famous for reflection, but reflection is not guaranteed. The best conditions are clear sky, low wind and shallow water.

- Activities: reflection photography, designated salt-lake walking, small tourist train if operating, salt-surface viewpoints.

- Protect feet and follow shoe-cover or walking rules. Salt can irritate skin and damage shoes.

Dining and Supply Suggestions

- Lunch: roadside/simple meal during transfer.

- Dinner: Chaka town noodle/lamb restaurant or hotel meal.

- Carry extra drinking water; salt-lake areas are dry and bright.

Accommodation Suggestions

- If staying in Chaka, confirm passport check-in in advance.

- If hotel quality is poor or departure plan requires, continue to Qinghai Lake area only if time is safe.

Foreign Visitor Notes / Practical Reminders

- Reflection may disappoint in bad weather; manage expectations.

- Do not walk into unsafe or restricted salt areas for photos.

- Use sunglasses to reduce glare from white salt.

Weather / Low-Energy / Delay Backup

- Cloudy/windy: shorten Chaka and continue to hotel.

- Low energy: sleep in Chaka instead of pushing to Qinghai Lake.

- Road delay: skip Chaka sunset and prioritize safe arrival.
