## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Nature Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Lanzhou | Arrival, rest, payment/network check, easy Yellow River walk | Lanzhou |
| Day 2 | Lanzhou Ink Danxia + Yellow River waterfront | Danxia warm-up, viewing platforms, Yellow River sunset | Lanzhou |
| Day 3 | Lanzhou - Zhangye | High-speed rail transfer, supplies and easy city rest | Zhangye |
| Day 4 | Zhangye Colorful Danxia + Binggou / Pingshan Lake | Colorful hills, sunset platforms, shape Danxia or canyon walking | Zhangye |
| Day 5 | Zhangye - Jiayuguan | Hexi Corridor transfer, Gobi-edge rest and supplies | Jiayuguan |
| Day 6 | Jiayuguan - Dunhuang + Mingsha Mountain | Desert dunes, Crescent Spring and sunset | Dunhuang |
| Day 7 | Mogao Caves + Dunhuang recovery | World Heritage cave-art site and oasis rest | Dunhuang |
| Day 8 | Dunhuang Yardang / Yumen Pass direction | Wind-eroded Yardang landforms and Gobi scenery | Dunhuang |
| Day 9 | Dunhuang - Dachaidan | Long transfer from Gobi desert to Qaidam Basin | Dachaidan |
| Day 10 | Dachaidan Emerald Lake | Blue-green salt ponds and mineral-color lake scenery | Dachaidan / Delingha |
| Day 11 | Dachaidan / Delingha - Chaka Salt Lake | Salt-lake mirror landscape and plateau driving | Chaka / Qinghai Lake area |
| Day 12 | Chaka - Qinghai Lake | Large lake, grassland, mountains and seasonal flowers | Qinghai Lake / Xining |
| Day 13 | Qinghai Lake - Xining; optional Kanbula | Final buffer day; optional red-rock lake valley | Xining |
| Day 14 | Depart from Xining | Departure day; no distant scenic area | - |
