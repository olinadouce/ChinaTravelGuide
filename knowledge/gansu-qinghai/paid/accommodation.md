## Accommodation Guide: Foreigner-Friendly Hotels and Standard-Room Price Ranges

This table only recommends hotels that are more suitable for international visitors: international chains, higher-star city hotels, or hotels commonly bookable on major international platforms. Still confirm passport check-in before payment, especially in Dachaidan, Chaka and Qinghai Lake areas.
