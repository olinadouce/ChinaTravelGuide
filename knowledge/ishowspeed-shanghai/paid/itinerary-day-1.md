## Day 1 · Traditional Shanghai to the Bund

Recommended pace: relaxed, with time for photos and food.

| Time | Attraction | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00–11:00 | Yu Garden & City God Temple area | Yuyuan Bazaar → Nine-Turn Bridge → Huxinting exterior → Yu Garden → snack streets | Classic Shanghai extension. Public commercial areas are usually free; the garden requires a separate ticket. |
| 11:00–13:00 | Lunch | Choose xiaolongbao, shengjianbao, pork chop rice cakes or a full local Shanghai meal. | Allow extra time at popular restaurants around Yu Garden. |
| 13:00–16:00 | Nanjing East Road city walk | People’s Square → Pedestrian Street → Century Square → Peace Hotel exterior → Bund direction | Use the route for shopping, street photography and an optional IP-inspired snack stop. |
| 16:00–18:00 | Pre-sunset Bund walk | Historic Bund architecture → Chen Yi Square → Huangpu River promenade → main skyline viewpoint | Arrive 45–60 minutes before sunset for daylight, sunset, blue hour and illumination. |
| 18:00–19:30 | Choose one night-view option | A. Bund waterfront walk · B. Huangpu River cruise · C. Lujiazui reverse view | Cruise piers and schedules vary. Confirm before travel. |
| 20:00–22:00 | Speed-inspired hotpot dinner | Choose a tomato, mushroom or divided broth; add beef, lamb, shrimp paste, tofu and vegetables. | RMB 120–300 per person is a practical reference. Avoid the highest spice level on a first visit. |
