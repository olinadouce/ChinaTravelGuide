### Tips for Foreign Guests

- Book with the exact English name shown on your passport.

- Carry the original passport for check-in.

- Choose hotels that clearly accept foreign guests or passport check-in.

- Reconfirm room type, windows, breakfast, cancellation policy and final tax-inclusive price before payment.
