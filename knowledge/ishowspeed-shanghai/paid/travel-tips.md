## Practical Information

| Topic | What to Do |
| --- | --- |
| Payment | Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay; link an international bank card and carry a small cash backup. |
| Internet | Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation and ride-hailing. |
| Navigation | Save Chinese place names and addresses; they are easier for drivers and local staff to recognize. |
| Hotel check-in | Carry the original passport and confirm the hotel accepts foreign guests. |
| Tickets | Use official channels, attraction mini programs, Trip.com or on-site service desks. |
| Crowds & safety | Keep valuables secure and add time buffers in the Bund, Nanjing Road and holiday periods. |
| High-speed rail | Use the passport name exactly and arrive 60–90 minutes before departure. |
