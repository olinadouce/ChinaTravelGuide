## Timed Tickets

### Book or check these first

Cruise times, tower slots and museum last-entry times can affect the day.

| Day 1 / 3 Pearl River Night Cruise · Tianzi / Xidi Wharf Optional river add-on Use it when the Pearl River evening needs to become a formal river experience. Wharf choice changes the night route, so match Tianzi with Beijing Road / Yanjiang Road and Xidi with Shamian / Xiguan-side evenings. |
| --- |

| Day 1 / 3 Canton Tower Exterior free · interior paid Keep the tower as a skyline anchor from the riverfront. Enter the tower only when the group wants a paid observation experience and has time for the slot. |
| --- |

| Day 2 Chen Clan Ancestral Hall · Guangdong Folk Art Museum Core cultural entry Use it as the Day 2 cultural starting point before Enning Road, Yongqing Fang and Old Xiguan. The last-ticketing time matters if the morning teahouse runs long. |
| --- |

| Day 4 Foshan Ancestral Temple · Zumiao Museum Area Core Foshan cultural entry Anchor Day 4 with ancestral architecture, Cantonese opera, martial-arts memory and Lingnan folk culture before Lingnan Tiandi or the return to Guangzhou. Confirm ticket price and opening hours on the dated booking page. |
