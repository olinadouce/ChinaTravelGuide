## Main Guangzhou Bases

### Pick the area by the route rhythm, not by a hotel list.

| Old-city walking base Guangzhou · Shamian · Old Xiguan Shamian · Old Xiguan Use this area when Day 2 is built around Old Xiguan, Enning Road, Yongqing Fang, Shamian and qilou arcade streets. Planning range ¥350–1,000 / night The area gives the route stronger old-city texture and keeps the Xiguan walking day close to teahouses, arcades, dessert shops and shaded street sections. Compare Shamian and Xiguan stays Area-based stay | Modern metro base Guangzhou · Tianhe · Taikoo Hui Tianhe · Taikoo Hui Use this area when the route needs modern malls, metro convenience and concentrated dining before moving toward Pearl River New Town. Planning range ¥500–1,500 / night Tianhe works for travelers who want the modern Guangzhou layer to be part of the stay, especially on the Day 3 city-to-waterfront section.
