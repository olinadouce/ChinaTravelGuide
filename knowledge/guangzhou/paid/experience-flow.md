## Experience Flow

### The route moves from taste to streets, then to water and culture.

The experience order keeps the short route easy to understand: first eat, then walk, then slow down by the river, then use Foshan to complete the Lingnan layer.

| Dining Table Tea, steamed baskets and shared dim sum shape the first full morning in Guangzhou. | Arcade Streets Qilou walkways, Xiguan alleys and shopfront shade show how the city works in heat and rain. |
| --- | --- |
| Waterfront Canton Tower, Haixinsha, bridges and river lights connect the old-city walk to modern Guangzhou. | Lingnan Foshan adds ancestral temple details, lion dance, Cantonese opera texture and old-street scale. |
