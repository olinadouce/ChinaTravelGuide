## Itinerary

### Four days with a clear Cantonese lifestyle thread.

The route keeps distances compact while changing scenes: riverfront, teahouse, Xiguan arcades, modern waterfront and Foshan’s cultural streets.

|  | Day 1 Arrival in Guangzhou Pearl River opening A light riverside evening around Huacheng Square, Haixinsha, Canton Tower viewing areas or the Pearl River waterfront. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 2 Dim Sum · Old Xiguan From dining table to alleyways Morning teahouse rhythm, Chen Clan Ancestral Hall, Enning Road, Yongqing Fang and old Xiguan streets. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 3 Modern Guangzhou · Pearl River Evening Old city to waterfront Dongshankou, Beijing Road or Yuexiu in the morning, then Pearl River New Town, Huacheng Square and a river evening. |
