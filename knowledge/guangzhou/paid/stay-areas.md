## Stay Areas

### Choose the stay by evening return and next-day route.

Pearl River New Town supports river evenings. Beijing Road and Yuexiu support central dining and metro movement. Shamian and Old Xiguan keep the old-city walking day close. Lingnan Tiandi works when Foshan becomes an overnight stop.
