## Free Walks Areas

### Open areas along the route

Huacheng Square, Haixinsha, Yongqing Fang, Enning Road and Lingnan Tiandi are open-area walks.

| Day 1 / 3 Pearl River Waterfront · Huacheng Square · Haixinsha Free outdoor area Use these areas to keep the first evening and Day 3 return scenic but light. |
| --- |

| Day 2 Yongqing Fang · Enning Road · Old Xiguan Streets Free neighborhood walk Keep this as a street-scale day of arcades, teahouses and Xiguan lifestyle, not a museum checklist. |
| --- |

| Day 4 Lingnan Tiandi Free cultural district Use it as the atmospheric closing after Foshan Ancestral Temple, especially when dinner or an optional Foshan overnight is planned. |
| --- |
