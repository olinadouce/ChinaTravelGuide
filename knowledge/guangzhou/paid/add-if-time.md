## Add If Time

### Extra experiences for a slower day

Water bus, tower entry, river cruise and Nanfeng Ancient Kiln fit better on a slower day.

| River option Guangzhou Water Bus · Tianzi Wharf Daytime river move Choose this for a lighter daytime Pearl River feel. It is not required if the group already plans a night cruise. Check current water-bus service before using, as routes and sailings can change. |
| --- |

| Day 4 add-on Nanfeng Ancient Kiln Optional ceramic depth Add it only when the route has enough time after Zumiao. On a short Day 4, keep the focus on Zumiao and Lingnan Tiandi. Confirm ticket price and opening hours on the dated booking page. |
| --- |

| Choose one Canton Tower Interior vs Pearl River Cruise Evening-time tradeoff For a cleaner city evening, choose either a tower entry or a river cruise. Doing both can make dinner and return movement too compressed. |
| --- |
