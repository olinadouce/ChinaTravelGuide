Route Rhythm

Dining table, alleyways, waterfront, Lingnan.

The route begins with tea and steamed baskets, enters the shade of qilou arcades, opens toward the Pearl River, then closes with Foshan’s ancestral temple, old streets and Guangfu craft details.

4 Days / 3 Nights

Day-by-day route.

|  | Day 1 Arrival in Guangzhou Begin beside the Pearl River. Arrive Settle near Pearl River New Town, Beijing Road, Shamian or Old Xiguan. Riverfront Walk around Huacheng Square, Haixinsha or the Canton Tower viewing area. Return Keep the hotel address and ride-hailing point ready before the evening walk. Local flavor & overnight: Wonton noodles, rice noodle rolls or sweet soup keep arrival light; riverfront and central old-city stays make the first night easy to return from. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 2 Dim sum and Old Xiguan Move from teahouse tables into arcade streets. Teahouse Start with tea, steamed baskets, rice noodle rolls and shared plates. Craft Visit Chen Clan Ancestral Hall for carving, stucco reliefs and Lingnan decoration. Lanes Walk Enning Road, Yongqing Fang, Xiguan lanes and nearby arcade streets. Local flavor & overnight: Shrimp dumplings, siu mai, barbecue pork buns, egg tarts, herbal tea and sweet soup match this old-city day; Old Xiguan, Shamian or Beijing Road keeps evening food and walking close. |
| --- | --- |

|  |
| --- |

|  | Day 3 Modern Guangzhou and river evening Connect old-city rhythm with waterfront lights. Neighborhood Choose Dongshankou, Beijing Road or Yuexiu for a lighter city walk. City Core Move toward Pearl River New Town, Huacheng Square or Tianhe. River Night Take a riverfront walk or night cruise around the Canton Tower view. Local flavor & overnight: Lunch can follow the chosen neighborhood; dinner near Pearl River New Town or the river pier keeps the night route compact. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 4 Foshan Lingnan extension Close with ancestral halls and old-street scale. Transfer Go from Guangzhou to Foshan by metro, intercity rail or ride-hailing. Ancestral Temple Look at roof-ridge decorations, wood carving, opera details and lion-dance space. Lingnan Tiandi Continue into old lanes, desserts, cafés and small cultural spaces. Local flavor & overnight: Double-skin milk, Cantonese desserts or Shunde-style dishes bring the route into Foshan’s food layer; return to Guangzhou or stay near the old-town area when the next morning starts in Foshan. |
