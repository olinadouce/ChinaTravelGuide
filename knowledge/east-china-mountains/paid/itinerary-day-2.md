## Day 2 | Hangzhou / Shanghai - Huangshan North - Tangkou

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Rail transfer and mountain preparation day. The key task is to reach Tangkou or Tunxi and prepare for Huangshan entry. |
| Route | Hangzhou / Shanghai -> Huangshan North -> Tangkou / Tunxi |
| Overnight | Tangkou / Tunxi |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,200 / USD 97-306 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast, check out and go to the high-speed rail station.

09:00-12:30 | High-speed rail to Huangshan North. Actual time varies by departure city.

12:30-14:00 | Transfer from Huangshan North to Tangkou or Tunxi hotel.

15:00-17:00 | If staying in Tangkou, locate the Transfer Center and buy raincoat, trekking pole, water and snacks.

18:00-20:00 | Hui cuisine dinner and early rest.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Huangshan North to Tangkou can be done by bus, taxi or private transfer. With luggage and first-time visitors, private transfer or hotel pickup is easier.

- If staying in Tunxi, leave earlier on Day 3; if staying in Tangkou, you save time for early mountain entry.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Dinner in Tangkou: choose a busy local Hui-style restaurant near the hotel or Transfer Center.

- Dinner in Tunxi: Laojie Diyi Lou, Piyun Huifu Cuisine or another well-rated Hui cuisine restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Tangkou is best for early mountain entry.

- Tunxi is better for late arrival and restaurants but requires an earlier start next morning.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Buy rail tickets with passport and carry the original passport for station checks.

- Confirm whether you will use Yungu Cableway or Yuping Cableway on Day 3.

Backup Plan

- Late arrival: stay in Tunxi and start later on Day 3.

- Bad weather forecast: still go to Tangkou, but prepare a shorter mountain route.
