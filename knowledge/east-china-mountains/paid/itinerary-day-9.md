## Day 9 | Wuyishan Core Day 1: Tianyou Peak and River Overview

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Start Wuyishan from above: climb Tianyou Peak for the best view over the Nine-Bend Stream and Danxia cliffs. |
| Route | Wuyishan Scenic Area |
| Overnight | Wuyishan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,500 / USD 111-347 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and go to scenic entrance.

09:00-12:30 | Tianyou Peak. Climb slowly, rest at platforms and focus on the river bends below.

12:30-13:30 | Lunch / rest.

14:00-16:00 | Wuyi Palace, Song Street or nearby river walk for a low-intensity afternoon.

16:00-17:30 | Optional Jade Maiden Peak viewpoint if weather is good.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner in Sangu Resort area.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Tianyou Peak is the best “mountain looking at river” viewpoint on this route. Start early to avoid heat and crowds.

- The climb has stairs; take it slowly and do not rush after several previous mountain days.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Lunch: simple scenic-area food or return to Sangu.

- Dinner: Yan Wuyi / local Wuyi cuisine restaurants, or resort restaurant for higher comfort.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Continue staying in the same Wuyishan hotel to avoid moving luggage.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Carry water and sun protection.

- If bamboo raft ticket is already reserved for Day 10, double-check the time tonight.

Backup Plan

- Rain or low energy: reduce Tianyou climb and use Wuyi Palace + tea tasting.

- If Tianyou is crowded, visit later or swap with Dahongpao trail.
