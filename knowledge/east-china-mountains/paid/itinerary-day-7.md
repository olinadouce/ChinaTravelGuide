## Day 7 | Sanqingshan Full Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The core Sanqingshan day: Nanqing Garden, signature granite rocks, cliff walkways and open peak views. |
| Route | Sanqingshan Scenic Area |
| Overnight | Sanqingshan / Shangrao |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:00-08:00 | Breakfast and transfer to cableway station.

08:00-09:00 | Take Jinsha or Waishuangxi Cableway uphill.

09:00-12:00 | Nanqing Garden: Goddess Peak, Python Emerging from the Mountain and compact granite viewpoints.

12:00-13:00 | Simple lunch or packed food.

13:00-15:30 | Sunshine Coast walkway for open peak-sea views.

15:30-17:00 | West Coast cliff walkway if energy and weather allow.

17:00-18:00 | Cableway down and return to hotel.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner near hotel or in Shangrao / Yushan.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Cableway queues can be long. Arrive early and confirm last descending time.

- Do not try to walk every minor viewpoint; prioritize Nanqing Garden + one coast route.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Lunch: bring simple food because mountain food is limited.

- Dinner: choose local Jiangxi dishes, chicken soup or noodle shops near the hotel.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- If you will transfer to Wuyishan on Day 8, stay where next morning transport is easiest.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Sanqingshan weather can change quickly; a brief fog window can still produce excellent scenery.

- Use trekking shoes; cliff walkways can be wet.

Backup Plan

- Foggy day: keep Nanqing Garden short loop and wait for moving clouds.

- Fear of heights: reduce West Coast and use Sunshine Coast / Nanqing Garden only.
