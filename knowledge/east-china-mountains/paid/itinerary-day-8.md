## Day 8 | Sanqingshan Flexible Morning / Transfer to Wuyishan

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Use the morning for a short second look if weather is good, then transfer to Wuyishan. |
| Route | Sanqingshan / Shangrao -> Wuyishan |
| Overnight | Wuyishan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,800 / USD 111-389 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

Morning Option A | If weather was poor on Day 7, do a short Sanqingshan re-entry or nearby viewpoint only if logistics allow.

Morning Option B | If Day 7 was complete, rest and transfer earlier.

11:00-15:30 | Car to rail station + high-speed rail toward Wuyishan / Nanping.

15:30-17:30 | Arrive in Wuyishan, check in and confirm bamboo-raft reservation.

18:30-20:00 | Wuyishan dinner: smoked goose, red-eye fish or tea-flavored dishes.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Use Shangrao as the practical rail hub. Confirm Wuyishan North / East / Nanping station before booking.

- Do not schedule a full Sanqingshan day and a long transfer on the same day.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Dinner: Wuyi local cuisine restaurant; try Langu smoked goose, red-eye fish and vegetables cooked with tea flavor.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay in Sangu Resort or near Wuyishan scenic entrances. This saves time for Days 9-12.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Reserve the Nine-Bend Stream bamboo raft early if traveling in peak season.

- Confirm scenic shuttle package validity.

Backup Plan

- If rail delay occurs, skip all evening activities and only check in.

- If bamboo raft slots are unavailable, swap Day 9 and Day 10 activities.
