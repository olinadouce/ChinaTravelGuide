## Day 4 | Huangshan Core Day 2: Sunrise - Bright Summit - Yuping - Descend

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Second Huangshan day: sunrise, high ridge walking and the Greeting Pine / Yuping front-mountain exit. |
| Route | Mountain-top hotel -> Yuping -> Tangkou / Huangshan City |
| Overnight | Tangkou / Huangshan City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

05:00-07:00 | Sunrise attempt. Choose Bright Summit, Lion Peak, Refreshing Terrace or a hotel-advised viewpoint.

07:00-08:30 | Breakfast and check out from mountain hotel.

08:30-11:30 | Walk through Bright Summit / Aoyu Peak / Baiyun area depending on hotel location.

11:30-13:00 | Simple lunch or snack stop.

13:00-15:30 | Yuping area: Greeting Pine, nearby rock formations and photo stops.

15:30-17:30 | Descend by Yuping Cableway or alternative route, shuttle back to Tangkou.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner in Tangkou or Huangshan City.

Transport and Distance Notes

- The mountain route is weather-sensitive. Cableway queues can be long during holidays, so do not leave descent too late.

- Hundred-Step Cloud Ladder and Aoyu Peak steps can be tiring; reduce side routes if knees are sore.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- After descending, choose a proper Hui cuisine dinner in Tangkou or Tunxi. This is better than eating too much on the mountain.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- If continuing to Sanqingshan next day, stay in Huangshan City / Tunxi or near Huangshan North. If you want a slower recovery, stay one more night in Tangkou.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Check cableway closing time in the morning.

- Do not miss last shuttle / cableway.

- Keep passport and hotel booking accessible.

Backup Plan

- Rain or heavy fog: descend earlier and use hot spring / Tunxi rest.

- Knee pain: use cableway and skip extra peak climbs.
