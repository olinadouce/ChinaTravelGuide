## Day 5 | Huangshan Flexible Recovery Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | A buffer day after Huangshan: choose nature backup, cultural village, hot spring or rest according to weather and body condition. |
| Route | Huangshan area |
| Overnight | Huangshan City / Tunxi |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 600-2,000 / USD 83-278 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-09:30 | Slow breakfast and energy check.

09:30-12:30 | Option A: Emerald Valley or mountain-foot scenic area for water and easier walking.

09:30-15:30 | Option B: Hongcun / Xidi if you want a cultural buffer with village scenery.

Afternoon | Option C: hot spring / café / hotel rest if legs are tired.

18:00-20:00 | Hui cuisine dinner and confirm Day 6 transfer.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Use this day as a pressure-release day. Do not force another difficult hike after Huangshan.

- If transferring from Tunxi to another city next day, stay near Huangshan North or city center.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Hui cuisine: stinky mandarin fish, Mao tofu, bamboo shoots, preserved pork and Huangshan pastry.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay in the same Tunxi / Huangshan City hotel if possible; moving hotels creates unnecessary effort.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- This is a good laundry and rest day.

- Confirm rail ticket to Shangrao / Yushan and the Sanqingshan hotel address.

Backup Plan

- Rain: choose museum / old street / rest.

- Low energy: skip all optional attractions and sleep.
