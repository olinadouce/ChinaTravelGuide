## Practical Tips for International Visitors

Documents and Ticketing

- Carry your original passport; do not rely only on photos. Hotels, railway stations, cableway ticket checks and scenic-area real-name systems may require the physical document.

- If an online ticketing system does not accept your passport, try Trip.com / Ctrip international version, ask hotel front desk staff, or use the manual ticket window.

- For Huangshan mountain-top hotels, Wuyishan bamboo rafts and holiday-period cableways, confirm bookings at least 1-3 days in advance.

Payment and Cash

- Use Alipay as your main payment tool and WeChat Pay as backup. Carry Visa / Mastercard and RMB 500-1,500 cash.

- Mountain snack stalls, luggage help, small shops and temporary drivers may require cash.

Luggage and Gear

- For Huangshan mountain-top stay, pack a small overnight backpack only. Store large luggage at Tangkou, Huangshan North, hotel or with a driver.

- Essential gear: non-slip shoes, raincoat, warm layer, power bank, waterproof phone bag, trekking pole, snacks and motion-sickness medicine.

- Do not wear sandals on Huangshan, Sanqingshan or Wuyishan rock stair routes.
