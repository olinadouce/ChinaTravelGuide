## Day 6 | Huangshan - Shangrao / Yushan / Sanqingshan Foot Area

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Transfer toward Sanqingshan. The goal is to position well for an early cableway start on Day 7. |
| Route | Huangshan -> Shangrao / Yushan -> Sanqingshan area |
| Overnight | Shangrao / Yushan / Sanqingshan foot |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,500 / USD 97-347 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and check out.

09:00-13:30 | High-speed rail or rail combination toward Shangrao / Yushan.

13:30-15:30 | Transfer to Sanqingshan foot hotel or stay in Shangrao / Yushan depending on schedule.

16:00-18:00 | Rest and confirm cableway entrance, ticket, weather and opening time.

18:00-20:00 | Local dinner: rice noodles, chicken soup or simple Jiangxi food.

Transport and Distance Notes

- There are multiple possible rail stations. Confirm whether your train arrives at Shangrao, Yushan South or another station.

- If staying near the cableway, private car from station is easier with luggage.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Try Yanshan hot rice noodles, Shangrao chicken legs or local chicken soup.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay near the cableway for early entry; stay in Shangrao / Yushan for transport and dining convenience.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Save the cableway name in Chinese.

- Ask the hotel about earliest breakfast and taxi to the cableway.

Backup Plan

- Late rail arrival: sleep in Shangrao / Yushan and start Sanqingshan later.

- Bad weather: prepare a shorter Sanqingshan loop.
