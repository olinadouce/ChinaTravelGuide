## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Hangzhou / Shanghai / Huangshan | Arrival, rest, payment and transport check | Hangzhou / Huangshan City |
| Day 2 | Hangzhou / Shanghai - Huangshan North - Tangkou | High-speed rail to Huangshan, transfer to Tangkou or Tunxi, prepare for mountain entry | Tangkou / Tunxi |
| Day 3 | Huangshan Core Day 1 | Yungu Cableway, Shixin Peak, Beihai, Lion Peak, Paiyun Pavilion, upper Xihai Grand Canyon | On Huangshan Mountain |
| Day 4 | Huangshan Core Day 2 | Sunrise, Bright Summit, Aoyu Peak, Hundred-Step Cloud Ladder, Yuping, Greeting Pine, descend by Yuping Cableway | Tangkou / Huangshan City |
| Day 5 | Huangshan Flexible Day | Emerald Valley, Hongcun / Xidi optional cultural buffer, hot spring or rest | Huangshan City / Tunxi |
| Day 6 | Huangshan - Shangrao / Yushan | Rail transfer toward Sanqingshan; arrival rest and local food | Shangrao / Yushan / Sanqingshan foot area |
| Day 7 | Sanqingshan Full Day | Nanqing Garden, Goddess Peak, Python Emerging from the Mountain, Sunshine Coast, West Coast | Sanqingshan / Shangrao |
| Day 8 | Sanqingshan Flexible / Transfer Day | Short Sanqingshan route or transfer toward Wuyishan | Wuyishan |
| Day 9 | Wuyishan Core Day 1 | Tianyou Peak, Nine-Bend Stream overview, Wuyi Palace or nearby river walk | Wuyishan |
| Day 10 | Wuyishan Core Day 2 | Nine-Bend Stream bamboo rafting, Wuyi Palace, Song Street, Jade Maiden Peak viewpoint | Wuyishan |
| Day 11 | Wuyishan Tea Valley Day | Dahongpao, Rock-Bone Flower-Fragrance trail, Water Curtain Cave and tea experience | Wuyishan |
| Day 12 | Wuyishan Danxia Walking Day | One-Line-Sky, Tiger Roaring Rock, Roaring Tiger Eight Views or low-energy alternative | Wuyishan |
| Day 13 | Wuyishan Buffer / Departure City | Rest, optional short scenic walk, transfer to Nanping / Fuzhou / Xiamen / Shanghai | Departure city buffer |
| Day 14 | Departure | No distant scenic area; airport or high-speed rail departure | - |
