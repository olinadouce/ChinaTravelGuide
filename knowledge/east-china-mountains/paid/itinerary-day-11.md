## Day 11 | Wuyishan Tea Valley Day: Dahongpao - Water Curtain Cave

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | A slower walking day through tea landscapes, rock walls and shaded valleys. |
| Route | Dahongpao -> Rock-Bone Flower-Fragrance Trail -> Water Curtain Cave |
| Overnight | Wuyishan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,300 / USD 97-319 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and transfer to Dahongpao area.

09:30-11:30 | Dahongpao mother-tree area and tea valley viewpoints.

11:30-13:00 | Lunch / tea break.

13:00-15:30 | Rock-Bone Flower-Fragrance trail toward Water Curtain Cave.

15:30-17:00 | Tea tasting or return to hotel.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner with tea-flavored dishes or local smoked goose.

Transport and Distance Notes

- This is the best day to understand Wuyishan as a tea landscape, not just a mountain.

- The path can be slippery in rain; wear proper shoes.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Try tea-flavored dishes, smoked goose and rock-tea tasting. Ask prices clearly before long tea sessions.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Same Wuyishan hotel. Staying in one place keeps the route comfortable.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Do not buy expensive tea without comparing prices.

- Keep walking distance moderate after several mountain days.

Backup Plan

- Heavy rain: do a shorter Dahongpao visit + indoor tea tasting.

- Low energy: skip Water Curtain Cave and return early.
