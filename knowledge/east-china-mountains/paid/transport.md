## Intercity Transport, Public Transport Use and Price Table

| Segment | Best Option | Time | Reference Price | Suitable For | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hangzhou / Shanghai airport -> city hotel | Taxi / airport hotel shuttle / metro where convenient | 30-90 min | RMB 50-250 / USD 7-35 | Arrival visitors | Do not make the arrival day too complex; late arrivals should sleep near the station or airport. |
| Hangzhou / Shanghai -> Huangshan North | High-speed rail | 2-4.5 hrs | RMB 120-400 / USD 17-56 | Most visitors | Book on 12306, Trip.com or at the station with passport. |
| Huangshan North -> Tangkou / South Gate | Bus / taxi / private transfer | 50-90 min | RMB 30-250 / USD 4-35 | Huangshan visitors | Private transfer is easier with luggage; buses are cheaper but require timing checks. |
| Tangkou -> Huangshan mountain gates | Scenic shuttle | 20-40 min | RMB 19-30 / USD 3-4 | All mountain visitors | Private cars normally cannot go directly to Yungu Temple or Ciguang Pavilion. |
| Huangshan City / North Station -> Shangrao / Yushan | High-speed rail + taxi/private car | 2.5-5 hrs | RMB 120-500 / USD 17-69 | Sanqingshan transfer | Check whether your train arrives at Shangrao, Yushan South or another station. |
| Shangrao / Yushan -> Sanqingshan cableway area | Taxi / private car / tourist bus | 1-2 hrs | RMB 50-350 / USD 7-49 | Sanqingshan visitors | Private car is recommended if carrying luggage or arriving late. |
| Sanqingshan -> Wuyishan | Car to rail station + high-speed rail + taxi | 3-5.5 hrs | RMB 150-600 / USD 21-83 | Most visitors | Use Shangrao as the practical rail hub; confirm Wuyishan North / East station. |
| Wuyishan Resort -> scenic entrances | Taxi / hotel drop-off / scenic shuttle | 10-30 min | RMB 20-80 / USD 3-11 | Wuyishan visitors | Stay in Sangu Resort to reduce transfer friction. |
| Wuyishan -> Nanping / Fuzhou / Xiamen | High-speed rail or private transfer | 1-4 hrs | RMB 80-500 / USD 11-69 | Departure visitors | Choose final city according to flight or rail departure. |
