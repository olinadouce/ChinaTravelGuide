## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For and Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Regular hotels / guesthouses, rail where possible, simple meals, fewer private cars | RMB 10,500-15,500 | USD 1,458-2,153 | Suitable for young travelers or visitors with China independent-travel experience. More transfers and lower comfort. |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Comfort hotels, mountain-top stay on Huangshan, key private transfers, local restaurants | RMB 18,000-28,000 | USD 2,500-3,889 | Best for most international visitors. Balances comfort, scenery and transport reliability. |
| High-End | High-end hotels / resorts, private transfers, better mountain hotels, selected restaurants | RMB 35,000-60,000 | USD 4,861-8,333 | Suitable for families, couples and high-budget visitors. Reduces transfer and communication pressure. |
