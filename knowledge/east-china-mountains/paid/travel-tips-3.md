## Risks and Backup Plans

| Risk | Possible Impact | Backup Plan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rain / Thunderstorm | Cableways, cliff paths, Xihai Grand Canyon, Sanqingshan walkways and Wuyishan rafting may be affected. | Huangshan: shorten to Beihai / Bright Summit / Yuping; Sanqingshan: short Nanqing Garden loop; Wuyishan: tea route, Wuyi Palace or indoor tea tasting. |
| Poor Visibility | Clouds may hide major viewpoints, especially on Huangshan and Sanqingshan. | Wait at major viewpoints if clouds are moving. If visibility stays poor, reduce deep walking and focus on safe routes. |
| Low Energy / Knee Pain | Long stairs and multi-day mountain walking can cause fatigue. | Use cableways, avoid deep Xihai canyon, reduce Sanqingshan West Coast, and replace Wuyishan Tiger Roaring Rock with Wuyi Palace or tea tasting. |
| Cableway / Raft Suspension | Weather, maintenance or queues may disrupt the planned route. | Keep flexible day order. If Wuyishan rafting stops, climb Tianyou Peak or walk Dahongpao. If Huangshan cableway queues are too long, descend earlier. |
| Ticketing Failure | Passport-based online reservations may fail or holiday quotas may sell out. | Use Trip.com / Ctrip, hotel assistance or manual ticket windows. Avoid relying on same-day tickets during holidays. |
| Transport Delay | Rail transfers and mountain-road connections can shift timing. | Use transfer days as transfer days. Stay near departure hubs before flights. Do not combine a full scenic area with a major departure. |
