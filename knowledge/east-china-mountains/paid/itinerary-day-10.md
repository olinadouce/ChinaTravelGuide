## Day 10 | Wuyishan Core Day 2: Nine-Bend Stream Bamboo Raft

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The signature Wuyishan water experience: see the red cliffs from the river. |
| Route | Nine-Bend Stream + Wuyi Palace |
| Overnight | Wuyishan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,600 / USD 125-361 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and go to bamboo-raft pier according to reserved time.

Morning | Nine-Bend Stream bamboo raft (about 1.5-2 hours). Keep valuables dry and follow staff instructions.

Late morning | Arrive near Wuyi Palace / Song Street area and walk slowly.

12:30-14:00 | Lunch in Sangu or near Wuyi Palace.

14:00-16:30 | Jade Maiden Peak viewpoint or relaxed riverbank photography.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner and optional Impression Dahongpao show if interested.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Bamboo raft time slots matter. Arrive early and do not miss the reserved time.

- Rafting may be suspended during unsafe water or weather conditions.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Lunch: Zixi rice noodles or local dishes.

- Dinner: Langu smoked goose, red-eye fish or tea cuisine.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay in Sangu Resort; it is convenient for dinner, show, taxi and next-day scenic entry.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Use a waterproof bag for phone, passport and wallet.

- If you do the evening show, avoid an overly heavy dinner beforehand.

Backup Plan

- Raft stops: replace with Tianyou Peak, Dahongpao trail or tea tasting.

- Low energy: raft + Wuyi Palace only.
