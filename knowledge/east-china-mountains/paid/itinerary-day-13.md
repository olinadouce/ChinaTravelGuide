## Day 13 | Wuyishan Buffer / Transfer to Departure City

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Recovery and departure preparation day. Choose a short optional activity or move to a final transport city. |
| Route | Wuyishan -> Nanping / Fuzhou / Xiamen / Shanghai |
| Overnight | Departure city buffer |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 600-2,200 / USD 83-306 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-10:00 | Breakfast and slow packing.

Morning Option A | Short Wuyishan walk, tea shopping or café if departure is late.

Morning Option B | Transfer earlier to Nanping / Fuzhou / Xiamen if your next-day flight is early.

Afternoon | Rail or car transfer to departure city.

Evening | Simple dinner near hotel or station.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Do not plan a full scenic day before a major departure.

- For international flights, staying near the airport or rail station the night before is safer.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Choose a reliable local restaurant near the final hotel, not a distant destination restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay near departure airport or high-speed rail station.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Check passport, tickets, charging cables and payment tools.

- Leave enough time for traffic and station security.

Backup Plan

- Transport delay: cancel optional activity and go directly to departure city.

- Bad weather: move earlier rather than staying in the scenic area.
