## Day 3 | Huangshan Core Day 1: Yungu Cableway - Beihai - Xihai

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | First major mountain day: rear-mountain entry, pine-and-rock viewpoints, and upper Xihai Grand Canyon if weather and energy allow. |
| Route | Tangkou -> Huangshan Scenic Area -> mountain-top hotel |
| Overnight | On Huangshan Mountain |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 1,500-4,000 / USD 208-556 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

06:30-07:30 | Breakfast, store large luggage and go to Tangkou Transfer Center.

07:30-09:00 | Scenic shuttle to Yungu Temple, then Yungu Cableway uphill.

09:00-11:30 | Shixin Peak and Beihai area: focus on ancient pines, stone peaks and short viewpoint loops.

11:30-12:30 | Simple lunch near Beihai / hotel area.

13:00-16:30 | Paiyun Pavilion and upper Xihai Grand Canyon. Walk only as deep as weather and knees allow.

17:00-18:30 | Sunset attempt at Paiyun Pavilion, Bright Summit or a viewpoint near your hotel.

18:30-20:00 | Mountain hotel dinner and early rest.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Tangkou -> scenic shuttle -> Yungu Cableway is the easiest first entry route for most foreign visitors.

- Xihai Grand Canyon has steep sections. Use the sightseeing monorail if descending deeply and saving energy.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Lunch/dinner: mountain hotel restaurant or simple scenic-area food. Bring snacks because choices are limited and expensive.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Stay at Baiyun, Xihai, Shilin, Beihai or similar mountain-top hotels according to route and sunrise plan. Book early.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Take only a small backpack.

- Weather changes quickly; keep raincoat and warm layer ready.

- Do not enter deep canyon routes in thunderstorm or icy conditions.

Backup Plan

- Poor visibility: focus on Shixin Peak and Beihai short loop; skip Xihai deep canyon.

- Low energy: go from Beihai to hotel early and save strength for Day 4.
