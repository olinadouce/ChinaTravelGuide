## Day 1 | Arrival in Hangzhou / Shanghai / Huangshan

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, rest, payment setup and transport check. Do not plan intensive sightseeing. |
| Route | Arrival city -> hotel |
| Overnight | Hangzhou / Huangshan City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 500-1,800 / USD 69-250 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

After arrival | Airport / rail station to hotel (30-90 minutes). Use taxi, hotel shuttle or a simple metro route only if convenient.

16:00-18:00 | Rest at the hotel and check Alipay, WeChat Pay, cash, passport, train tickets and translation app.

18:30-20:00 | Easy dinner. In Huangshan City, try Hui cuisine; in Hangzhou / Shanghai, choose a stable restaurant near the hotel.

After 20:00 | Confirm Day 2 rail station, train time, Huangshan North arrival, and the Chinese address of your Tangkou / Tunxi hotel.

Transport and Distance Notes

- The goal is a smooth start, not saving money through complicated transfers.

- Huangshan North Station is not the same as Huangshan Scenic Area; Tangkou still requires about 50-90 minutes by road.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- If staying in Huangshan City: Laojie Diyi Lou, Piyun Huifu Cuisine or Huangshan pastry for a light snack.

- If staying in Hangzhou / Shanghai: choose a restaurant near the hotel; do not cross the city for dinner.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Late arrivals: stay near the airport or rail station.

- Early arrivals: continue to Huangshan City or Tangkou if the rail schedule is comfortable.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Save all Chinese addresses.

- Prepare RMB 500-1,000 in cash.

- Do not place your passport in checked or stored luggage.

Backup Plan

- Flight delay: cancel dinner outside and rest.

- Payment failure: use cash/card first and ask the hotel for help the next morning.
