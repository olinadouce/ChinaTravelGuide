## Day 14 | Departure Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | No distant scenic area. Focus on smooth departure. |
| Route | Departure city -> next destination |
| Overnight | - |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 100-600 / USD 14-83 excluding long-distance tickets |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

Morning | Hotel breakfast, final packing and document check.

Depending on departure time | Optional short walk or simple meal near hotel only.

Before departure | Go to airport / railway station. Arrive early for passport checks and security.

Transport and Distance Notes

- Airport / station transfer usually takes 30-90 minutes depending on city and hotel.

- Confirm station name carefully; many cities have more than one rail station.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Use hotel breakfast or a simple restaurant near the station / airport.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- No planned accommodation. If delayed, choose a chain hotel near the station or airport.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Do not schedule distant scenic sites.

- Carry passport, rail/flight booking and payment tools together.

Backup Plan

- If flight or train changes, stay near the departure hub and avoid returning to Wuyishan scenic area.
