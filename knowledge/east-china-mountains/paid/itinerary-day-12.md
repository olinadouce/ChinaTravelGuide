## Day 12 | Wuyishan Danxia Walking Day: One-Line-Sky / Tiger Roaring Rock

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | A more physical Danxia day with narrow passages, rock stairs and compact viewpoints. |
| Route | One-Line-Sky / Tiger Roaring Rock / low-energy alternative |
| Overnight | Wuyishan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,300 / USD 97-319 |

Recommended Route (in Suggested Order)

08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and choose the route based on body condition.

09:30-11:30 | One-Line-Sky: narrow rock passage experience; not suitable for claustrophobic visitors.

11:30-13:00 | Lunch and rest.

13:00-16:00 | Tiger Roaring Rock / Roaring Tiger Eight Views for Danxia stairs and viewpoints.

16:00-17:30 | Return to hotel, rest and prepare for transfer day.

18:00-20:00 | Final Wuyishan dinner.

Transport and Distance Notes

- This day is optional in intensity. If you are tired, do not force both One-Line-Sky and Tiger Roaring Rock.

- Rock passages and steps can be wet or narrow.

Dining Suggestions for the Day

- Dinner: choose a concrete Wuyishan restaurant such as a well-rated local cuisine place in Sangu, Langu smoked goose shop or a resort restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions for the Day

- Continue Wuyishan hotel; pack tonight for Day 13 transfer.

Foreign Visitor Notes

- Avoid narrow cave routes if you dislike tight spaces.

- Do not wear sandals on rock stairs.

Backup Plan

- Low energy: replace with Wuyi Palace, Jade Maiden Peak viewpoint and tea tasting.

- Rain: reduce rock stair sections and keep only easy scenic walks.
