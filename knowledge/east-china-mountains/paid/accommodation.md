## Accommodation Guide: Location, Foreign-Visitor Convenience and Reasons to Stay

| Destination | Recommended Stay | Location | Why Recommended | Foreign-Visitor Convenience | Reference Average Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hangzhou / Shanghai | Airport or railway-station chain hotels | Near international arrival airport or main high-speed rail station | Best if arriving late; reduces first-night stress before mountain transfers. | High | RMB 400-1,200 / USD 56-167 |
| Huangshan City / Tunxi | Crowne Plaza Huangshan Yucheng / international or upper-mid hotels | Tunxi urban area, near restaurants and transport | Good for late arrivals and easier passport check-in; more dining choices than Tangkou. | High | RMB 500-1,200 / USD 69-167 |
| Huangshan City / Tunxi | Old Street / Tunxi comfort hotels | Near Tunxi Old Street or city center | Convenient for Hui cuisine and pre-mountain supplies. Confirm passport registration if using small inns. | Medium-High | RMB 250-700 / USD 35-97 |
| Tangkou | Transfer Center area hotels | Near Huangshan South Gate / Tangkou Transfer Center | Best for early mountain entry. Ask whether the hotel can store large luggage. | Confirm before booking | RMB 250-800 / USD 35-111 |
| Huangshan Mountain-top | Baiyun Hotel / Xihai Hotel / Shilin Hotel / Beihai Hotel type stays | Inside Huangshan Scenic Area | Required for convenient sunrise or sunset. Expensive and limited; book early. | Medium-High | RMB 900-3,000+ / USD 125-417+ |
| Shangrao / Yushan | Hilton Sanqingshan Resort or similar resort hotels | Near Sanqingshan scenic area or cableway access | Useful for early cableway entry and more stable service. | Medium-High | RMB 600-1,500 / USD 83-208 |
| Sanqingshan foot area | Cableway-area guesthouses / comfort hotels | Near Jinsha or Waishuangxi cableway | Most efficient for a full Sanqingshan day; confirm passport registration and breakfast time. | Confirm before booking | RMB 250-900 / USD 35-125 |
| Wuyishan | C&D Resort Wuyi Mountain / Wuyi Mountain Yuehua type hotels | Sangu Resort or near scenic entrances | Best balance of transport, dining and scenic access; reliable for foreign visitors. | High | RMB 600-1,600 / USD 83-222 |
| Wuyishan | Dahongpao Resort / tea-themed hotels | Near resort area or tea valleys | Good for tea landscape atmosphere and slower final days. | Medium-High | RMB 500-1,500 / USD 69-208 |
| Final night city | Nanping / Fuzhou / Xiamen airport or station hotels | Near departure station or airport | Use as a buffer if departing early. Do not stay deep inside Wuyishan if you need an early flight. | High | RMB 350-1,200 / USD 49-167 |

| Accommodation note: International visitors normally need the original passport to check in. International brands, higher-star hotels and major-platform hotels usually handle foreign guests more smoothly. Mountain-top hotels, small Tangkou inns, Sanqingshan foot guesthouses and tea-valley stays should be contacted before payment to confirm passport check-in. |
