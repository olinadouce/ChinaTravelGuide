## Tour Highlights

You will trace the flow of time through three distinguished museums that showcase the splendid civilizations of the Zhou, Qin, Han and Tang dynasties. The Shaanxi History Museum preserves relics spanning thousands of years and presents the full vicissitudes of Chinese civilization; the Xi'an Museum integrates ancient temples and pagodas, letting you appreciate the timeless and gentle cultural heritage of Chang'an; and the Mausoleum of the First Qin Emperor Scenic Area revives the mighty spirit of the Qin Dynasty and reveals the extraordinary grandeur of the imperial mausoleum. You can climb the Ming City Wall from Yongning Gate, stroll along the aged brick paths, overlook the bustling Zhuque Avenue, and immerse yourself in the six-centuries-old epic, feeling the distinctive charm where ancient traditions meet modern prosperity in Chang'an. Explore two popular food districts, the Muslim Quarter and Yongxingfang, and delight your taste buds with crispy and hearty roujiamo, rich and fragrant lamb pita soup, as well as soft and sweet steamed rice cake to experience the classic flavours of Chang'an.

The more than 1,000 soldiers' terracotta statues unearthed here have different appearances and vivid expressions, and are a brilliant pearl in the history of ancient Chinese sculpture art. It is hailed as "one of the great discoveries in the history of archaeology in the 20th century".
