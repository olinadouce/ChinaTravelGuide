## Day 4 Yuanyang → Duoyishu Terraced Fields → Azheke Terraced Fields

→ Terraced Fields Citywalk → Mile

(Approx. 250 km, driving time: 4 hours)

06:00 — Pack breakfast from the hotel (most hotels only offer takeaway breakfast for

sunrise viewing).

06:30—Arrive at 【Duoyishu Terraced Fields】 before sunrise. This is the best spot to

watch the morning scenery in Yuanyang. Pugao Old Village, colorful dawn clouds, sea of

clouds, terraced fields, villages and morning mist create a poetic and magnificent view.

09:30—Arrive at 【Azheke Village】, a filming location of Forever Young. It has original

Hani mushroom houses, quiet and simple atmosphere, and rich ethnic culture. Local Hani

guides will take you on a tour and share Hani traditions in simple and authentic words.

13:00—Depart for【Mile】. Check in at a hot spring hotel and enjoy free time & hot spring

soaking.

17:30—Free activities.

Tips:

You will stay at a hot spring hotel in Mile today; please bring your swimsuit.
