## Day 5 Mile → Dongfengyun Town → Kunming Airport & Tour Ends

(Approx. 140 km, driving time: about 2 hours)

08:30—Depart after breakfast at the hotel.

09:00—Head to the popular scenic spot 【Dongfengyun Art Town】 – an artistic town

integrating leisure vacation, Buddhist culture, folk custom experience and ecological

sightseeing. The buildings in the scenic area are mainly built with local red bricks, blending

three local cultures with distinctive design.

The bottle-shaped buildings represent Mile’s red wine culture, Yi people’s fire culture, and

the spirit of mutual growth.

15:00—Arrive at【Kunming Changshui International Airport】

Tips:

For passengers returning from Kunming Airport, it is recommended to book flights

after 16:30 on the same day.
