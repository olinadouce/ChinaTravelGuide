## Day 2 Kunming → Fuxian Lake → Jianshui Shuanglong Bridge

(Approx. 210 km, driving time: 3.5 hours)

08:30—Depart after breakfast at the hotel.

09:30— Arrive at 【Fuxian Lake】 — the largest freshwater lake by water volume in

China, with crystal-clear, unpolluted water. The plateau breeze ripples the vast blue lake.

We will visit the highlight 【 Lucong Scenic Area】 , walk along the beach, and climb

Bijiashan Hill for a panoramic view of Fuxian Lake.

(Bijiashan has gentle stone steps; it takes about 20 minutes to reach the top, easy and

suitable for all ages.)

11:30—Lunch

12:30—Drive around the lake. Walk along the Time Plank Road built above the lake, enjoy

reeds and gentle waves, stop to take photos, and enjoy afternoon tea with a full view of the

brilliant blue Fuxian Lake.

17:00—Arrive in【Jianshui】. Check in, then walk through Chaoyang Tower (No.1 Tower

of Southern Yunnan) — known as the predecessor of the Tiananmen Tower. Stroll along

Lin’an Old Street, visit Dabojing Ancient Well, and explore local tofu workshops.

19:00 — Dinner recommendations: grilled tofu, steam pot chicken, grass sprout rice

noodles, barbecue.

Tips:

The drive is quite long today; prepare some snacks for the trip.

Jianshui Ancient City has abundant night food, but most dishes are spicy.
