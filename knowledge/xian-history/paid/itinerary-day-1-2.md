## Day 1: Departure → Kunming

If you wish to explore Kunming on your own, you may visit 【 Dianchi Lake 】 for a

lakeside stroll or boat ride. Nearby are Daguan Park, West Mountain Forest Park and

Yunnan Nationalities Village, which faces them across the water. You can also visit

Yunnan Nationalities Village to experience the diverse ethnic cultures of Yunnan. For local

food, Crossing-the-Bridge Rice Noodles, Flower Cakes and Steam Pot Chicken are highly

recommended.

 Kunming Old Street

 Admission: Free

 Opening Hours: All day

 Haigang Dam

 Admission: Free

 Opening Hours: All day

 Jinma Bijifang

 Location: Landmark & city center of Kunming

 Admission: Free

 Opening Hours: All day
