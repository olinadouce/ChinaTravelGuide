## Product Message

To poets, Chang'an was the land of ideals, a dream high above the mortal world.

Meng Jiao wrote:

Riding on elation, my horse gallops fast;

I view all blossoms of Chang'an in one day's past.

Bai Juyi wrote:

Beneath the Big Wild Goose Pagoda my name stands tall;

Among seventeen top scholars, I am the youngest of all.

Wang Wei wrote:

Heavenly gates swing open to reveal imperial halls;

Envoys from every land bow before the royal thrones.

Li Bai wrote:

My longing lingers always, right here in Chang'an.

This city once witnessed gallant youths in fine robes and on fiery steeds, and also bore witness to sorrowful verses of unfulfilled ambitions.

When longing drifts back to Chang'an and the glorious Tang Dynasty, no heart can resist its allure.

When longing stays with Chang'an, we dwell in days of poetry and wine, with youthful passion and unreserved spirits.

Stand in Xi'an, and gaze upon the eternal grace of ancient Chang'an!
