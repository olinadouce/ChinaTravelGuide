## Timeless Elegance, Roam Chang'an

——Xi'an Double-Flight Five-Day Tour

Product Message

To poets, Chang'an was the land of ideals, a dream high above the mortal world.

Meng Jiao wrote:

Riding on elation, my horse gallops fast;

I view all blossoms of Chang'an in one day's past.

Bai Juyi wrote:

Beneath the Big Wild Goose Pagoda my name stands tall;

Among seventeen top scholars, I am the youngest of all.

Wang Wei wrote:

Heavenly gates swing open to reveal imperial halls;

Envoys from every land bow before the royal thrones.

Li Bai wrote:

My longing lingers always, right here in Chang'an.

This city once witnessed gallant youths in fine robes and on fiery steeds, and also

bore witness to sorrowful verses of unfulfilled ambitions.

When longing drifts back to Chang'an and the glorious Tang Dynasty, no heart can

resist its allure.

When longing stays with Chang'an, we dwell in days of poetry and wine, with

youthful passion and unreserved spirits.

Stand in Xi'an, and gaze upon the eternal grace of ancient Chang'an!

Tour Highlights

You will trace the flow of time through three distinguished museums that showcase

the splendid civilizations of the Zhou, Qin, Han and Tang dynasties. The Shaanxi

History Museum preserves relics spanning thousands of years and presents the full

vicissitudes of Chinese civilization; the Xi'an Museum integrates ancient temples

and pagodas, letting you appreciate the timeless and gentle cultural heritage of

Chang'an; and the Mausoleum of the First Qin Emperor Scenic Area revives the

mighty spirit of the Qin Dynasty and reveals the extraordinary grandeur of the

imperial mausoleum. You can climb the Ming City Wall from Yongning Gate,

stroll along the aged brick paths, overlook the bustling Zhuque Avenue, and immerse

yourself in the six-centuries-old epic, feeling the distinctive charm where ancient

traditions meet modern prosperity in Chang'an. Explore two popular food districts,

the Muslim Quarter and Yongxingfang, and delight your taste buds with crispy and

hearty roujiamo, rich and fragrant lamb pita soup, as well as soft and sweet steamed

rice cake to experience the classic flavours of Chang'an.
