## 3. The economic development in the northwest region is lagging behind. The scale and facilities of

hotels in this region are inferior to those in Jiangsu, Zhejiang and other coastal areas (for example, a

three-star hotel is equivalent to a two-star hotel in developed areas). Newly-built and well-equipped

hotels are generally located around the city center, and the driving distance from the city center is

approximately 20 to 30 minutes.
