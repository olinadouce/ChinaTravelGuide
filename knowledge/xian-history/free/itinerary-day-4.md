## 5-Day 4-Night Southeast Yunnan Travel Guide

(Flight + Self-Driving)

Travel Tips

 Many areas in Yunnan have high altitude, which may cause altitude sickness such as

dizziness, headache, fatigue, or insomnia. Mild symptoms usually disappear within 1–2 days

as your body adapts. Avoid alcohol, tobacco, and overexertion before the trip; do not do

excessive aerobic exercise. You may take Rhodiola in advance. Eat more vegetables and

fruits, drink plenty of water, sleep well, and stay relaxed.

 Yunnan is on the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau with strong ultraviolet radiation. Prepare

sunscreen, sunglasses, and masks for protection.

 Long distances and scattered attractions mean long driving times. Prepare motion sickness

medicine. Temperature varies greatly in summer; bring basic medications to avoid

heatstroke.

 Yunnan is home to many ethnic minorities. Please respect local customs and traditions.
