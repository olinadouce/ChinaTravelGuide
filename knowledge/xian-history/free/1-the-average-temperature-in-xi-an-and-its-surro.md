## 1. The average temperature in Xi'an and its surrounding areas: 15-25℃ in spring and autumn, 25-35℃

in summer, and 0-10℃ in winter (with significant temperature difference in the morning and evening).

Please pay attention to the weather forecast before traveling! The average humidity throughout the year

is 40-60% (relatively dry), and tourists should replenish water during the trip.
