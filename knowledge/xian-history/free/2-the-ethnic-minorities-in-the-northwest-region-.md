## 2. The ethnic minorities in the northwest region mostly follow the Islamic religion and are Muslims.

They all follow halal dietary customs (mainly abstaining from pork). Tourists should pay attention to

their behavior and manners in areas where Muslims gather, respect the religious beliefs of the ethnic

minorities, and avoid unnecessary troubles and misunderstandings!
