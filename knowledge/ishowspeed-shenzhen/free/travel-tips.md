## Travel Tips

| Topic | What to Know |
| --- | --- |
| Payment | Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay with an international bank card before arrival. Carry a small amount of RMB as backup. |
| Internet | Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation and ride-hailing. |
| Crowds | Do not recreate crowd-chasing or unsafe livestream behavior. Add buffer time at popular sites. |
| Tickets | Use official attraction channels or recognized booking platforms and reconfirm operating details shortly before travel. |
| Hotel check-in | Book with the exact passport name and carry the original passport. |

| Timing Rule Ticket prices, opening hours, reservation rules and showroom availability can change. Reconfirm all operational details shortly before travel. |
