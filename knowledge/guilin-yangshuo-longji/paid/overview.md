GUILIN · YANGSHUO · LONGJI

Featured Theme Route · Light Hiking

Walk into China’s landscapes

A seven-day journey through Guilin’s water-and-mountain cityscape, Yangshuo’s Li River peak forests and Longji’s hillside rice terraces.

| Route Feel Easy to moderate scenic walks | Landscape Flow Water city · river valley · terraces | Suitable Travelers First-time South China nature travelers |
| --- | --- | --- |

This route turns classic Guangxi scenery into a walkable experience: an easy city entry, a scenic Li River transfer, riverbank and village walking in Yangshuo, and a final terrace-trail memory in Longji.

| Route Overview | From lakeside Guilin to valley Yangshuo, then up to Longji terraces. |
| --- | --- |

This route turns classic Guangxi scenery into a walkable experience: an easy city entry, a scenic Li River transfer, riverbank and village walking in Yangshuo, and a final terrace-trail memory in Longji.

| Guilin Water city entry Two Rivers and Four Lakes, cave landforms and the Elephant Trunk Hill riverside. | Li River Scenic transfer Peak forests, river bends, bamboo groves and villages unfold during the cruise. | Yangshuo Valley walking Yulong River paths, village roads, Gongnong Bridge and Ten-Mile Gallery. | Longji Terrace completion Stone roads, wooden villages, rice-field edges and morning viewpoints. |
