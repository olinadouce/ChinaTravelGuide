Walk into China’s landscapes.

From Guilin’s lakeside light to the Li River, Yangshuo’s countryside paths and Longji’s terrace trails, this seven-day route keeps the scenery close to the walk.

Route Rhythm

From water city to river valley, then up to terrace trails.

| 01 Guilin Entry Lakeside paths, bridge reflections and low-intensity city scenery open the route. | 02 Li River Transfer Peak forests, river bends and villages turn the move to Yangshuo into the day’s scenery. |
| --- | --- |
| 03 Yangshuo Walks Riverbanks, fields, village roads and karst peaks carry the light-hiking section. | 04 Longji Finish Stone roads, wooden villages and terrace-side trails close the journey in mountain light. |

Landscape Highlights

Four scenes shape the journey.

| Li River Peak forests along the water The river carries the route from Guilin into Yangshuo, with karst hills, bamboo groves and village edges changing at each bend. | Yulong River Fields, bridges and close peaks Walking beside the river keeps the scenery at human scale: rafts, farmland, village roads and mountain outlines stay in the same frame. |
| --- | --- |
|  |  |
| Xianggong Mountain A short climb above the river bend The elevated view brings the Li River, villages and layered peaks together before the route returns to riverside paths. | Longji Terraces Stone paths through hillside fields The final section changes from river valley to mountain farming landscape, with village roofs, field edges and morning light along the trail. |

Daily Route

Seven days, with walking kept close to the scenery.

|  | Day 1 Guilin arrival Enter the landscape beside the lakes. Arrive Check in near Two Rivers and Four Lakes, Elephant Trunk Hill or Zhengyang Pedestrian Street. Walk Take a short lakeside walk for water reflections, bridge light and distant mountain silhouettes. Close Keep the evening near the hotel and keep the hotel address ready for easy return. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 2 Guilin city walk Move from cave landform to riverside scenery. Start Visit Reed Flute Cave through fixed paths, stone steps and layered karst interiors. Walk Return downtown for an easy riverbank walk around Elephant Trunk Hill. Evening Use the lake area for a short stroll or early rest before the Li River transfer. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 3 Li River to Yangshuo Let the river carry the route into countryside peaks. Depart Leave Guilin for the Li River pier with the Chinese pier name and pickup point ready. Travel Cruise through river bends, bamboo groves, villages and layered karst peaks. Arrive After reaching Yangshuo, keep only a short walk near the hotel. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 4 Yulong River countryside Walk through riverbanks, fields and village roads. River path Start near Jiuxian, a Yulong River pier or the hotel area for a segmented countryside walk. Viewpoints Pause at Gongnong Bridge for river reflections, bamboo rafts and peaks on both sides. Return Use Ten-Mile Gallery through short walking stops and return before the evening becomes heavy. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 5 Xianggong Mountain and Xingping Climb briefly, then return to the Li River’s old-town edge. Climb Leave early for Xianggong Mountain and follow the steps to the river-bend viewpoint. Riverbank Walk in Xingping for old streets, ferry atmosphere and mountain shadows near the water. Prepare Return to Yangshuo and organize a light backpack for the Longji transfer. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 6 Yangshuo to Longji Rise from river valley into hillside terraces. Transfer Leave Yangshuo by reliable road transfer and keep luggage handling simple. Trail After check-in, walk village stone roads and field-edge paths near the guesthouse. Settle Keep the evening in the village; mountain roads are quiet after dark. |
| --- | --- |

|  | Day 7 Longji morning trail Close the route among terraces, villages and morning light. Morning Set out light for a nearby viewpoint through village roads and hillside paths. Return Come back by the same path or a short loop, pausing near field edges and wooden houses. Depart Return to Guilin or continue to the station or airport with enough buffer time. |
| --- | --- |

Travel Notes

Small preparations keep the route easy to follow.

| Walking Gear Non-slip shoes, a light backpack, rain cover, sun protection and water fit the river paths and terrace steps. | Address Cards Keep hotel names, piers, pickup points and village stays saved in Chinese for taxis and transfers. |
| --- | --- |
| Weather Rhythm Rainy or misty days can stay close to cave paths, riverside walks, village lanes and terrace layers. | Luggage Ease Longji has steps and village roads; keep large luggage with the stay or transfer service and walk with a light pack. |
