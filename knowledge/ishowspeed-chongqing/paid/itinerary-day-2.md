## Day 2 · Continue the Route and Depart

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00–11:00 | Xiahao Old Street | Walk a hillside neighborhood and view elevated roads. | Steep paths; wear supportive footwear. |
| 11:30–12:30 | Lunch | Try suanla fen, grilled fish or non-spicy options. | Hydrate in hot weather. |
| 13:00–14:30 | Yangtze River Cableway | Cross the river for aerial views of the city. | Queues can be long; ticketing and operating hours vary. It will take about 10 minutes’ drive and 20 minutes subway from Xiahao Old Street. |
| 15:00–17:00 | Chaotianmen / Raffles City riverside | Modern architecture and river confluence views. | This is a practical route extension. It will take about 20 minutes’ drive or subway from Yangtze River Cableway. |
| After 18:00 | Departure | Departure. | Avoid rushing after a late cruise. |

### After 18:00 · Departure

- Allow 60–90 minutes at Railway Station before departure.

- Use the exact English name and passport number shown on your travel document.

- Carry the original passport; some travelers may need the staffed inspection lane.

- Book through the official railway channel or a foreigner-friendly platform such as Trip.com.
