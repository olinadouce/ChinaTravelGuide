## Day 1 · 8D Cyber City

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00–10:00 | Jiefangbei | Start at the central monument and surrounding pedestrian streets. | Use this area for breakfast and orientation. |
| 10:00–11:30 | Kuixinglou | Experience the famous “ground floor on the 22nd level” urban layout. | Stay behind barriers; do not imitate backflips or unsafe stunts. |
| 12:00–13:00 | Lunch | Choose Chongqing noodles or a lighter local meal. | Save hotpot for dinner. |
| 13:30–15:00 | Liziba Station | Ride Line 2 and use the official viewing platform. | Peak periods are crowded; avoid blocking residents and station access. It will take about 10 minutes’ drive and 20 minutes subway from Kuixinglou. |
| 15:30–16:30 | Crown Grand Escalator | Ride the 112 m escalator between Lianglukou and Caiyuanba. | Hold the handrail and follow operating notices. It will take about 10 minutes’ drive and 20 minutes subway from Liziba Station. |
| 17:00–19:00 | Chongqing hotpot | Order a divided pot, beef, tripe, tofu and vegetables. | CNY 100–220 pp; spice can be intense. |
| 19:00-19:30 | Hongyadong | Explore the multi-level complex, view the illuminated stilt-house architecture, and photograph Hongyadong from the riverside or Qiansimen Bridge. | Extremely crowded after dark. Use designated crossings, avoid stopping on roads, and note that lighting times may vary by season. It will take about 15 minutes’ drive and 30 minutes subway from Crown Grand Escalator. |
| 19:30-21:30 | Choose one night tour | A. Two Rivers cruise · B. riverside walk. | Cruise schedules and lighting vary. |
