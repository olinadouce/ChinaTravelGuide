## Attraction & Booking Notes

| Stop / Experience | Admission | Suggested Time | Key Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Liziba viewing platform | Free | 45–60 min | For the train-through-building view, visit during Line 2 operating hours, approximately 06:30–22:30. |
| Crown Escalator | CNY 2 one way / CNY 4 round trip | 20–40 min | Open 08:00–20:00; extended to 08:00–21:00 on statutory public holidays. Tickets can be purchased on site or paid using a local transit card. |
| Yangtze River Cableway | Approx. CNY 20 one-way / 30 round trip | 45–90 min | Queues may exceed the ride time. Mar–Nov: 08:00–22:00; Dec–Feb: 08:00–21:00; statutory public holidays: 07:30–22:30. Enter at least 20 minutes before closing. Purchase through the official WeChat account and obtain a queue number. |
| Two Rivers cruise | From CNY 138-168 | 60–90 min | Confirm pier, route and weather in advance. Night cruises generally operate on a rolling basis from 19:30–22:00. Classic cruise: about CNY 138 / 45 min; premium cruise: about CNY 168 / 50 min. Pier, vessel and departure time depend on the selected ticket. Holiday fares and schedules may change. |
| Hongyadong exterior | Free | 45–90 min | Open approximately 09:00–23:00. Admission is free. Exterior lights generally operate around 18:00–23:00, but the switch-on time may vary with season and daylight conditions. |
