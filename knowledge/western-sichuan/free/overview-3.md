## Overall Itinerary

This free version gives the route structure and main planning logic. It intentionally keeps timing, hotels, restaurants and ticket choices broad so you can first judge whether the route fits your travel style.

| Stage | Route | Recommended Area to Stay | Food Ideas / Transfer Style |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1-2 | Arrive in Chengdu -> buffer day and light nature opening | Chengdu city center / Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li / Chengdu East area | Sichuan dishes, hotpot, panda-base morning visit · metro + taxi + rail preparation |
| Day 3-5 | Chengdu East -> Huanglongjiuzhai Station -> Jiuzhaigou core route | Jiuzhaigou entrance / Zhangzha Town | Yak beef soup pot, Tibetan-style food, Sichuan dishes · rail + station shuttle / private car |
| Day 6-7 | Jiuzhaigou -> Huanglong -> Chuanzhusi / Songpan -> Chengdu | Chuanzhusi / Songpan, then Chengdu | Warm soup pot, simple transfer meals · cableway/shuttle + return rail or car |
| Day 8-11 | Chengdu -> Mount Siguniang Town -> Shuangqiao, Changping and flexible valley day | Mount Siguniang Town / Rilong Town | Yak beef soup pot, noodles, town restaurants · mountain-road transfer + sightseeing buses |
| Day 12-14 | Return Chengdu -> buffer day / optional Dujiangyan or Qingcheng Mountain -> departure | Chengdu downtown, East Railway Station or airport area | Final Chengdu meal, snacks or hotel dining · recovery buffer, avoid distant sights on departure day |
