## Travel Tips

Keep these points in mind before you start the route.

| Payment Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry a small amount of RMB as backup. | Internet Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment. |
| --- | --- |
| Altitude Jiuzhaigou, Huanglong and Mount Siguniang are highland areas. Walk slowly, avoid alcohol and keep warm. | Long transfers This route includes rail transfers and mountain roads. Keep transfer days lighter and leave early. |
| Scenic tickets Use official channels, 12306, Trip.com, Ctrip, hotel desks or scenic counters. Reconfirm rules close to travel. | Weather Rain, snow or fog can affect viewpoints, roads and cableways. Keep Chengdu buffer days flexible. |
