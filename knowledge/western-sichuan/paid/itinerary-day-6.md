## Day 6 | Jiuzhaigou - Huanglong - Chuanzhusi / Songpan

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Visit Huanglong core travertine pools and overnight in Chuanzhusi/Songpan as a buffer. |
| Route | Jiuzhaigou -> Huanglong -> Chuanzhusi / Songpan |
| Overnight | Chuanzhusi or Songpan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,500 / USD 125-486 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:00-08:00 | Breakfast and departure to Huanglong | 1 hr | Bring warm clothing, sunscreen, raincoat, water and light food. |
| 08:00-10:30 | Jiuzhaigou to Huanglong | 2-2.5 hrs | Mountain transfer with rising altitude. Avoid alcohol and strenuous activity. |
| 10:30-11:30 | Cableway / shuttle upward | 30-60 min | Recommended: take cableway up to reduce high-altitude climbing. |
| 11:30-13:00 | Five-Color Pond | 1-1.5 hrs | Huanglong’s key site. Focus on layered pool colors and snow-mountain background. |
| 13:00-15:30 | Huanglong Temple, Zhengyan Pools, Golden Sand Pavement, Bonsai Pools downhill | 2-2.5 hrs | Walk downhill to see travertine terraces and pool layers. Shorten route if energy is low. |
| 15:30-17:00 | Leave Huanglong for Chuanzhusi / Songpan | 0.5-1.5 hrs | Do not travel too far tonight. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Hot dinner and rest | 1.5 hrs | Choose yak beef soup pot or Sichuan food and sleep early. |

Scenic Focus: Five-Color Pond is the target point of Huanglong. Golden Sand Pavement and Zhengyan Pools show the gradual change of the travertine landform.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If altitude discomfort appears, use the cableway and see only the Five-Color Pond highlights. If symptoms are strong, skip Huanglong and rest in Chuanzhusi/Songpan.

Dining Suggestions: Lunch: simple meal or packed food. Dinner: yak beef soup pot / Sichuan food in Chuanzhusi or Songpan.

Accommodation Suggestions: Choose Chuanzhusi or Songpan hotels with heating, hot water and oxygen support if possible.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Huanglong is higher than Jiuzhaigou. Walk slowly, avoid running, and do not drink alcohol.
