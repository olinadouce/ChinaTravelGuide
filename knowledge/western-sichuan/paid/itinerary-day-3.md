## Day 3 | Chengdu East - Huanglongjiuzhai Station - Jiuzhaigou Entrance

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Enter northwest Sichuan by rail and rest near Jiuzhaigou. |
| Route | Chengdu East -> Huanglongjiuzhai Station -> Jiuzhaigou entrance / Zhangzha Town |
| Overnight | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,500 / USD 125-486 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:00-08:00 | Go to Chengdu East Railway Station | 40-60 min | Use taxi or direct metro. Carry original passport and use staffed/passport gate if needed. |
| 08:00-11:00 | Chengdu East to Huanglongjiuzhai Station | Approx. 1.5-2.5 hrs | Train time depends on the actual 12306 timetable. Rest on the train and avoid overexertion. |
| 11:00-13:30 | Huanglongjiuzhai Station to Jiuzhaigou entrance | Approx. 2 hrs | After arrival, use official shuttle, shared car or pre-booked private car. Confirm destination as Jiuzhaigou entrance / Zhangzha Town hotel. |
| 13:30-15:00 | Hotel check-in and lunch | 1.5 hrs | Check in first, then have a simple lunch. Do not enter the park today. |
| 15:00-17:30 | Entrance-area adaptation and ticket check | 1-2.5 hrs | Confirm Day 4 ticket, sightseeing bus, entry time and walking/transfer time to the visitor center. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Early dinner and rest | 1.5 hrs | Choose Tibetan yak soup pot, Sichuan food or hotel restaurant. Sleep early. |

Scenic Focus: Today’s goal is not sightseeing but a safe transition from Chengdu to Jiuzhaigou. The altitude and temperature are different from Chengdu, so leave time to adapt.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If rail tickets are sold out, consider flying to Jiuzhai Huanglong Airport and using private transfer to the entrance. If arrival is late, cancel the entrance-area walk and only have dinner and rest.

Dining Suggestions: Jiuzhaigou entrance: Abuluzi-style Tibetan food, yak beef soup pot, barbecue or hotel restaurant. Budget: RMB 70-180 / USD 10-25.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay near Jiuzhaigou Visitor Center / entrance / Zhangzha Town. The closer to the visitor center, the easier two-day park entry will be.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Jiuzhaigou requires real-name reservation and original valid identity document for entry. Do not enter the park immediately after arrival. Prepare a jacket for cold evenings.
