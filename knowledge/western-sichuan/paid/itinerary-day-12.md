## Day 12 | Mount Siguniang Town - Chengdu

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Return to Chengdu during the day as a recovery and city buffer. |
| Route | Mount Siguniang Town -> Chengdu |
| Overnight | Chengdu |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,800 / USD 97-389 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout | 1 hr | Confirm luggage, driver and return route. |
| 09:00-14:30 | Mount Siguniang Town to Chengdu | 5-5.5 hrs | Private car is the most stable option. Short stops are fine, but do not add a major scenic area. |
| 14:30-16:00 | Chengdu hotel check-in and rest | 1-1.5 hrs | Rest after returning from high altitude. |
| 16:00-18:00 | Laundry / supplies / free time | 1-2 hrs | Handle clothes, photos and travel supplies. |
| 18:30-20:30 | Chengdu dinner | 1.5-2 hrs | Choose a comfortable Sichuan meal or hotpot, but avoid excessive alcohol. |

Scenic Focus: The main value is safety and recovery. Daylight return reduces mountain-road risk and leaves time to recover in Chengdu.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If the road is affected by snow or heavy rain, leave earlier or postpone the return. Do not drive late at night on mountain roads.

Dining Suggestions: Dinner: Mawangzi, Taode Clay Pot, hotpot or hotel/mall restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay in Chengdu. Choose Chunxi Road/Taikoo Li for dinner convenience or airport/station area for departure logistics.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Keep this day simple. Do not add Dujiangyan or Qingcheng Mountain unless the itinerary is running very smoothly.
