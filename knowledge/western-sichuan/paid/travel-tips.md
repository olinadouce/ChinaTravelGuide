## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For and Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Regular hotels/guesthouses + public transport/coaches + simple meals | RMB 11,000-16,000 | USD 1,528-2,222 | Young travelers or visitors with China independent-travel experience; more transfers and lower comfort. |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Comfort hotels + private cars for key segments + local restaurants | RMB 20,000-30,000 | USD 2,778-4,167 | Best for most international visitors; balances safety, comfort and execution difficulty. |
| High-End | High-end hotels/resorts + mostly private cars + selected restaurants | RMB 38,000-65,000 | USD 5,278-9,028 | Families, couples and higher-budget visitors; reduces language and transfer pressure. |

| Category | Budget | Comfort | High-End |

| Tickets / Sightseeing Buses | RMB 2,000-3,000 / USD 278-417 | RMB 2,500-3,500 / USD 347-486 | RMB 3,500-5,000 / USD 486-694 |
| Dining | RMB 1,500-3,000 / USD 208-417 | RMB 3,500-6,000 / USD 486-833 | RMB 7,000-12,000 / USD 972-1,667 |
| Backup Cash / Miscellaneous | RMB 500-1,500 / USD 69-208 | RMB 1,000-2,500 / USD 139-347 | RMB 2,000-5,000 / USD 278-694 |

| Budget note: These are per-person reference budgets, excluding international flights, shopping and travel insurance. If two travelers share hotels and private cars, the per-person accommodation and transport cost may decrease. During Chinese public holidays, autumn peak season or high-end hotel sell-out periods, accommodation and private-car prices may rise significantly. |
