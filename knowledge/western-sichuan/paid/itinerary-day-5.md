## Day 5 | Second Day in Jiuzhaigou / Low-Intensity Photography Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Second entry into Jiuzhaigou. Add Shuzheng Valley, repeat Five-Flower Lake, and adjust by energy. |
| Route | Jiuzhaigou entrance -> Shuzheng Valley / Rize repeat -> entrance |
| Overnight | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,300 / USD 125-458 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and second entry | 1 hr | The second day can be slower than Day 4. |
| 08:30-10:30 | Shuzheng Valley highlights | 1.5-2 hrs | Shuzheng Lakes, Wolong Lake, Sparkling Lake and Reed Lake are good for slower water and boardwalk photos. |
| 10:30-12:00 | Mirror Lake / Five-Flower Lake repeat | 1-1.5 hrs | If the weather is good, revisit reflection points and wait for better light. |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch / rest | 1 hr | Self-prepared light food saves time. |
| 13:00-15:30 | Flexible add-on according to energy | 2-2.5 hrs | Add Panda Lake, Nuorilang or Long Lake if energy is good. Do not force every site. |
| 15:30-17:00 | Exit early and rest | 1-1.5 hrs | Save energy for Huanglong high-altitude walking on Day 6. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Dinner and luggage preparation | 1.5 hrs | Confirm Day 6 private car/shared car departure time. |

Scenic Focus: The value of the second day is slower pacing. Shuzheng Valley’s connected lakes and boardwalks are better for relaxed viewing than the first core day.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If tired, do only a half-day entry and leave after lunch. If weather is poor, prioritize closer Shuzheng Valley sites instead of long transfers inside the park.

Dining Suggestions: Lunch: self-prepared food or simple park meal. Dinner: Tibetan soup pot or hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay near Jiuzhaigou entrance again. Do not change hotels before a transfer day.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Keep walking intensity moderate. Prepare warm clothing and rain gear for Huanglong.
