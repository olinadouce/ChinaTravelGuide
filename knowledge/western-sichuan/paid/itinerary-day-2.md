## Day 2 | Chengdu Buffer and Light Nature Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Panda Base and city supplies before entering higher-altitude areas. |
| Route | Chengdu city |
| Overnight | Chengdu |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,800 / USD 90-389 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and departure | 1 hr | Pandas are usually more active in the morning, so leave early. |
| 08:30-11:30 | Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding | 2.5-3 hrs | Focus on adult panda activity areas, young panda areas and bamboo paths. Avoid arriving around noon when pandas may rest. |
| 11:30-13:00 | Return downtown for lunch | 1-1.5 hrs | Choose Chengdu Chike, Taode Clay Pot or a mall restaurant. |
| 14:00-16:00 | People’s Park / Wangjianglou / hotel rest | 1-2 hrs | Use this as a light city buffer. Do not walk too much. |
| 16:00-18:00 | Supplies and transport confirmation | 1-2 hrs | Buy sunscreen, warm layer, non-slip shoes and motion-sickness medicine. Confirm Day 3 rail ticket, passport and station transfer. |
| 18:30-20:00 | Formal Sichuan dinner | 1.5 hrs | Choose Mawangzi, Chen Mapo Tofu or hotpot. Do not drink alcohol before high-altitude travel. |

Scenic Focus: The Panda Base is the best light natural opening for Chengdu. It is recognizable, easy to execute and not too physically demanding.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If you do not want to visit the Panda Base, replace it with Chengdu Museum, People’s Park or Wangjianglou Park. Families with children can do only half a day and then return to the hotel.

Dining Suggestions: Lunch: Chengdu Chike or Taode Clay Pot. Dinner: Chen Mapo Tofu, Mawangzi or hotpot. Budget: RMB 80-220 / USD 11-31.

Accommodation Suggestions: Continue staying in Chengdu. Prioritize a hotel convenient for Chengdu East Railway Station if taking an early train next day.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Do not drink alcohol before high-altitude travel. Prepare warm clothes, rain gear and sunscreen. Make sure the passport number on the 12306/Trip.com rail order matches the actual passport.
