14-Day Western Sichuan Nature Route

Sichuan Jiuzhaigou - Huanglong - Mount Siguniang

This handbook is written directly for international visitors. It is not a general sightseeing introduction, but an actionable 14-day nature route guide. Visitors can use it to choose accommodation areas, plan daily sightseeing, connect transport segments, select meals, estimate budgets and handle backup plans. All prices, ratings and schedules are reference information only. Final costs should be checked again according to travel dates on official platforms, 12306, Trip.com, Ctrip, Dianping, hotel front desks or scenic-area ticket counters.

| Basic Information | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Route Theme | Highland lakes, colorful travertine pools, snow-mountain valleys, alpine forests, panda habitat and light-hiking nature route in Sichuan. |
| Recommended Duration | 14 days / 13 nights |
| Route Entry / Exit | Enter from Chengdu; leave from Chengdu |
| Suitable For | First-time international visitors to China, nature photographers, families, couples and light-hiking travelers. |
| Best Seasons | April-June and September-November. Mid-to-late October is especially good for Jiuzhaigou autumn colors. Summer has stronger water flow but more rain. Winter is quieter but road and weather uncertainty is higher. |
| Difficulty | Medium to high. Jiuzhaigou and Huanglong are high-altitude scenic areas. Mount Siguniang Town, Shuangqiao Valley, Changping Valley and Haizi Valley are also at high elevation, so walking intensity should be controlled. |
| Exchange Rate Used | USD 1 approximately RMB 7.2. USD amounts are rounded and for budgeting only. |
