## Day 11 | Haizi Valley / Maobiliang / Rest Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose snow-mountain viewpoints, short hiking or full rest according to energy. |
| Route | Around Mount Siguniang Town |
| Overnight | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 450-2,000 / USD 63-278 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:30-09:30 | Breakfast and energy check | 1 hr | This day is flexible. Decide based on fatigue after Days 9-10. |
| 09:30-12:00 | Option A: Maobiliang + easy town photos | 2-2.5 hrs | Best for ordinary visitors. See distant views and the line of the four peaks. |
| 09:00-14:00 | Option B: Short Haizi Valley route | 3-5 hrs | For photographers and stronger walkers. Walk toward Guozhuangping / Chaoshanping. Do not push deep toward Dahaizi unless prepared. |
| Morning/afternoon | Option C: Full rest | Half day or full day | Sleep in, café, laundry and photo sorting. Good preparation for return to Chengdu. |
| 16:00-18:00 | Confirm Day 12 transport | 30-60 min | Confirm driver/coach, departure time, weather and road conditions. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Final dinner in the mountain town | 1.5 hrs | Choose warm soup pot or easy-to-digest food. |

Scenic Focus: Haizi Valley has more open snow-mountain views but requires stronger walking ability. For most visitors, Maobiliang plus rest is safer and still scenic.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If tired, choose a complete rest day. If weather is bad, do not enter Haizi Valley; stay in town and prepare for return to Chengdu.

Dining Suggestions: Town cafés, hotel restaurants, yak beef soup pot or simple noodles. Budget: RMB 50-160 / USD 7-22.

Accommodation Suggestions: Continue staying in the same town hotel/guesthouse.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Do not underestimate Haizi Valley. It is not the default route for ordinary visitors.
