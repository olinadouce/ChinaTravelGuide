## Route Logic and City / Scenic Area Introduction

This route focuses on the natural landscapes of the Sichuan plateau. It connects Jiuzhaigou’s colorful lakes, Huanglong’s high-altitude travertine pools, Mount Siguniang’s snow-mountain valleys, and Chengdu as the transport and recovery base. The route is not designed around shopping or nightlife. It emphasizes scenery, light hiking, photography, and safe transfers between highland areas.

| Place | City / Scenic Area Introduction | Accommodation and Travel Advice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chengdu | The provincial capital of Sichuan and the route’s entry/exit city. It works as an arrival buffer, transport hub, dining base and final recovery stop. | Stay near Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li, Tianfu Square, Chengdu East Railway Station or the airport. Choose Chengdu East if taking an early train; choose Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li for dining convenience. |
| Jiuzhaigou | A UNESCO World Heritage landscape famous for blue-green lakes, travertine waterfalls, autumn forests and snow-mountain reflections. The scenic area has a Y-shaped layout: Rize Valley, Zechawa Valley and Shuzheng Valley. | Stay near the entrance / Zhangzha Town. Two days inside the park are more comfortable than one day, especially for photographers and international visitors. |
| Huanglong | Known for high-altitude travertine pools, Five-Color Pond, Golden Sand Pavement, snow-mountain views and forests. It is higher and more physically demanding than Jiuzhaigou. | Visit during the day from Jiuzhaigou or Chuanzhusi / Songpan. Use the cableway and shuttle combination if energy is limited. |
| Chuanzhusi / Songpan | A key transport and overnight node between Jiuzhaigou, Huanglong and Huanglongjiuzhai Railway Station. Songpan Ancient Town can be used as a short stop, while Chuanzhusi is more practical as a transport base. | Use this area as a one-night altitude buffer after Huanglong. Choose hotels with heating, hot water and oxygen support if available. |
| Mount Siguniang | Part of the Sichuan Giant Panda Sanctuaries World Heritage context. The main tourist valleys are Shuangqiao Valley, Changping Valley and Haizi Valley. | Stay in Mount Siguniang Town / Rilong Town. Shuangqiao Valley is best for most visitors; Changping Valley is for light hiking; Haizi Valley is better for photography and stronger walkers. |
| Wolong / Dujiangyan Corridor | The overland corridor between Chengdu and Mount Siguniang, passing Dujiangyan, Yingxiu, Wolong and Balang Mountain areas. | Use mainly as a transfer route. Unless time is generous, do not add too many stops on long transfer days. |
