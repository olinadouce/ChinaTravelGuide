## Day 7 | Chuanzhusi / Songpan - Chengdu

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Return from the highland area to Chengdu and recover. |
| Route | Chuanzhusi / Songpan -> Huanglongjiuzhai Station / Songpan Station -> Chengdu East |
| Overnight | Chengdu |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,800 / USD 97-389 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and checkout | 1 hr | Depart according to train/flight timing; do not cut it too close. |
| 09:00-11:00 | Go to station / airport / transport node | 0.5-1.5 hrs | Confirm transfer time from hotel to Huanglongjiuzhai Station / Songpan Station. |
| 11:00-14:00 | Return to Chengdu | Approx. 1.5-3 hrs by rail / depends on transport | Use actual train/flight schedule. |
| 14:00-16:00 | Chengdu hotel check-in and rest | 1-2 hrs | After returning from high altitude, avoid intensive sightseeing. |
| 17:00-20:00 | Dinner, laundry and supplies | 2-3 hrs | Wash clothes, restock snacks and medicine, and prepare for Mount Siguniang. |

Scenic Focus: This is a recovery and logistics day. Returning to Chengdu reduces altitude pressure and gives the route a safe reset before Mount Siguniang.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If return tickets are unavailable, stay one more night in Songpan/Chuanzhusi and adjust the Chengdu buffer. Do not force a late-night mountain-road return.

Dining Suggestions: Dinner in Chengdu: Taode Clay Pot, Mawangzi, hotpot or hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay in Chengdu. If departing early for Mount Siguniang on Day 8, choose a hotel with convenient pickup.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Confirm Day 8 driver/vehicle and weather. Prepare motion-sickness medicine and warm clothing.
