## Day 13 | Chengdu Buffer / Optional Dujiangyan or Qingcheng Mountain

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Weather, transport and energy buffer day, with optional light nature sightseeing. |
| Route | Chengdu surroundings or Chengdu city |
| Overnight | Chengdu |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 500-2,500 / USD 69-347 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00-10:00 | Sleep in and breakfast | 1 hr | Do not start too early unless going to Dujiangyan / Qingcheng Mountain. |
| 10:00-16:00 | Option A: Dujiangyan easy visit | 4-6 hrs | See the irrigation system, riverside paths and Nanqiao area. Good for nature plus engineering. |
| 10:00-16:30 | Option B: Qingcheng Mountain front mountain | 4-6 hrs | Good for forest paths and Taoist mountain scenery. Stone steps can be slippery in rain. |
| All day | Option C: Chengdu rest | Half day or full day | People’s Park, Wangjianglou, malls, cafés, laundry and souvenirs. |
| 18:00-20:30 | Final dinner | 1.5-2 hrs | Choose Sichuan cuisine, hotpot or a stable hotel/mall restaurant. |

Scenic Focus: This day protects the whole route from weather, road delays and accumulated fatigue. It is intentionally flexible.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If tired or rainy, stay in Chengdu and rest. If the route was delayed earlier, use today to absorb the delay instead of adding sightseeing.

Dining Suggestions: Final Chengdu dinner: Chen Mapo Tofu, Chengdu Chike, Mawangzi, hotpot, or hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay in Chengdu. Choose based on next-day departure airport/station.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Do not schedule a faraway scenic area before departure day.
