## Accommodation Guide: Location, Foreign-Visitor Convenience and Reasons to Stay

| Destination | Recommended Stay Type | Location | Why Recommended | Foreign-Visitor Convenience | Reference Average Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chengdu | Luxury: The Temple House, Niccolo, St. Regis, InterContinental-style hotels | Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li / Tianfu Square | Close to dining, shopping and metro. Foreign-guest check-in is smoother. Good for first and final nights. | High | RMB 1,200-3,000 / USD 167-417 |
| Chengdu | Comfort: Atour / JI / Hampton-style hotels near Chengdu East | Chengdu East Railway Station area | Best for early train departure and less morning pressure. Confirm passport check-in before booking. | Medium-High | RMB 350-800 / USD 49-111 |
| Chengdu | Airport-area chain hotels | Shuangliu Airport / Tianfu Airport | Good for late arrivals or early departures, not ideal for city sightseeing. | Medium-High | RMB 300-800 / USD 42-111 |
| Jiuzhaigou | Luxury: Hilton Jiuzhaigou, Banyan Tree-style resorts | Zhangzha Town / resort area | Good for families and higher budgets; breakfast and car arrangements are usually more stable. Check exact distance to the park entrance. | High | RMB 900-2,500 / USD 125-347 |
| Jiuzhaigou | Comfort hotels near park entrance / Zhangzha Town | Near Jiuzhaigou Visitor Center | Best for two days inside the park; shorter morning transfer and more dinner choices. | Medium-High | RMB 350-1,000 / USD 49-139 |
| Jiuzhaigou | Budget inns / guesthouses | Zhangzha Town or outer entrance area | Lower cost, but confirm passport check-in, heating, breakfast and transport to the visitor center. | Confirm before booking | RMB 180-500 / USD 25-69 |
| Chuanzhusi / Songpan | Comfort / oxygen-support hotels | Chuanzhusi Town or Songpan Ancient Town | Good transition before or after Huanglong. Prioritize heating, oxygen, elevator and hot water. | Confirm before booking | RMB 250-800 / USD 35-111 |
| Mount Siguniang | View hotels / better guesthouses | Mount Siguniang Town / Rilong Town | Close to valley entrances. Good for Shuangqiao, Changping and rest days. Confirm heating and hot water. | Confirm before booking | RMB 300-1,200 / USD 42-167 |
| Mount Siguniang | Budget guesthouses | Town center / along main road | Budget option; confirm foreign passport registration, heating and car pickup point before payment. | Confirm before booking | RMB 150-450 / USD 21-63 |
| Chengdu final nights | Comfort or luxury city hotels | Chunxi Road, Tianfu Square, Chengdu East or airport | Use Chengdu as the recovery and departure buffer after highland travel. | High | RMB 350-3,000 / USD 49-417 |

| Accommodation note: International visitors usually need their original passport to check in at hotels in China. Chengdu, higher-star hotels and international brands usually handle foreign guests more smoothly. Small inns in Jiuzhaigou, Chuanzhusi, Songpan and Mount Siguniang should be contacted before payment to confirm passport check-in, heating, hot water, elevators and oxygen availability. |
