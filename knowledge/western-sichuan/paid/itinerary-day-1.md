## Day 1 | Arrival in Chengdu

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, hotel check-in, payment and network check. Do not schedule intensive sightseeing. |
| Route | Departure city -> Chengdu |
| Overnight | Chengdu |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 500-2,500 / USD 69-347 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| After arrival | Airport / railway station to hotel | 45-90 min | Use taxi or hotel pickup. First-time international visitors should not take complicated transfers with luggage. |
| 16:00-18:00 | Hotel check-in and rest | 1-2 hrs | Check passport, visa page, hotel booking, Alipay, WeChat Pay, international bank card, cash and mobile data. |
| 18:30-20:00 | First Chengdu dinner | 1-1.5 hrs | If staying near Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li, choose Chen Mapo Tofu, Taode Clay Pot or a mall restaurant. If not used to spicy food, choose hotel dining. |
| After 20:00 | Short walk or direct rest | 30-60 min | Stay close to the hotel. Do not schedule long-distance night transport. |

Scenic Focus: Chengdu is not the natural climax of the route. It is the transport and adaptation base. The most important tasks today are a smooth arrival, functioning mobile payment and enough rest.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If the flight is delayed or arrival is late at night, cancel all evening activities and go straight to the hotel. If mobile payment fails, use cash first or ask the hotel front desk for help.

Dining Suggestions: Dinner: Chen Mapo Tofu, Taode Clay Pot, Chengdu Chike, or hotel restaurant. Budget: RMB 70-180 / USD 10-25.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay near Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li, Tianfu Square, Chengdu East Railway Station or the airport. For an early train next day, choose Chengdu East; for food convenience, choose Chunxi Road / Taikoo Li.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Save the hotel Chinese name, address and phone number. Use official taxi or hotel pickup for late-night arrivals. Prepare rail tickets, scenic tickets and the original passport for later days.
