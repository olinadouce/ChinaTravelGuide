## Day 10 | Changping Valley

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Forest, streams, boardwalks and light hiking in a snow-mountain valley. |
| Route | Mount Siguniang Town -> Changping Valley -> Mount Siguniang Town |
| Overnight | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,500 / USD 90-347 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and go to Changping Valley Visitor Center | 1 hr | Today involves more walking. Wear non-slip shoes. |
| 09:00-09:30 | Sightseeing bus to Lama Temple area | Approx. 30 min | Ticket and sightseeing bus are counted separately. |
| 09:30-10:15 | Lama Temple area | 30-45 min | See the forested valley entrance and use it as the hiking start point. |
| 10:15-12:00 | Boardwalk toward Kushu Tan / lower valley | 1.5-2 hrs | Best light-hiking section for most visitors, with forest, streams and snow-mountain angles. |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch / rest | 1 hr | Bring light food and water. |
| 13:00-15:00 | Continue toward Muluozi direction or return | 2 hrs | Strong walkers may continue; ordinary visitors should turn back before energy drops. |
| 15:00-16:30 | Return to entrance and town | 1-1.5 hrs | Do not exit too late. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Dinner and rest | 1.5 hrs | Eat warm and easy-to-digest food. |

Scenic Focus: Changping Valley is more immersive than Shuangqiao. It is best for forest, stream and valley walking. It should be treated as a light hike, not a bus-tour day.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If energy is low, walk only the boardwalk near Lama Temple and Kushu Tan. If raining or snowy, reduce walking and return earlier.

Dining Suggestions: Bring packed lunch or snacks. Dinner: town soup pot / hotel meal.

Accommodation Suggestions: Continue staying in Mount Siguniang Town.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Do not attempt long hikes without preparation. Weather changes quickly; carry rain gear and a warm layer.
