## Day 8 | Chengdu - Mount Siguniang Town

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long mountain-road transfer and altitude adaptation in Mount Siguniang Town. |
| Route | Chengdu -> Dujiangyan / Yingxiu / Wolong -> Mount Siguniang Town |
| Overnight | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,200 / USD 125-444 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and departure | 1 hr | Private car is recommended. Driver picks up at the hotel. Bring jacket, motion-sickness medicine and snacks. |
| 09:00-12:00 | Chengdu toward Wolong | Approx. 3 hrs | Pass Dujiangyan, Yingxiu and Wolong direction. Short toilet/photo stops are acceptable. |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch on the road | 1 hr | Choose simple food; avoid very oily meals. |
| 13:00-15:30 | Wolong / Balang Mountain direction to Mount Siguniang Town | 2-2.5 hrs | Road has curves and weather changes quickly. |
| 15:30-17:00 | Hotel check-in and altitude adaptation | 1-1.5 hrs | Do not immediately do strenuous walking after arrival. |
| 17:00-18:30 | Maobiliang viewpoint if weather and energy allow | 30-60 min | Good distant view of Mount Siguniang. Cancel if tired. |
| 18:30-20:00 | Dinner and early rest | 1.5 hrs | Warm soup pot or Sichuan food is recommended. |

Scenic Focus: The transfer itself introduces western Sichuan mountain scenery. Maobiliang is the easiest optional viewpoint if weather is good.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If road conditions are poor or arrival is late, cancel Maobiliang and rest in town. If prone to carsickness, sit in front and avoid heavy lunch.

Dining Suggestions: Lunch: simple road meal. Dinner: yak beef soup pot or town restaurant, RMB 80-160 / USD 11-22.

Accommodation Suggestions: Stay in Mount Siguniang Town / Rilong Town. Confirm heating, hot water, oxygen support and passport check-in before booking.

Foreign Visitor Notes: Altitude is higher than Chengdu. Walk slowly after arrival and do not drink alcohol.
