## Day 9 | Shuangqiao Valley

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The most accessible Mount Siguniang valley, with sightseeing buses, snow mountains, lakes, meadows and redwood forest. |
| Route | Mount Siguniang Town -> Shuangqiao Valley -> Mount Siguniang Town |
| Overnight | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,400 / USD 90-333 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and go to Shuangqiao Valley Visitor Center | 1 hr | Bring passport, ticket order, warm clothing and sunscreen. |
| 09:00-10:00 | Take sightseeing bus to Redwood Forest | Approx. 1 hr | Recommended method: ride to the endpoint first, then visit stops while coming down. |
| 10:00-10:50 | Redwood Forest | 40-50 min | High-altitude forest, snow mountains and valley-end scenery. Very photogenic in clear weather. |
| 11:00-11:40 | Potala Peak / Hunter Peak / Eagle Beak Rock viewpoints | 30-40 min | See mountain shapes and glacial landforms. |
| 11:40-12:30 | Simple lunch / packed food | 40-60 min | Food options are limited. Bring water and energy food. |
| 12:30-13:30 | Sigu Nacuo | 45-60 min | Lake reflections, dead trees and snow mountains are among the valley’s best combinations. |
| 13:30-14:30 | Longzhucuo / Science Base | 45-60 min | Water color, travertine deposits and ecological boardwalks. |
| 14:30-16:00 | Nianyuba / meadow sections | 1-1.5 hrs | See open valley scenery and take easy walks. |
| 16:00-17:00 | Return to town | 1 hr | Rest after returning. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Dinner | 1.5 hrs | Warm soup or hotel restaurant. |

Scenic Focus: Shuangqiao Valley is the most suitable Mount Siguniang valley for ordinary visitors. The sightseeing bus reduces physical pressure while still giving snow-mountain, lake and meadow views.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If weather is poor, keep only Redwood Forest, Sigu Nacuo and one mid-valley viewpoint. If altitude discomfort appears, stay on the bus more and reduce walking.

Dining Suggestions: Bring lunch/snacks into the valley. Dinner in town: yak beef soup pot, noodles or hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Continue staying in Mount Siguniang Town. Avoid changing hotels during the valley days.

Foreign Visitor Notes: The valley is high-altitude. Keep warm and use sunscreen. Do not run after buses or walk fast.
