## Day 4 | Jiuzhaigou Core Route

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | First entry into Jiuzhaigou. Prioritize Rize Valley, Zechawa Valley and Nuorilang core sites. |
| Route | Jiuzhaigou Visitor Center -> Rize Valley -> Nuorilang -> Zechawa Valley -> Shuzheng highlights |
| Overnight | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-3,300 / USD 125-458 |

| Time | Plan | Suggested Stay | Execution Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:00-08:00 | Breakfast and go to visitor center | 1 hr | Bring passport, ticket order, sunscreen, raincoat and water. Enter early. |
| 08:00-09:00 | Enter and take sightseeing bus toward upper Rize Valley | Approx. 1 hr | Follow scenic-area dispatching and head to the core Rize Valley direction first. |
| 09:00-10:00 | Arrow Bamboo Lake / Panda Lake area | 40-60 min | See highland lakes, forest reflections and morning light. Shorten this stop if time is limited. |
| 10:00-11:00 | Five-Flower Lake | 45-60 min | One of Jiuzhaigou’s most important lakes, with layered water colors, autumn forests and lake-bottom patterns. |
| 11:00-12:00 | Pearl Shoal Waterfall | 45-60 min | Walk along the travertine shoal and waterfall. Slow down if the boardwalk is wet. |
| 12:00-13:00 | Nuorilang Center lunch / rest | 1 hr | Food choices are limited. Bring snacks if preferred. |
| 13:00-14:30 | Long Lake + Five-Color Pond | 1-1.5 hrs | Core Zechawa Valley sites. Five-Color Pond is small but color is concentrated and highly photogenic. |
| 14:30-16:30 | Nuorilang Waterfall + Shuzheng highlights | 1.5-2 hrs | Keep Nuorilang as a priority and add Shuzheng lakes according to energy. |
| 16:30-17:30 | Exit park and return to hotel | 1 hr | Avoid leaving too late during peak season. |
| 18:00-20:00 | Dinner and rest | 1.5 hrs | Hot soup or hotel dining is recommended after a long walking day. |

Scenic Focus: Five-Flower Lake, Pearl Shoal Waterfall, Long Lake, Five-Color Pond and Nuorilang Waterfall are the key sites to keep on the first day.

Alternative / Low-Energy Plan: If entry is late, prioritize Five-Flower Lake, Pearl Shoal, Five-Color Pond and Nuorilang. If raining, reduce boardwalk walking and use sightseeing buses more.

Dining Suggestions: Lunch at Nuorilang Center or bring light food. Dinner near the entrance: yak beef soup pot / Sichuan food / hotel restaurant.

Accommodation Suggestions: Continue staying near the entrance to avoid moving hotels before the second park day.

Foreign Visitor Notes: The scenic area is large and must be visited by sightseeing bus. Stay on boardwalks and enter early during autumn peak season.
