## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Chengdu | Arrival, rest, payment and network check | Chengdu |
| Day 2 | Chengdu buffer and light nature day | Panda Base / city supplies / rail preparation | Chengdu |
| Day 3 | Chengdu East - Huanglongjiuzhai Station - Jiuzhaigou | High-speed rail into northwest Sichuan and rest near the park entrance | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Day 4 | Jiuzhaigou core route | Rize Valley + Zechawa Valley + Nuorilang | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Day 5 | Second day in Jiuzhaigou | Shuzheng Valley, repeat Five-Flower Lake, photography or lower-intensity route | Jiuzhaigou entrance |
| Day 6 | Jiuzhaigou - Huanglong - Chuanzhusi / Songpan | Huanglong pools, Five-Color Pond and travertine landscape | Chuanzhusi / Songpan |
| Day 7 | Chuanzhusi / Songpan - Chengdu | Return to Chengdu to reduce altitude and transfer fatigue | Chengdu |
| Day 8 | Chengdu - Mount Siguniang Town | Long mountain-road transfer and altitude adaptation | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Day 9 | Shuangqiao Valley | Snow mountains, alpine lakes, meadows and redwood forest by sightseeing bus | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Day 10 | Changping Valley | Forest, streams, boardwalks and light hiking | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Day 11 | Haizi Valley / Maobiliang / Rest | Snow-mountain viewpoints, short hiking or recovery | Mount Siguniang Town |
| Day 12 | Mount Siguniang Town - Chengdu | Return to Chengdu and recover | Chengdu |
| Day 13 | Chengdu buffer / optional Dujiangyan or Qingcheng Mountain | Weather, transport and energy buffer day | Chengdu |
| Day 14 | Depart from Chengdu | Departure day, no distant scenic area | - |
