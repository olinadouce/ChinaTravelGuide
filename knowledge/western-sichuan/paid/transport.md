## Intercity Transport and Public Transport Usage

| Segment | Best Option | Time | Reference Price | Suitable For | Usage / Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chengdu airport -> city hotel | Metro / taxi / hotel pickup | 45-90 min | RMB 20-250 / USD 3-35 | All visitors | For late arrival, use taxi or pickup. Save the hotel Chinese name. |
| Chengdu hotel -> Chengdu East Railway Station | Metro / taxi | 20-60 min | RMB 5-80 / USD 1-11 | Rail passengers | Use Alipay/WeChat transit code or buy a metro ticket on site. |
| Chengdu East -> Huanglongjiuzhai Station | High-speed rail / EMU | Approx. 1.5-2.5 hrs | Approx. RMB 100-250 / USD 14-35 | Jiuzhaigou visitors | Buy via 12306/Trip.com with passport information. Carry the original passport and use staffed/passport channels if needed. |
| Huanglongjiuzhai Station -> Jiuzhaigou entrance | Official shuttle / shared car / private car | Approx. 2 hrs | RMB 60-600 / USD 8-83 | All visitors | Use official/regular shuttles after arrival. If booking private car, confirm destination as Jiuzhaigou entrance / Zhangzha Town. |
| Jiuzhaigou entrance -> visitor center | Walk / hotel shuttle / short transfer | 5-25 min | RMB 0-50 / USD 0-7 | Park visitors | Stay near the entrance if visiting twice. Do not schedule the morning too tightly. |
| Jiuzhaigou -> Huanglong | Private car / shared car | 2-3 hrs | RMB 100-800 / USD 14-111 | Day 6 visitors | Altitude rises during this segment. Avoid alcohol and strenuous activity. |
| Huanglong -> Chuanzhusi / Songpan | Private car / shared car | 0.5-1.5 hrs | RMB 80-300 / USD 11-42 | After Huanglong | Do not rush a night return to Chengdu after Huanglong. |
| Chuanzhusi / Songpan -> Chengdu | Rail / flight / private transfer | 1.5-8 hrs | RMB 100-1,200 / USD 14-167 | Return to Chengdu | Rail depends on station and timetable. Private car is longer and more tiring. |
| Chengdu -> Mount Siguniang Town | Private car / shared car / bus | 4.5-6 hrs | RMB 150-1,200 / USD 21-167 | All visitors | Private car is safest and clearest for international travelers with luggage. |
| Mount Siguniang Town -> Shuangqiao Valley | Taxi / hotel car / scenic transport | 15-30 min | RMB 20-100 / USD 3-14 | Day 9 visitors | The valley uses sightseeing buses internally. |
| Mount Siguniang Town -> Changping Valley | Taxi / hotel car / short transfer | 10-25 min | RMB 20-80 / USD 3-11 | Day 10 visitors | Prepare for more walking than Shuangqiao. |
| Mount Siguniang Town -> Chengdu | Private car / bus | 4.5-6 hrs | RMB 150-1,200 / USD 21-167 | All visitors | Return in daylight to reduce mountain-road weather risk. |

| Public transport usage for international visitors: For high-speed rail, use 12306, Trip.com or station ticket counters. The passport used for booking must match the passport used for travel. If an automatic gate does not read a passport, use the staffed gate. For Chengdu metro, use Alipay/WeChat transit codes or buy single-ride tickets. For scenic shuttles and coaches, keep the Chinese boarding point, driver phone number, vehicle plate and destination name. For long mountain-road segments, confirm the driver, vehicle, price, tolls, parking fees and hotel pickup/drop-off one day in advance. Avoid unlicensed tout vehicles. |
