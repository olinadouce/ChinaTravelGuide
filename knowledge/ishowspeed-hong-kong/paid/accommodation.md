### Tips for Foreign Guests

- Book with the exact English name shown on the passport.

- Carry the original passport at check-in.

- Confirm that the hotel accepts foreign guests and supports the intended payment method.

- Reconfirm room type, window, breakfast, taxes, cancellation policy and final price.
