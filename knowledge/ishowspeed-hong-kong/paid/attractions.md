## Attraction & Booking Notes

| Stop / Experience | Admission | Suggested Time | Key Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Villain Hitting under the Canal Road Flyover | No admission ticket; practitioner fees vary | 30–40 min | This is a public street-based folk practice with no fixed official opening hours or standard ticket price. Practitioners may not always be present. Confirm the price before participating and ask for permission before filming. |
| Ocean Park Hong Kong | Adult: HK$538; Child aged 3–11: HK$269 | 4–6 h | Current park calendars commonly list 10:00–18:30 on operating days. Opening days, animal viewing and attraction schedules vary. Purchase tickets through the official website and check the calendar for the selected date. |
| Victoria Peak, Peak Tram & Sky Terrace 428 | Peak public area: Free; Peak Tram adult: HK$116 return / HK$82 single; Sky Terrace 428: HK$80; Tram + Sky Terrace adult combo: HK$182 return / HK$146 single | 1.5–2 h | Peak Tram: 07:30–23:00 daily. Sky Terrace 428: 10:00–22:00 Monday–Friday; 08:00–22:00 Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays. Advance booking is recommended for sunset and evening visits. |
| Man Mo Temple | Free admission | 45–60 min | Open 08:00–18:00 daily. Opens 07:00–18:00 on the 1st and 15th days of each lunar month and on selected Chinese festivals. Religious ceremonies may affect visitor access. |
| Hong Kong Tramways | Adult flat fare: HK$3.30 per ride | 45–60 min | Regular tram services operate from approximately 05:00 until around midnight, but first and last departures vary by route and day. Pay when leaving through the front exit using Octopus, cash, supported bank cards or mobile payment. |
| Kam Shan Country Park | Free | 2–3 h | This is an open country park without a ticket gate or published daily opening and closing time. Visit during daylight, ideally around 07:00–18:00. Do not feed, touch, chase or approach macaques, and keep all food out of sight. |
| Temple Street Night Market | Free public market | 1–2 h | The street is publicly accessible throughout the day, but the night market generally becomes active after 19:00. The recommended visiting period is approximately 18:00–22:00. Stall numbers and opening times vary according to weather and individual vendors. |
| Avenue of Stars | Free | 45–60 min | Open-air public promenade with no admission gate or fixed visitor session. Temporary closures or crowd controls may apply during major events. “A Symphony of Lights” is normally staged at 20:00 each evening, subject to weather warnings and operational changes. |
| Victoria Harbour Cruise — Star Ferry Water Tour | Dazzling Night Tour: HK$280 adult / HK$200 concessionary fare | 45 min | The night tour is scheduled for 19:45–20:30 under the timetable effective from 1 February 2026. The Tsim Sha Tsui ticket counter opens 11:30–19:45 and stops selling tickets 10 minutes before departure. Online advance purchase is recommended. |

| Timing Rule Ticket prices, opening hours, reservation rules and showroom availability can change. Reconfirm all operational details shortly before travel. |
