## Practical Information

| Topic | What to Do |
| --- | --- |
| Payment | Use Octopus, widely accepted cards and supported mobile payments; carry small cash for some markets. |
| Internet | Use an eSIM or roaming. |
| Navigation | Use MTR / bus apps and save English plus Chinese place names. |
| Hotel check-in | Carry the original passport. |
| Wildlife | Never feed macaques or expose food at Kam Shan. |
| Crowds | Avoid following or surrounding public figures and keep station access clear. |
| Cross-border travel | Check immigration documents and allow extra time. |
