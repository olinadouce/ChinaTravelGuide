## Day 2 · Continue the Route and Depart

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:00–11:00 | Chengdu Panda Base extension | Enter early, follow the official route and use the passport lane. | This is a route extension, tickets needed to be purchased in advance. This spot is a little bit far from the central area which may take around 45minutes’ drive. |
| 11:30–13:00 | Lunch | Try mapo tofu, kung pao chicken or a casual noodle meal. | Keep spice levels manageable before travel. |
| 13:00–15:30 | Chunxi Road / IFS / Taikoo Li | Modern shopping district, panda installation and city-walk photos. | Instagrammable spots in Chengdu and can give you a good experience about modern Sichuan lifestyle. These spots are closed to each other and will take within 20 minutes’ walk. |
| 16:00–17:00 | Optional cultural stop | A. Wenshu Monastery · B. Jinli. | Added route extension; choose one only. These spots are closed to each other and will take within 30 minutes’ drive. |
| After 17:00 | Departure | Travel to the airport or railway station. | Allow extra time during rush hour. |

### Choose One Optional Extension

| Option | What You’ll See | Best For | Planning Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wenshu Monastery | Temple complex and vegetarian food | Quiet cultural stop | Dress respectfully. Admission is free, but if you need a guided tour, it costs approximately 499 yuan for 1.5 hours (small groups of up to 10 people). Please purchase guided tour services through official channels, such as scenic spot service desks and mini-programs of travel websites. Opening Hours: 8:00 - 17:00 (Last admission at 16:00) |
| Jinli Ancient Street | Food, crafts and evening lights | First-time visitors | Can be crowded and commercial. |
