## Attraction & Booking Notes

| Stop / Experience | Admission | Suggested Time | Key Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kuanzhai Alley | Free public area | 2–3 h | Commercial experiences and show tickets cost extra. |
| People’s Park tea house | Pay for tea / services | 1.5–2 h | Ask before photographing performers or local guests. |
| Sichuan Opera show | Approx. CNY 100–300 | 1–1.5 h | Venue, seat and included services affect price. |
