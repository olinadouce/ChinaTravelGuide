## Day 1 · Imperial Icons

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:30–09:00 | Tiananmen exterior | Security check and exterior photo stop before entering the Palace Museum. You can also experience riding a bicycle along Chang'an Avenue and passing by Tiananmen Square | Carry your passport; crowd controls can change. If you want to watch the national flag-raising ceremony, you need to arrive at Tiananmen Square at 4:00 a.m. to queue up. |
| 09:00–12:30 | Forbidden City / Palace Museum | Central Axis route: Meridian Gate → Hall of Supreme Harmony → Palace of Heavenly Purity → Imperial Garden. | Official site lists 08:30–17:00 and CNY 60 in peak season; book in advance. |
| 12:30–14:00 | Lunch | Choose zhajiangmian, dumplings or instant-boiled mutton. | Avoid overloading the schedule after a long palace walk. |
| 14:00–16:00 | Jingshan or hutong extension | A. Jingshan panorama · B. Nanluoguxiang / Drum Tower area. | These are Beijing-featured extensions |
| 16:30–18:00 | Wangfujing | Try tanghulu, old Beijing Baodu; curious travelers may sample douzhi in a small portion. | It is recommended to visit during the daytime. |
| 18:30–20:00 | Peking duck dinner | Share duck, pancakes, cucumber, scallion and sauce. | Reserve at popular restaurants. |
