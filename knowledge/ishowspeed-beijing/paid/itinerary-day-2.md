## Day 2 · Continue the Route and Depart

| Time | Attraction / Experience | Suggested Plan | Practical Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 07:30–09:00 | Transfer to Mutianyu | Private transfer, tourist bus or booked day-tour pickup. | Mutianyu is used as a practical visitor option. |
| 09:00–13:00 | Mutianyu Great Wall | Cable car or walking route; choose a moderate section and allow photo stops. | No flips, climbing on walls or unsafe imitation. Wear non-slip and comfortable shoes. |
| 13:00-15:30 | Return to Central Beijing and Rest | Private transfer, tourist bus or booked day-tour pickup. Try Chinese massage. | Search for traditional Chinese massage parlors on Dianping to experience traditional Chinese massage |
| 15:30-17:00 | Optional Extension | A. Temple of Heaven B. Summer Palace C. 798 Art District D. Shichahai | Choose only one option based on your interests, location and departure time. Check opening hours, ticket requirements and last admission in advance. The Temple of Heaven and Summer Palace normally require more time, while 798 Art District is more suitable for a flexible 1–1.5-hour visit. |
| 17:00–18:30 | Early Dinner | Return from Mutianyu, check in or collect luggage, and have an early dinner around Qianmen before the evening performance. | Traffic from Mutianyu may vary significantly. Leave sufficient buffer time and avoid booking a tightly timed afternoon show. |
| 18:30–21:20 | Traditional Chinese Variety Show at Lao She Teahouse | Arrive approximately 30 minutes early. Watch a Beijing-style variety performance featuring Peking Opera excerpts, Sichuan face-changing, Chinese kung fu, tea art, folk music and acrobatics. | The main show normally runs around 19:50–21:20. Lion-dance performances are not guaranteed and may appear only in selected or seasonal programmes. Check the programme and ticket availability before booking. Price：RMB 180-580 pp. |
| Next Morning | High-speed rail to next city | Travel to the correct station, pass security and board with the original passport. | Arrive 60 minutes early. |
