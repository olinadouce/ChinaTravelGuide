## Travel Tips

Keep these points in mind before you start the route.

| Payment Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry a small amount of RMB as backup. | Internet Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment. |
| --- | --- |
| Crowds The Palace Museum, Tiananmen Square and Wangfujing are busiest all day. Add buffer time for walking and photos. | Temporary Road Closure Temporary road closures may occur for important conferences and events. It is recommended that you confirm your route in advance or take the subway when traveling. |
| Tickets Use official attraction channels, WeChat mini programs, Trip.com or on-site service desks. Reconfirm opening hours before travel. | Hotel check-in Book with the exact passport name and carry the original passport. |
