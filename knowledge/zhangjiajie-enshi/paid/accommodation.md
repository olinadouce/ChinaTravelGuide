## Accommodation Guide: Location, Foreign-Visitor Convenience and Reasons to Stay

| Destination | Recommended Stay | Location | Why Recommended | Foreign-Visitor Convenience | Reference Average Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Zhangjiajie City | Pullman / Hampton / Atour / chain hotels near Tianmen Cableway | Tianmen cableway station, Dayong Ancient Town or city center | Best for late arrival, Tianmen Mountain day and transfer logistics. Taxis and restaurants are convenient. Chain/international hotels usually handle foreign-passport check-in more smoothly. | High / confirm for non-chain hotels | RMB 350-1,200 / USD 49-167 |
| Wulingyuan | Pullman Zhangjiajie / Neodalle / Xibu Street hotels | Wulingyuan Landmark Gate, Xibu Street or East Gate area | Most convenient for consecutive Forest Park days. Early entry is easier and evening restaurants are concentrated. | High for major hotels; confirm guesthouses | RMB 350-1,500 / USD 49-208 |
| Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon area | Cili / Grand Canyon area hotels or stay in Wulingyuan | Grand Canyon visitor center area or Wulingyuan day trip base | For a half-day visit, no hotel change is needed. If combining Grand Canyon, Huanglong Cave and Baofeng Lake, Wulingyuan remains the more stable base. | Medium; confirm before booking | RMB 250-900 / USD 35-125 |
| Enshi City | Movenpick / Ramada / Vienna / Atour / railway station hotels | Enshi Railway Station, Daughter City or city center | Best hub for Enshi Grand Canyon, Qingjiang River and Pingshan. More restaurant and car resources. | High for chain hotels | RMB 300-1,000 / USD 42-139 |
| Enshi Grand Canyon | Guesthouses / hotels near canyon visitor center | Visitor center / Mufu Town area | Good for early entry and less morning driving, but restaurants are limited and evenings are quiet. | Medium; must confirm passport check-in | RMB 250-900 / USD 35-125 |
| Hefeng / Pingshan | Pingshan Canyon county hotels / guesthouses | Hefeng county or near Pingshan Canyon | A same-day round trip from Enshi is tiring. Staying closer improves the chance of completing the canyon smoothly. Confirm foreign-passport registration. | Confirm before booking | RMB 250-800 / USD 35-111 |
| Lichuan | City chain hotels near the railway station | Lichuan Railway Station or city center | If adding Tenglong Cave, staying in Lichuan is easier than a same-day round trip from Enshi. | Medium-high for chain hotels | RMB 250-700 / USD 35-97 |

| Accommodation note: International visitors usually need the original passport for hotel check-in in China. Major hotels in Zhangjiajie City, Wulingyuan and Enshi city are usually more reliable. Guesthouses around Pingshan, Enshi Grand Canyon and small towns should be contacted before payment through Trip.com, Ctrip customer service or the hotel phone number to confirm foreign-passport registration. |
