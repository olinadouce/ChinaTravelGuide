## Day 6 | Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon / Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose a thrilling canyon, a rain-proof cave or a relaxed lake according to weather and fear of heights. |
| Route | Zhangjiajie City / Wulingyuan -> Grand Canyon / Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake |
| Overnight | Zhangjiajie City or Wulingyuan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,500 / USD 97-347 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and decide route based on weather and ticket availability.

Option A 09:30-14:30 | Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon + Glass Bridge. Best for visitors who want a high-adrenaline glass-bridge experience and canyon walk.

Option B 09:30-12:30 | Huanglong Cave. Best in rain or when visibility is poor. Includes cave chambers, underground river sections and lighting.

Option C 09:30-12:30 | Baofeng Lake. Best for families, older visitors and low-energy days. Boat trip plus easy lake scenery.

15:00-17:00 | Return, rest and prepare for Zhangjiajie-Enshi transfer on Day 7.

18:30-20:00 | Final Zhangjiajie dinner; choose a specific restaurant rather than random tourist food.

Transport and distance notes: Wulingyuan to Grand Canyon is about 40-70 minutes by car, RMB 150-300 / USD 21-42. Huanglong Cave and Baofeng Lake are closer to Wulingyuan and easier as weather backups.

Dining suggestions: Dinner: Suoxi Shanzhai, Wulong Shanzhai, Hu Shifu or Fu Zhengyi depending on where you sleep.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay where Day 7 transport is easiest. If taking train from Zhangjiajie West, stay in city or near the station.

International visitor notes: Grand Canyon / Glass Bridge may require time-slot tickets. Passport booking can be easier through Trip.com or hotel help.

Backup plan: If afraid of heights or rain is heavy, do not force Grand Canyon glass bridge. Use Huanglong Cave or Baofeng Lake.
