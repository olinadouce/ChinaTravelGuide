## Route Logic and City / Scenic Area Introduction

This route combines the vertical quartz-sandstone peak forests of Zhangjiajie with the karst gorges, river scenery and cliff landscapes of Enshi. Zhangjiajie is about vertical drama: stone pillars, cliff paths, cableways, elevators, glass bridges and high viewpoints. Enshi is about canyon depth: fissures, cliffs, Qingjiang River, underground rivers, limestone forests and mountain villages. Together, the two areas create strong visual variation and avoid repeating the same type of mountain scenery every day.

| Place | City / Scenic Area Introduction | Accommodation and Travel Advice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Zhangjiajie City | The location of Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport, Zhangjiajie West Railway Station and the Tianmen Mountain cableway station. It works well for arrival, Tianmen Mountain and long-distance transfers. Dining choices are broader than in Wulingyuan and taxis are easier at night. | If arriving late or visiting Tianmen Mountain the next day, stay near the Tianmen cableway station, Dayong Ancient Town or the city center. If entering Wulingyuan early the next morning, stay directly in Wulingyuan after arrival. |
| Wulingyuan / Zhangjiajie National Forest Park | The core World Heritage area, including Yuanjiajie, Tianzi Mountain, Yangjiajie, Golden Whip Stream and Ten-Mile Gallery. The area is too large for one day and should be split into 2-3 days. | Stay near Wulingyuan Landmark Gate / East Gate. This saves morning entry time and keeps restaurants and convenience stores close at night. |
| Tianmen Mountain | A high-altitude scenic day beside Zhangjiajie city, focused on the long cableway, Tongtian Avenue, glass skywalks, Guigu Plank Road, Tianmen Cave and the 999 steps. It feels different from the Wulingyuan peak forest. | Stay near the Tianmen cableway station the night before. In peak season, reserve the entry line and time slot in advance. |
| Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon | Known for the glass bridge, canyon walkways, waterfalls, streams and high-adrenaline activities. It works as a half-day or full-day experience after Wulingyuan. | You may continue staying in Wulingyuan for a day trip. If you are afraid of heights or it rains, use Huanglong Cave or Baofeng Lake instead. |
| Enshi City | The transport and dining hub of Enshi Prefecture. Enshi Grand Canyon, Qingjiang River, Suobuya Stone Forest and Pingshan Canyon all require transfers from the city or nearby towns. | Stay near Enshi Railway Station, Tusi City / Daughter City area or the city center for restaurants, taxis and car arrangements. |
| Enshi Grand Canyon | The main attractions are Qixingzhai cliff peaks and Yunlong Ground Fissure. Qixingzhai has wide views but heavy walking; Yunlong Ground Fissure is wetter and more canyon-like. | Can be visited as a day trip from Enshi city, or stay near the visitor center for early entry. Stay near the canyon if you want to reduce morning driving. |
| Pingshan Canyon / Hefeng | Famous for clear canyon water, floating-boat photos, narrow gorge walls and plank roads. Weather and water levels strongly affect the experience. | Use a private car or stay one night in Hefeng / near Pingshan. A same-day round trip from Enshi is tiring and requires advance confirmation of opening status and boat operation. |
