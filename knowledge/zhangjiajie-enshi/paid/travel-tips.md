## Three-Level Budget Estimate (14 Days, Excluding International Flights)

| Level | Configuration | Total RMB Budget | Total USD Budget | Suitable For and Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Budget | Regular hotels/guesthouses, some public transport, limited private cars, simple meals | RMB 10,500-15,500 | USD 1,458-2,153 | Suitable for travelers with independent China travel experience who can accept transfers and lower comfort. |
| Comfort (Recommended) | Comfort hotels + private cars for key segments + concrete local restaurants | RMB 17,500-28,000 | USD 2,431-3,889 | Best for most international visitors. Reduces communication pressure while keeping the full experience. |
| High-End | High-end / scenic hotels + mostly private cars + better restaurants | RMB 35,000-60,000 | USD 4,861-8,333 | Best for families, couples and higher budgets. Reduces physical and transport uncertainty. |
