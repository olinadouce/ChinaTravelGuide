## Day 7 | Zhangjiajie -> Enshi

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Long-distance transfer day; do not plan a major attraction. |
| Route | Zhangjiajie -> Enshi |
| Overnight | Enshi City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 700-2,500 / USD 97-347 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and check out. Confirm passport, train/car booking and hotel address in Enshi.

09:00-16:00 | Transfer to Enshi by train combination or private transfer. Build in buffer time.

16:00-18:00 | Check into Enshi hotel, rest and confirm Day 8 / Day 9 plans.

18:30-20:00 | Enshi first dinner: Zhangguan Hezha, Tujia home-style dishes, kang potatoes or local cured-pork dishes.

Transport and distance notes: Zhangjiajie to Enshi may require train connections and takes about 4.5-8 hours depending on schedule. Use 12306, Trip.com or manual counter. Carry the original passport used for ticket purchase.

Dining suggestions: Do not rely on scenic-area food on transfer day. Eat a proper dinner in Enshi city.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay near Enshi Railway Station, Daughter City or city center. This helps with restaurants and cars to the canyon.

International visitor notes: Avoid tight transfers. Station names and train times must be checked carefully.

Backup plan: If the transfer is delayed, cancel all evening sightseeing and only eat near the hotel.
