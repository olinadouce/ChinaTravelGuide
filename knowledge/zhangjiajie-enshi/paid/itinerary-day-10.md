## Day 10 | Enshi Grand Canyon 2: Yunlong Ground Fissure or Recovery

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Add the lower, wetter canyon section or use the day to recover. |
| Route | Yunlong Ground Fissure / Enshi City |
| Overnight | Enshi City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 500-2,000 / USD 69-278 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and energy check after Qixingzhai.

Option A 09:30-12:30 | Yunlong Ground Fissure. Walk through the lower canyon, waterfalls and wet stone paths.

Option B 10:00-13:00 | Enshi city rest: coffee, light food, laundry and supply refill.

14:00-16:00 | If energy is good, add a short local stop or easy walk; otherwise rest.

18:00-20:00 | Dinner in Enshi city and confirm Pingshan plan for Day 11.

Transport and distance notes: If visiting Yunlong Ground Fissure from Enshi city, expect another 1.5-2.5 hours each way unless staying near the canyon. Private car is recommended.

Dining suggestions: Choose lighter food after a heavy hiking day: hezha, rice dishes, noodles or soup.

Accommodation suggestions: Return to Enshi city tonight unless staying close to Pingshan/Hefeng for Day 11.

International visitor notes: Ground Fissure paths are wet and slippery. Wear non-slip shoes and avoid rushing.

Backup plan: In rain or fatigue, skip Yunlong Ground Fissure and rest. This is better than losing the Pingshan day.
