## Intercity Transport and Public Transport Use

| Segment | Best Option | Time | Reference Price | How to Use / Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Zhangjiajie Airport / West Station -> city hotel | Taxi / ride-hailing / hotel pickup | 20-45 min | RMB 40-120 / USD 6-17 | Save the hotel Chinese name. Alipay/WeChat ride-hailing works in the city; for late arrivals, use hotel pickup or official taxi. |
| Zhangjiajie Airport / West Station -> Wulingyuan | Taxi / private car | 50-75 min | RMB 120-250 / USD 17-35 | Recommended if you enter the Forest Park next morning. Do not accidentally book a city hotel if your plan starts in Wulingyuan. |
| Wulingyuan hotel -> Landmark Gate | Walk / taxi | 5-20 min | RMB 0-30 / USD 0-4 | Staying near Landmark Gate allows walking entry, which is important for consecutive park days. |
| Wulingyuan -> Tianmen cableway station | Private car / taxi | 50-70 min | RMB 120-250 / USD 17-35 | Tianmen Mountain is in Zhangjiajie city, not Wulingyuan. Change to a city hotel the night before or leave early. |
| Wulingyuan -> Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon | Private car / taxi | 40-70 min | RMB 150-300 / USD 21-42 | Grand Canyon, Huanglong Cave and Baofeng Lake can be combined, but do not overload one day. |
| Zhangjiajie -> Enshi | High-speed/train connection + taxi | 4.5-8 hrs | RMB 200-600 / USD 28-83 | Use 12306 English site, Railway 12306 App, Trip.com or station ticket counter. Buy with passport and carry the same original passport when boarding. |
| Enshi Station -> city hotel | Taxi / ride-hailing | 15-35 min | RMB 20-80 / USD 3-11 | Taxis are convenient in Enshi. After check-in, confirm the next-day car to the canyon. |
| Enshi city -> Enshi Grand Canyon | Private car / scenic direct bus / local bus | 1.5-2.5 hrs | RMB 30-300 / USD 4-42 | Most international visitors should use a private car or hotel-arranged transfer. Local buses are cheaper but harder to manage. |
| Enshi city -> Pingshan Canyon | Private car / small group day tour | 2.5-3.5 hrs one way | RMB 500-1,000 / USD 69-139 per car | Confirm opening, water level and return plan before leaving. Same-day round trip is tiring; leave very early or stay in Hefeng. |
| Enshi -> Lichuan / Tenglong Cave | Train + taxi or private car | 1-2.5 hrs | RMB 80-400 / USD 11-56 | Good if adding Tenglong Cave. Lichuan Station works better than returning from Enshi the same day. |
| Enshi -> Yichang / Wuhan | Train / high-speed rail | 2-6 hrs | RMB 100-500 / USD 14-69 | Useful exit directions after the route. Book through 12306 / Trip.com and carry the passport used to buy the ticket. |

| Public transport use for international visitors: For trains, use the 12306 English website, Railway 12306 App, Trip.com or a staffed station ticket counter. Use the passport name exactly as printed. For scenic-area tickets, if an online page does not accept passports, try Trip.com / Ctrip international version, hotel assistance or the manual ticket window. Alipay and WeChat work well in cities, but carry RMB 500-1,500 cash for drivers, small restaurants, guesthouses and mountain areas. |
