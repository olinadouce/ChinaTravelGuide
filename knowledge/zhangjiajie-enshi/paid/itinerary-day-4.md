## Day 4 | Golden Whip Stream / Huangshizhai / Yangjiajie Choice Day

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose valley hiking, extra high viewpoints or a stronger adventure route; do not force all options. |
| Route | Wulingyuan / Zhangjiajie National Forest Park |
| Overnight | Wulingyuan or Zhangjiajie City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 650-2,400 / USD 90-333 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and choose the plan based on energy and weather.

Option A 09:00-13:00 | Golden Whip Stream classic walk. Follow Forest Park Gate - Golden Whip Rock - Zicao Pool - Shuiraosimen. This is the best valley-floor route and gives a different perspective from high viewpoints.

Option B 09:00-12:30 | Huangshizhai. Good for extra panoramic views if you still want another high platform.

Option C 09:00-14:00 | Yangjiajie / Wulong Village - Tianbo Mansion. Only for fit visitors comfortable with stairs and narrow paths.

15:00-17:00 | Return to hotel, rest or transfer to Zhangjiajie city if Day 5 Tianmen Mountain starts early.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Wulingyuan or Zhangjiajie city depending on overnight choice.

Transport and distance notes: For Golden Whip Stream, the main cost is already covered by the park ticket. Huangshizhai or Yangjiajie may require cableway fees depending on route. If moving to Zhangjiajie city tonight, taxi/private car from Wulingyuan costs about RMB 120-250 / USD 17-35.

Dining suggestions: Before a transfer, use simple food such as noodle/rice shops. For dinner in Zhangjiajie city, choose Hu Shifu, Fu Zhengyi or Wulong Shanzhai.

Accommodation suggestions: If Day 5 Tianmen Mountain is fixed early, move to Zhangjiajie city tonight. Otherwise stay in Wulingyuan for one more night.

International visitor notes: Golden Whip Stream has monkeys. Do not feed them and keep food in bags.

Backup plan: If knees are tired, skip Yangjiajie. If rain is heavy, choose Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake or a rest day.
