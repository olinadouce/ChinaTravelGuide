## Day 9 | Enshi Grand Canyon 1: Qixingzhai

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The classic high-view section of Enshi Grand Canyon: Yizhuxiang, cliff walkway and peak clusters. |
| Route | Enshi City -> Enshi Grand Canyon Qixingzhai -> Enshi / canyon area |
| Overnight | Enshi City or near Grand Canyon |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,500 / USD 111-347 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

06:30-07:30 | Breakfast and early departure from Enshi city. If staying near the canyon, start later.

08:30-09:30 | Arrive at visitor center, handle tickets and scenic transport.

09:30-13:00 | Qixingzhai upper route: cliff walkway, peak clusters and major viewpoints. Walk slowly and take breaks.

13:00-14:00 | Simple lunch / packed food.

14:00-16:30 | Yizhuxiang, Welcoming Pine and remaining viewpoints. Do not rush the whole route if knees are tired.

16:30-18:30 | Return to hotel or stay near the canyon.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner: Enshi local dishes or canyon-area simple food.

Transport and distance notes: Enshi city to Grand Canyon takes 1.5-2.5 hours each way. Private car is easier for international visitors; local buses are cheaper but require more Chinese communication.

Dining suggestions: Bring snacks and water; scenic meals are limited. Dinner in Enshi city is better if returning the same day.

Accommodation suggestions: If you want early entry and less driving, stay near the canyon visitor center. Otherwise stay in Enshi city for better food.

International visitor notes: Qixingzhai has significant walking and stairs. Use knee support if needed.

Backup plan: If the weather is bad or energy is low, split Qixingzhai and Yunlong Ground Fissure across two days and skip optional sections.
