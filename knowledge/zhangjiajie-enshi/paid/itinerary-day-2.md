## Day 2 | Wulingyuan Core 1: Yuanjiajie - Bailong Elevator - Tianzi Mountain

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | First full view of Zhangjiajie peak forest, with high viewpoints and stone pillars. |
| Route | Wulingyuan Landmark Gate -> Shuiraosimen -> Bailong Elevator -> Yuanjiajie -> Tianzi Mountain -> Wulingyuan |
| Overnight | Wulingyuan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

07:30-08:30 | Breakfast and enter from Wulingyuan Landmark Gate as early as possible.

08:30-09:30 | Take scenic shuttle to Shuiraosimen, then Bailong Elevator up to Yuanjiajie. Treat the elevator as transport plus experience; do not spend too long waiting for photos during peak queues.

09:30-12:30 | Yuanjiajie highlight route: Mihuntai, Qiankun Pillar / Hallelujah Mountain, First Bridge under Heaven and Back Garden. Spend 10-25 minutes at each viewpoint.

12:30-13:30 | Simple scenic lunch or packed food. Do not expect a high-quality restaurant inside the park.

13:30-16:30 | Tianzi Mountain: Helong Park, Imperial Brush Peaks, Fairy Scattering Flowers and West Sea Peak Forest. Best when visibility is good.

16:30-18:00 | Go down by Tianzi Mountain cableway or scenic shuttle, then return to Wulingyuan.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner: Suoxi Shanzhai, Lao Zaotai Sanxiaguo or a Tujia restaurant near Xibu Street.

Transport and distance notes: Inside Wulingyuan, use official scenic shuttle buses. The park is very large; do not walk between distant zones blindly. Bailong Elevator one way is about RMB 65-75 / USD 9-10; Tianzi cableway is about RMB 72 / USD 10.

Dining suggestions: Eat a real breakfast and bring snacks/water. Dinner should be outside the park in Wulingyuan, where restaurant quality is better.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay in Wulingyuan again to save time for Day 3. Hotels near Landmark Gate are most convenient.

International visitor notes: Carry passport or ticket booking screenshot. If online passport purchase fails, use Trip.com, hotel assistance or manual window.

Backup plan: If fog is heavy at Yuanjiajie/Tianzi Mountain, switch the order: walk Golden Whip Stream first, then return to high viewpoints when visibility improves.
