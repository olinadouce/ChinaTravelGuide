## 14-Day Route Overview

| Day | Route | Main Experience | Overnight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Zhangjiajie | Arrival, check-in, payment/network check and easy local dinner | Zhangjiajie City / Wulingyuan |
| Day 2 | Wulingyuan Core 1 | Yuanjiajie, Bailong Elevator, Mihuntai, Qiankun Pillar, First Bridge under Heaven | Wulingyuan |
| Day 3 | Wulingyuan Core 2 | Tianzi Mountain, Helong Park, Imperial Brush Peaks, West Sea Peak Forest, Ten-Mile Gallery | Wulingyuan |
| Day 4 | Golden Whip Stream / Huangshizhai / Yangjiajie choice day | Valley walking, monkeys, low-angle peak views; or higher viewpoints at Yangjiajie / Huangshizhai | Wulingyuan |
| Day 5 | Tianmen Mountain | Cableway, glass skywalk, Guigu Plank Road, Tianmen Cave and 999 steps | Zhangjiajie City |
| Day 6 | Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon / Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake | Glass bridge and canyon, or cave / lake backup in rain | Zhangjiajie City / Wulingyuan |
| Day 7 | Zhangjiajie -> Enshi | Long transfer day and arrival rest | Enshi City |
| Day 8 | Enshi buffer / Qingjiang choice | Qingjiang Butterfly Cliff boat trip or easy city day | Enshi City |
| Day 9 | Enshi Grand Canyon 1 | Qixingzhai: Yizhuxiang, cliff walkway, welcoming pine and peak clusters | Enshi City / Grand Canyon area |
| Day 10 | Enshi Grand Canyon 2 / Recovery | Yunlong Ground Fissure or rest after Qixingzhai | Enshi City |
| Day 11 | Pingshan Canyon | Clear-water canyon, boat photos, gorge walkway | Hefeng / Enshi |
| Day 12 | Suobuya Stone Forest / Tenglong Cave | Limestone forest or large cave system | Enshi / Lichuan |
| Day 13 | Flexible Enshi buffer | Make-up sightseeing, rest, food, laundry, transfer buffer | Enshi / Yichang / Wuhan direction |
| Day 14 | Departure | Departure day, no distant scenic area | - |
