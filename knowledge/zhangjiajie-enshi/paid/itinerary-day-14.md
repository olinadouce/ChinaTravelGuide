## Day 14 | Departure from Enshi / Yichang / Wuhan Direction

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Departure day; no distant scenic area. |
| Route | Enshi -> next destination |
| Overnight | - |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 100-800 / USD 14-111 excluding long-distance ticket |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

Morning | Hotel breakfast, final packing and passport/ticket check.

If departure is late | Only do a short nearby walk, coffee, light lunch or station-area meal. Do not visit a distant attraction.

Before departure | Arrive at railway station 1-1.5 hours early. For flights, allow extra time for traffic and passport-related processes.

Transport and distance notes: Confirm whether you depart from Enshi Station, Enshi Airport, Yichang or Wuhan. Do not confuse stations. Carry the original passport used for rail ticket purchase.

Dining suggestions: Choose simple food near the hotel, station or airport. Avoid unfamiliar spicy food right before long travel.

Accommodation suggestions: No planned accommodation. If delayed, choose a chain hotel near the station or airport.

International visitor notes: Keep cash, power bank and passport accessible. Do not pack passport deep inside checked luggage.

Backup plan: If transport is delayed or canceled, stay near the station/airport and rebook through 12306, airline counter, Trip.com or hotel assistance.
