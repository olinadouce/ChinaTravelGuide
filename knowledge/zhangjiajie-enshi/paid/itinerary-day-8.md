## Day 8 | Enshi Buffer Day / Qingjiang River Choice

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Switch from Zhangjiajie to Enshi at an easier pace before canyon hiking. |
| Route | Enshi City -> Qingjiang River or easy city day |
| Overnight | Enshi City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 500-1,800 / USD 69-250 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:30-09:30 | Late breakfast and energy check.

Option A 10:00-15:30 | Qingjiang Butterfly Cliff boat trip. Good for river cliffs and a relaxed water day.

Option B 10:00-13:00 | Tusi City / Daughter City easy walk. Good if you want a short cultural-and-food buffer without heavy hiking.

Afternoon | Rest, laundry, supplies, and confirm Day 9 Enshi Grand Canyon transport.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Enshi: hezha, Tujia cuisine or local snacks.

Transport and distance notes: Qingjiang trips require transfers from Enshi city and are easier with a car or local day tour. City buffer activities can be done by taxi or ride-hailing.

Dining suggestions: Try Zhangguan Hezha, Tujia cured-pork dishes, kang potatoes and shefan rice.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay in the same Enshi city hotel for convenience.

International visitor notes: Use this day to reduce fatigue. Do not move directly from Zhangjiajie transfer into a full canyon day if tired.

Backup plan: If weather is poor, keep the city rest version and move Qingjiang to a flexible later day if needed.
