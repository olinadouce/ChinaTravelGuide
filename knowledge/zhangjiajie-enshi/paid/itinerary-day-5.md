## Day 5 | Tianmen Mountain

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | High cableway, cliff paths, glass skywalk and Tianmen Cave. |
| Route | Zhangjiajie City -> Tianmen Mountain -> City |
| Overnight | Zhangjiajie City |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 800-2,800 / USD 111-389 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

07:30-08:30 | Breakfast near hotel and arrive at Tianmen cableway / entrance according to reserved time.

08:30-10:00 | Cableway or shuttle up the mountain depending on Line A/B/C. Enjoy the transition from city to cliffs.

10:00-12:30 | West or east loop cliff path. Include Guigu Plank Road and selected glass skywalk if comfortable with heights.

12:30-13:30 | Simple lunch / snacks on the mountain.

13:30-15:30 | Tianmen Cave area and 999 steps. Use escalators if available and if energy is limited.

15:30-17:00 | Descend by official route and return to city hotel.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Zhangjiajie city: fish head, sanxiaguo or Wulong Shanzhai.

Transport and distance notes: Tianmen Mountain packages are around RMB 278-300 / USD 39-42. The route is controlled by booked Line A/B/C and current operations. Do not assume you can choose any path on arrival.

Dining suggestions: Bring snacks and water. Dinner in the city is better than relying on mountain food.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay in Zhangjiajie city near the cableway station or city center after this day.

International visitor notes: Glass skywalks may require small shoe-cover fees. If you are afraid of heights, skip glass sections and keep the normal cliff path or Tianmen Cave.

Backup plan: If cableway closes or fog is severe, use Day 6 as a swap day and visit Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake today.
