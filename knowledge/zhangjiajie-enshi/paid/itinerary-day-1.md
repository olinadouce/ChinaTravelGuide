## Day 1 | Arrival in Zhangjiajie

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Arrival, check-in, payment and network check. No intensive sightseeing. |
| Route | Departure city -> Zhangjiajie City or Wulingyuan |
| Overnight | Zhangjiajie City / Wulingyuan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 450-1,800 / USD 63-250 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

After arrival | Airport / railway station to hotel, 20-75 minutes. Stay in Wulingyuan if Day 2 starts in the Forest Park; stay in Zhangjiajie city if arriving late or visiting Tianmen Mountain first.

16:00-18:00 | Rest at the hotel and check Alipay, WeChat Pay, cash, translation app, map app and ticket bookings.

18:30-20:00 | First proper meal: Hu Shifu Sanxiaguo, Fu Zhengyi Sanxiaguo, Wulong Shanzhai or a hotel-area Tujia restaurant.

After 20:00 | Do not plan distant night sightseeing. Confirm Day 2 entrance gate, ticket, passport and departure time.

Transport and distance notes: Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport to city center usually takes 20-40 minutes; to Wulingyuan 60-75 minutes. Zhangjiajie West Station to Wulingyuan is around 45-60 minutes. Taxi/private car reference: RMB 40-250 / USD 6-35. Avoid complex buses at night.

Dining suggestions: Try sanxiaguo dry pot, Tujia cured pork and simple stir-fries. If you need milder food, show the phrase "less spicy" and choose Wulong Shanzhai or a hotel restaurant.

Accommodation suggestions: If entering the Forest Park next day, stay near Wulingyuan Landmark Gate / Xibu Street. If visiting Tianmen Mountain next day, stay near the cableway station or Dayong Ancient Town.

International visitor notes: Save hotel name, address and phone number in Chinese. Zhangjiajie has several hotel zones; check location carefully before booking.

Backup plan: If flight or train is delayed, cancel all evening plans. Day 2 can be converted to an easier park entry or Tianmen Mountain half day.
