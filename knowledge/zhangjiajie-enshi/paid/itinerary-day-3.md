## Day 3 | Wulingyuan Core 2: Tianzi Mountain, Ten-Mile Gallery and Easy Backups

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Supplement high panoramic views and a lower-intensity symbolic peak route. |
| Route | Wulingyuan -> Tianzi Mountain / Ten-Mile Gallery -> optional Baofeng Lake or Huanglong Cave |
| Overnight | Wulingyuan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 750-2,500 / USD 104-347 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and weather check. If the sky is clear, prioritize high viewpoints; if foggy, start with Ten-Mile Gallery or Huanglong Cave.

09:00-12:00 | Tianzi Mountain make-up viewpoints if Day 2 was foggy. Focus on Imperial Brush Peaks, West Sea and open platforms.

12:00-13:00 | Simple lunch inside or outside the scenic area.

13:30-15:30 | Ten-Mile Gallery. Take the mini-train one way and walk back if energy allows. Look for Old Man Gathering Herbs and Three Sisters Peak.

16:00-18:00 | Optional Baofeng Lake for a relaxed boat view or Huanglong Cave if it rains.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner in Wulingyuan: Tujia dishes, cured pork, local chicken or sanxiaguo.

Transport and distance notes: Ten-Mile Gallery mini-train costs RMB 38-76 / USD 5-11. Baofeng Lake usually needs a taxi/private car from Wulingyuan, about 20-40 minutes depending on hotel location.

Dining suggestions: Lunch can be simple. Save your main meal for dinner outside the park: Suoxi Shanzhai, Wulingyuan Sanxiaguo restaurants or Xibu Street noodle/snack shops.

Accommodation suggestions: Continue Wulingyuan stay to avoid hotel switching.

International visitor notes: Do not force high viewpoints in thick fog. Zhangjiajie views depend heavily on visibility.

Backup plan: Rain/fog backup: Huanglong Cave. Low-energy backup: Ten-Mile Gallery mini-train + hotel rest.
