## Day 12 | Suobuya Stone Forest / Tenglong Cave

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | Choose between an outdoor limestone forest and a large cave system. |
| Route | Enshi -> Suobuya / Lichuan Tenglong Cave |
| Overnight | Enshi / Lichuan |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 600-2,400 / USD 83-333 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

08:00-09:00 | Breakfast and decide based on weather.

Option A 09:30-14:30 | Suobuya Stone Forest. Best for outdoor karst formations and medium walking intensity.

Option B 09:00-15:30 | Tenglong Cave near Lichuan. Best in rain, heat or when you prefer cave scenery and underground-river atmosphere.

16:00-18:00 | Return to Enshi or stay in Lichuan if the next transport direction is easier.

18:30-20:00 | Dinner: Lichuan beef/hotpot or Enshi local cuisine.

Transport and distance notes: Suobuya is easier from Enshi by car. Tenglong Cave works well with train/taxi to Lichuan or private car. Confirm return transport before entering the scenic area.

Dining suggestions: For Suobuya, bring water and simple snacks. For Lichuan, try local beef or hotpot-style restaurants.

Accommodation suggestions: Stay in Enshi if departing from Enshi; stay in Lichuan if onward rail route is better.

International visitor notes: Do not combine both Suobuya and Tenglong Cave in one day unless using a very efficient private car and strong energy.

Backup plan: In heavy rain, prefer Tenglong Cave. If tired, make Day 12 a city rest and food day.
