## Day 11 | Pingshan Canyon

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Today’s Focus | The clearest-water canyon day of the route, but highly dependent on weather and water levels. |
| Route | Enshi -> Hefeng / Pingshan Canyon -> Enshi or Hefeng |
| Overnight | Hefeng / Enshi |
| Estimated Cost | RMB 900-2,800 / USD 125-389 |

Recommended route (follow in this order)

06:30-07:30 | Early departure from Enshi if doing a day trip. A later start risks rushing the canyon and return drive.

09:30-10:30 | Arrive near Pingshan, confirm ticket, boat and opening status.

10:30-13:30 | Pingshan Canyon main route: clear water, narrow gorge walls, plank road and boat-photo sections if operating.

13:30-14:30 | Simple lunch near the scenic area or Hefeng county.

14:30-17:30 | Return to Enshi or stay in Hefeng depending on energy and next-day plan.

Evening | Dinner in Hefeng or Enshi; do not arrange more sightseeing.

Transport and distance notes: Enshi to Pingshan is 2.5-3.5 hours one way. Private car or small group tour is strongly recommended. Reference: RMB 500-1,000 / USD 69-139 per car, plus tickets.

Dining suggestions: Food near Pingshan is limited. Plan a simple lunch and eat a better dinner in Hefeng/Enshi. Look for local Tujia dishes, cured pork and river-fish restaurants.

Accommodation suggestions: For a smoother trip, stay in Hefeng or near Pingshan the night before or after. Confirm passport check-in.

International visitor notes: Pingshan is weather- and water-level dependent. The famous clear-water effect is not guaranteed after rain.

Backup plan: If the canyon is closed, water is muddy or boat stops, switch to Suobuya Stone Forest, Tenglong Cave or an Enshi rest day.
