## Overall Itinerary

This free version gives the route structure and main planning logic. It intentionally keeps timing, hotels, restaurants and ticket choices broad so you can first judge whether the route fits your travel style.

| Stage | Route | Recommended Area to Stay | Food Ideas / Transfer Style |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Arrive in Zhangjiajie -> rest and setup | Zhangjiajie City or Wulingyuan | Sanxiaguo dry pot, Tujia dishes · airport/rail taxi + hotel check-in |
| Day 2-4 | Wulingyuan core areas -> Yuanjiajie / Tianzi Mountain / Golden Whip Stream choice days | Wulingyuan Landmark Gate / Xibu Street / East Gate area | Tujia cuisine, simple scenic snacks · scenic shuttle + Bailong Elevator/cableway |
| Day 5-6 | Tianmen Mountain -> Grand Canyon / Huanglong Cave / Baofeng Lake | Zhangjiajie City or Wulingyuan | City restaurants, fish head, dry pot · cableway/taxi/private car |
| Day 7-8 | Zhangjiajie -> Enshi -> Qingjiang or easy city buffer | Enshi Railway Station / Daughter City / city center | Hezha, Tujia dishes, local snacks · train + taxi/private car |
| Day 9-11 | Enshi Grand Canyon -> Yunlong Ground Fissure / recovery -> Pingshan Canyon | Enshi City, canyon area or Hefeng / Pingshan | Packed snacks, Enshi local meals · private car/small group transfer |
| Day 12-14 | Suobuya Stone Forest / Tenglong Cave -> flexible buffer -> departure | Enshi / Lichuan / Yichang or Wuhan direction | Final local meal · buffer day, no distant sights on departure day |
