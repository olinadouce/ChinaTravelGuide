## Travel Tips

Keep these points in mind before you start the route.

| Payment Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry RMB cash backup for drivers, small restaurants and mountain areas. | Internet Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment. |
| --- | --- |
| Heights & walking This route includes cliff paths, cableways, glass walkways, stairs and long scenic-area walking. Do not force every viewpoint. | Weather Fog, rain and visibility strongly affect Zhangjiajie and Enshi views. Use caves, lakes or rest days as backups. |
| Tickets Use official channels, Trip.com, Ctrip, hotel desks or manual counters. Reconfirm passport-based ticketing rules and time slots. | Transfers Pingshan Canyon and Enshi Grand Canyon need early starts and reliable transport. Confirm opening status, water level and return plan. |
