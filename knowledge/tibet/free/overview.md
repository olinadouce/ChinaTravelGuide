Route Rhythm

From highland city to snowland nature

The route opens in Lhasa, slows through monastery neighborhoods, then moves into Nyingchi’s river valleys, forests, lake water and canyon scale.

| Day 1 Arrival light | Day 2 Sacred streets | Day 3 Monastery space | Day 4 Valley transfer | Day 5 Lake or forest | Day 6 Canyon scale | Day 7 Return rhythm |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Route

| Lhasa city rhythm Potala skyline, Jokhang Temple area, Barkhor streets and tea houses create the first highland layer. | Monastery mountain space Sera or Drepung brings white walls, golden roofs, hillside views and a quieter walking pace. |
| --- | --- |
| Nyingchi soft nature Basum Lake and Lulang shift the route into water, forests, meadow villages and open valleys. | Snowland scale The Grand Canyon and Namcha Barwa form the route’s widest natural scene. |
