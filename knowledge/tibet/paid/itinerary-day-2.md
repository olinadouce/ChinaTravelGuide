## Day 2

¥145–240 / person

Classic Lhasa flavors fit this day best: tea-house rhythm, noodles, momo and a gentle evening meal.

| Breakfast | Sweet tea, Tibetan noodles and a small steamed bun or tingmo-style bread. Planning range: ¥20–40. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | Around Barkhor, pick yak momo, a noodle set or a tea-house meal for a relaxed midday stop. Planning range: ¥45–70. |
| Dinner | Choose yak stew, butter tea and one shared vegetable dish at a traveler-friendly Tibetan restaurant. Planning range: ¥80–130. |
