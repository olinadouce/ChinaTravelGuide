## Highlights

What the journey feels like

From Lhasa’s teahouses to Nyingchi’s lakes, forests and canyon views, the route moves from sacred-city life into softer highland nature.

| Lhasa Teahouse rhythm Sweet tea and neighborhood pauses make Lhasa feel lived-in rather than only monumental. | Nyingchi Lake and forest day Basum Lake and Lulang offer softer highland scenery after Lhasa’s sacred-city intensity. |
| --- | --- |
|  | Grand Canyon Snowland scale The route closes with scale: river, canyon, clouds and the possibility of Namcha Barwa views. |
