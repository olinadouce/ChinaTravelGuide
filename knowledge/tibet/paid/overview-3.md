## Route Overview

Highland culture first, snowland nature next.

The route enters through Lhasa’s sacred-city neighborhoods and sweet tea houses, then moves into Nyingchi’s river valleys, forests, lakes and canyon views. The pace is deliberately spacious so every day has one clear experience and a practical reason to be there.

| Day 1–3 Lhasa Arrival, Potala skyline, Barkhor Street, Jokhang neighborhood and monastery layers. | Day 4 Transfer The route shifts from sacred-city rhythm into the Niyang River valley. |
| --- | --- |
| Day 5–6 Nyingchi Basum Lake, Lulang Forest, Yarlung Zangbo Grand Canyon and Namcha Barwa atmosphere. | Day 7/8 Closure A low-intensity return or departure day keeps the full route steady. |
