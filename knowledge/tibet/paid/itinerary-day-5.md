## Day 5

¥170–285 / person

This is the best day for a stronger regional meal, especially if the Lulang branch is chosen.

| Breakfast | Hotel breakfast plus local yogurt, porridge or bread and tea. Planning range: ¥25–45. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | For Basum Lake, choose a simple lakeside set meal or hot soup. For Lulang, choose stone-pot chicken, usually shared. Planning range: ¥45–80 for Basum Lake, about ¥100–160 per person for Lulang stone-pot chicken. |
| Dinner | Back in Nyingchi, keep dinner restorative: soup noodles, highland vegetables or a lighter valley-style meal. Planning range: ¥45–80. |
