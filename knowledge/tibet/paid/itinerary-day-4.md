## Day 4

¥120–205 / person

Transfer-day meals should be predictable, warm and easy to fit around transport timing.

| Breakfast | Sweet tea, buns, bread or noodles before leaving Lhasa. Planning range: ¥25–45. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | During the transfer, use a simple route lunch such as noodle soup, rice with vegetables or a station / roadside set. Planning range: ¥35–60. |
| Dinner | In Nyingchi town, shift to valley flavor with hot soup, mushroom dishes or a mild stir-fry set. Planning range: ¥60–100. |
