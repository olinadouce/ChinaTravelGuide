## Day 1

¥70–140 / person

Warm, light and easy to digest — the first day is about settling into Lhasa without rushing.

| Breakfast | If you arrive early, start with Tibetan yogurt, plain bread or a cup of sweet tea near the hotel. Planning range: ¥20–35. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | Choose Tibetan noodles or a mild noodle soup around the outer Barkhor / Potala area. Planning range: ¥25–45. |
| Dinner | Keep dinner warm and simple: thenthuk, clear yak broth or vegetable-and-noodle soup. Planning range: ¥45–80. |

Daily Dining

Potala · Jokhang · Barkhor
