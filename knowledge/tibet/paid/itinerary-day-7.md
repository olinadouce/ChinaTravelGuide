## Day 7/8

¥125–220 / person

The final day stays light and transport-friendly, with room for one relaxed farewell meal.

| Breakfast | Sweet tea or butter tea, noodles or soft buns. Planning range: ¥20–40. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | A simple rice or noodle set before transport. Planning range: ¥35–60. |
| Dinner | If spending the final evening in Lhasa, choose momo, yak stew and one last teahouse stop. Planning range: ¥70–120. |
