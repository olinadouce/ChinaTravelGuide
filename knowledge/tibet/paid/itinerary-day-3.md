## Day 3

¥120–205 / person

Food stays moderate so the monastery day feels spacious, quiet and comfortable.

| Breakfast | Tsampa porridge or barley porridge, sweet tea and an egg or bread. Planning range: ¥20–35. |
| --- | --- |
| Lunch | After the monastery, choose vegetable momo, thenthuk or a simple rice-and-soup set. Planning range: ¥35–60. |
| Dinner | Yak meat, seasonal vegetables and rice or noodles, or an international–Tibetan mix for variety. Planning range: ¥60–110. |
