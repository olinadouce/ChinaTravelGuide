Poetic Chang'an · Product Message

To poets, Chang'an was the land of ideals, a dream high above the mortal world.

Meng Jiao

Riding on elation, my horse gallops fast;
I view all blossoms of Chang'an in one day's past.

Bai Juyi

Beneath the Big Wild Goose Pagoda my name stands tall;
Among seventeen top scholars, I am the youngest of all.

Wang Wei

Heavenly gates swing open to reveal imperial halls;
Envoys from every land bow before the royal thrones.

Li Bai

My longing lingers always, right here in Chang'an.

This city once witnessed gallant youths in fine robes and on fiery steeds, and also bore witness to sorrowful verses of unfulfilled ambitions.

When longing drifts back to Chang'an and the glorious Tang Dynasty, no heart can resist its allure.
When longing stays with Chang'an, we dwell in days of poetry and wine, with youthful passion and unreserved spirits.

Stand in Xi'an, and gaze upon the eternal grace of ancient Chang'an!

Tour Highlights

Three Grand Museums

Trace Zhou, Qin, Han & Tang civilizations: Shaanxi History Museum (thousands-year relics), Xi'an Museum (ancient pagoda culture), Terracotta Army (Qin imperial grandeur).

Ming City Wall · Yongning Gate

Stroll 600-year-old brick paths, overlook downtown Zhuque Avenue, blend ancient history and modern downtown scenery.

Two Classic Food Districts

Muslim Quarter & Yongxingfang: Roujiamo, lamb pita soup, sweet rice cake, authentic Chang'an local snacks.

Detailed 5-Day Itinerary

D1

Your Hometown → Xi'an | Arrival Day

Check in hotel after arriving in Xi'an by your own flight/traffic.

Accommodation: Downtown Xi'an

Xi'an, one of World Four Great Ancient Capitals, known as China's Natural History Museum.

D2

Cultural Heritage Tour: Museum & Ancient Streets

Morning

Shaanxi History Museum (2.5h). Note: Ticket shortage can switch to Qin-Han special exhibition hall.

Noon

Lunch at Yongxingfang, free Songsheng Washe experience included

Afternoon

Shuyuanmen → Xiangzi Temple St → Defu Lane → Yongning Gate City Wall (1.5h)

Attraction Intro:
  • Shaanxi History Museum: National first-class museum, slogan "One day here, ten thousand years of history".
  • Yongxingfang: Tang Dynasty Fang, intangible cultural heritage food street, former residence of Wei Zheng.
  • Shuyuanmen: Calligraphy & painting ancient cultural street.
  • Ming City Wall: Built 1370, total length 13.7km, well-preserved ancient city wall of Ming Dynasty.

Accommodation: Downtown Xi'an

D3

Time Travel Tour: Terracotta Army & Tang Night View

Morning

Terracotta Army Museum (2.5h). Shuttle & audio guide extra fee.

Noon

Chinese lunch in Lintong District

Afternoon & Night

Romance of Xi’an Show (1h) → Tang Ever-bright City (Hanfu self-experience)

Attraction Intro:
  • Terracotta Army: World Eight Wonders, underground army of Emperor Qin Shi Huang, 20th century top archaeological discovery.
  • Romance of Xi’an: Immersive dance drama showing Zhou/Qin/Han/Tang history through overseas Chinese root-seeking story.
  • Tang Ever-bright City: Restore prosperous Tang Chang'an street scene, Hanfu photo experience available.

Accommodation: Downtown Xi'an

D4

Leisure Buddhist & Food Tour

Morning

Xi'an Museum (1.5h) → Daci’en Temple (Big Wild Goose Pagoda, 1.5h)

Noon

Authentic local lunch

Afternoon

Bell & Drum Tower Square + Muslim Quarter Snack Street

Attraction Intro:
  • Xi'an Museum: Contains Xiaoyan Pagoda, 14,400+ precious cultural relics; closed Tuesday, switch to Eighth Route Army Office if no ticket.
  • Daci’en Temple: Core Tang Buddhist temple, landmark Big Wild Goose Pagoda for blessing prayer.
  • Muslim Quarter: Hundreds of Xi'an classic snacks, halal food street with thousand-year bell-drum culture.

Accommodation: Downtown Xi'an

Free Time & Return Flight

Free leisure time after breakfast, take return flight back home in the afternoon.

Travel Notes for Xi'an

  • Climate & Humidity: Spring/Autumn 15-25℃; Summer 25-35℃; Winter 0-10℃, big day-night temperature gap. Humidity 40%-60% dry, drink enough water daily. Check weather forecast before travel.
  • Ethnic & Religious Etiquette: Northwest Muslim minorities follow halal diet (no pork). Respect local religious customs in Muslim Quarter, avoid inappropriate words & behaviors to prevent misunderstandings.
  • Hotel Standard Difference: Northwest hotel facilities lag coastal Jiangsu/Zhejiang. Local 3-star ≈ coastal 2-star. New well-equipped hotels are 20-30min drive from city center.
  • Diet Habit: Staple food is noodles, heavy spicy taste. Local water & soil slightly alkaline, keep hydrated during tour.
