Travel Tips

Prepare before you start

Keep these practical points in mind before following the route.

💳

Payment

Use Octopus, widely accepted cards and supported mobile payments; carry small cash for markets and small eateries.

📱

Internet

Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing and mobile payment.

👥

Crowds

Do not recreate crowd-chasing or unsafe livestream behavior. Add buffer time at popular sites.

🌃

Night Views

Visit Victoria Peak or the Avenue of Stars from late afternoon to evening for sunset, blue hour and harbour lights.

🎟️

Tickets

Use official attraction channels, WeChat mini programs, Trip.com or on-site service desks. Reconfirm opening hours before travel.

🏨

Hotel Check-In

Book with the exact passport name and carry the original passport.
