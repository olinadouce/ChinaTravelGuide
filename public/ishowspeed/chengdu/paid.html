Optional Extensions

Choose one based on time and interest

Wenshu Monastery

Temple complex and vegetarian food

Best for: Quiet cultural stop
Dress respectfully. Admission is free, but if you need a guided tour, it costs approximately 499 yuan for 1.5 hours (small groups of up to 10 people). Please purchase guided tour services through official channels, such as scenic spot service desks and mini-programs of travel websites. Opening Hours: 8:00 - 17:00 (Last admission at 16:00)

Jinli Ancient Street

Food, crafts and evening lights

Best for: First-time visitors
Can be crowded and commercial.

6. Chengdu Food Guide

Local food choices for first-time visitors

Budget references are approximate and vary by restaurant, season, serving size and number of diners.

Hotpot

Sichuan hotpot

CNY 100-20 pp
Choose a divided pot and explain spice tolerance.

Classic Sichuan

Mapo tofu, Kung Pao Chicken, Twice-Cooked Pork, Fish-Fragrant Shredded Pork, Sichuan Boiled Beef, Sichuan Boiled Fish, Chongqing Spicy Chicken, Mouthwatering Chicken, Fuqi Feipian, Dry-Fried Green Beans, Boiled Cabbage in Clear Broth, Tea-Smoked Duck

CNY 35-00 per dish
Spicy signature dishes of Chengdu. The prices of Boiled Beef and Boiled Fish are slightly higher than those of other dishes.

Noodles

CNY 12-0 per bowl
Small, flavorful serving; often spicy.

Snack

Zhong dumplings, Long Chao Shou, Guokui

CNY 15-0
Dumplings in a sweet-spicy sauce.

Street snack

San da pao

CNY 10-5
Glutinous rice snack thrown onto a drum-like surface during preparation.

Dessert

Bingfen

CNY 8-5
Cooling jelly dessert, useful after spicy food.
