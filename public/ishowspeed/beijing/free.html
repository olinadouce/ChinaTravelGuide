Travel Tips

Prepare before you start

Keep these practical points in mind before following the route.

💳

Payment

Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival and carry a small amount of RMB as backup.

📱

Internet

Prepare an eSIM or international roaming for maps, translation, ride-hailing, and mobile payment.

👥

Crowds

The Palace Museum, Tiananmen Square, and Wangfujing are busy. Add buffer time for walking and photos.

🚧

Temporary Road Closures

Closures may occur during important conferences and events. Reconfirm the route or use the subway.

🎟️

Tickets

Use official attraction channels, WeChat mini programs, Trip.com, or on-site service desks. Reconfirm opening hours.

🏨

Hotel Check-in

Book with the exact passport name and carry the original passport for check-in.
